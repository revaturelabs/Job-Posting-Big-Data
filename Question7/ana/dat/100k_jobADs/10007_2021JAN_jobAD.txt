Administrator Jobs in SeattleFind Systems Administrator Jobs in the Seattle AreaSystems Administrators are the workers in charge of setting up, maintaining and supporting the users of a company's data network, Intranet and databases. Users request support from the Systems Administrator when they have difficulty with a network application or their computer or phone system. Typical responsibilities include configuration management of the network, databases and ancillary equipment, backing up data, updating the systems and processes when necessary and ensuring the reliability and availability of the system. Many venture capitalists from the Bay Area have migrated north and are now investing in the Seattle area.
