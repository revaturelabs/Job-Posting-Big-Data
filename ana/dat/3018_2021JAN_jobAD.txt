 Quality Improvement/Infection Prevention/Risk Management RN 
 Apply for the position, Quality Improvement/Infection Prevention/Risk Management RN, with Rush County Memorial Hospital today or contact Melanie Arbuckle for more information. Posted Sep 17, 2020. 
  7852222545  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Rush County Memorial Hospital is seeking a Quality Improvement/Infection Prevention/Risk Management RN.  The successful candidate will provide leadership and management while monitoring and improving the quality and appropriateness of patient care while minimizing risk and potential loss, hospital-wide. 
 Qualifications: 
 ·         RN, licensed to practice in KS 
 ·         Minimum 2 years professional nursing experience 
 ·         Demonstrated leadership and management experience 
 Responsibilities: 
 ·         Plan, organize and direct quality, risk management and infections operations and activities 
 ·         Provide information to hospital committees/departments to formulate changes in policies, procedures and/or processes to reduce the spread of infection 
 ·         Develop and coordinate infection prevention and control throughout the facility by formulating, establishing and evaluating policies and procedures.  Perform annual assessments and establish actions plans. 
 ·         Evaluate programs and effect needed changes to improve quality programs and assure compliance with requirements, hospital standards, policies and procedures. 
 ·         Assure coordination, standardization and continuity of quality improvement programs. 
 ·         Monitor, supervise and evaluate quality performance. 
 ·         Identify new opportunities to improve service and reduce costs. 
 ·         Maintain established hospital policies/procedures, mission statement and goals while ensuring all staff participate toward goal achievement. 
 To apply: 
 · Email cover letter and resume to  [email protected] 
 · Apply online:  www.rushcountymemorialhospital.com/jobs.html 
 Rush County Memorial offers a complete benefit package, including: competitive wages; health, dental and vision insurance; AFLAC; pension and disability; earned time off and extended illness leave. 
 Rush County Memorial Hospital is an EEO Employer. 
 _ _ 
 _Rush County Memorial Hospital is a progressive and growing Critical Access Hospital in La Crosse, Kansas serving Rush County and the surrounding area.   We have a 16-bed acute care unit, 20-bed long term care unit and Rural Health Clinic.  Services provided include:  ER, Acute Care, Swing/Skill/Intermediate Swing Bed, Radiology, Laboratory and various outpatient services. _
