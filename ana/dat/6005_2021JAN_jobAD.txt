 About Our Client 
 Our client is has a strong focus a privately owned agricultural business. The client is based in Rubyvale and has a strong commitment to Health and Safety. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking an experienced Farm Hand to commence at a role for a local client in Rubyvale. You will be working Monday to Friday with successful candidates starting as soon as possible. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operate Tractors and farming equipment 
 
 
 General farm duties 
 
 
 Maintain a safe working environment 
 
 
 "Hands on" attitude 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 Tractor experience 
 
 
 Previous worked as a Farm Hand 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence and Right to work within Australia
