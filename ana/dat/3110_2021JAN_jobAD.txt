 Financial Controller 
 Financial Controller 
 
 Location 
 
 Birmingham, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £60000.00 - £65000.00 per annum + Car, Bonus, Pension, Healthcare 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Julie Smyth 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 julie.smyth@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH20722_1589266814 
 
 Published: 
 
 8 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-06-11 
 I have been instructed on an exclusive basis to recruit a Financial Controller for an exciting PE backed business in Birmingham. This is a newly created role and will see you support the Commercial Finance Director with all things technical including M&A activity within their UK wide operation. You will be a fully qualified accountant, have trained in a Top 10 practice and be able to work comfortably in a very fast moving environment. 
 The ideal candidate will have multi-site experience and will have a high aptitude for technical accounting coupled with strong team management skills. The role of  Financial Controller  will see you working closely with an extremely talented and highly experienced Commercial Finance Director who retains a sense of humour and level head at all times. This role will also allow the successful candidate exposure to Operations where you will provide financial support and analytical challenge to ensure the organisation achieves the financial targets it has been set. 
 1st Stage Interviews - Week Commencing 25th May 
 The Role: 
 As the  Financial Controller  you will be technically astute and will be required to undertake the following: 
 
 Manage and develop a small but well-formed finance team. 
 Establish effective relationships with all members of the senior management team to develop a deep knowledge and understanding of the business. 
 Ensure completeness, accuracy and timely delivery of financial reporting. 
 Identify and follow risks and opportunities. 
 Full understanding of company profit & loss, balance sheet and cash flow. 
 Deliver smooth month-end closing & consolidation process. 
 Maintain robust internal control environment. 
 Conduct balance sheet reviews - ledger & reconciliation review / validation and provision review. 
 The delivery of management and statutory reporting, including analysis and supporting information. 
 Supporting the FD with the co-ordination of the annual budgeting, monthly forecasting and strategic planning processes. 
 Liaising with external Advisors in relation to audit, taxation and other areas. 
 Managing the team to ensure all deadlines are met, accounts and reports are accurate and statutory obligations adhered to. 
 M&A projects. 
 
 **  The Candidate: ** 
 As the successful  Financial Controller  you will be / have: 
 
 A fully qualified accountant trained in Practice - ACA qualified; 
 Strong technical accounting knowledge; 
 A strong communicator who is comfortable challenging the status quo; 
 Strong team leadership and motivation of people; 
 Able to interpret and communicate financial issues to non-finance stakeholders; 
 A sense of humour. 
 
 **  The Benefits Package: ** 
 The following benefits package is available to the successful  Financial Controller  : 
 
 Salary of £60K - £65K 
 Car allowance 
 Bonus 
 Pension 
 Healthcare 
 Fun, fast paced environment 
 Career progression
