 You will manage a portfolio of existing and new clients. This will include finance brokers and lenders across the UK.  You will be rewarded based on net promoter score, usage levels, the number of advocates you can foster relationships with and the new sales opportunities you can generate from your portfolio. As our business grows and you directly contribute to that, you will build up highly valuable skills and experiences that you can take forward throughout your career.
