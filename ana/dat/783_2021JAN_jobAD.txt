 Job Description 
 Overview** 
 **  PURPOSE OF POSITION ** 
 Entry level position, call center representatives field customer inquiries and finding innovative ways to respond to varying questions and issues. Interact with customers in a collaborative and engaging environment, maintain confidentiality and security while assisting with needs to meet and exceed Client’s expectations. 
 Responsibilities 
 **  PRIMARY DUTIES, RESPONSIBILITIES AND REQUIREMENTS ** 
 
 Respond respectively, carefully and effectively to handle all customer inquiries via phone and email 
 Provide excellent customer service through active listening regarding sales 
 Maintain confidentiality and security of customer information and sales leads 
 Follow up on customer inquiries in a timely manner 
 Proactively aim to resolve issues on the first call 
 Logical problem-solving skills and ability to multi-task 
 
 Qualifications 
 **  QUALIFICATIONS ** 
 
 6 months customer service experience including sales 
 Must be 18 years of age 
 Must have typing speed of 25 wpm 
 High School Graduate or GED 
 Must have ability to work various shifts influence by current business needs 
 Ability to work with sales customers honestly and confidently 
 Proficiency with desktop computer system and internet skills 
 Proven oral & written communication skills 
 Organization and work prioritization skills 
 Must pass background and drug tests
