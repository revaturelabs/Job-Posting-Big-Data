 Caregiver Job Vacancy Abroad 
 Posted on  January 31, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Two POEA accredited institutions in the field of manpower recruitment are presently looking for caregivers who are qualified to fill Caregiver Job Vacancy Abroad. These caregiver job vacancy abroad are both in the holy land of Israel and were approved for recruitment by the POEA for two recruit agencies who are both located in the city of Makati. Specified below are the number of caregiver job vacancy abroad for each of the recruiting agency for caregiver work abroad. 
** 
NYD International Placement Agency Inc.** --- 13 caregiver job vacancy 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc.  ----------------- 24 caregiver job vacancy 
 To submit your resume and application to any of the recruiting agency for the said Caregiver Job Vacancy Abroad, proceed directly to their offices. For any question or you like to know more info regarding the said caregiver job vacancy abroad, you may dial their telephone numbers provided below.
