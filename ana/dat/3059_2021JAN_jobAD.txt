 The Team: The Xpressfeed team is responsible for generating content feeds for the overall Data Management Solutions platform. Currently we have 12 agile teams working on Xpressfeed development and are looking to add 1 additional team to help scale out for the strong demand we are seeing from the business. 
 The Impact: We are working to scale out the development team to deliver new value to customers faster. Type of projects include backend database development, Tools to configure metadata driven system, Scaling into cloud environment, new delivery platforms, and expanding and scaling infrastructure. 
 What's in it for you: We are a global company and a global team with a history of delivering high value product feeds which drives over 500M in revenue for the S&P; Global Market Intelligence division. You will get hands on experience developing, deploying, and support the products. Being small agile teams, you will see the results of your development and feel the success of the business as you successfully deliver on projects. In addition you will get to work with a highly knowable and energetic team working to solve complex business problems in a fast, highly productive environment. 
Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Plan/manage tasks as per the release cycles. 
 
 
 Design, Document and Develop solutions to support key business needs. 
 
 
 Engineer components and common services based on standard corporate development models, languages and tools. 
 
 
 Produce system design documents and contribute to technical walkthroughs. 
 
 
 Collaborate effectively with technical and non-technical stakeholders. 
 
 
 Support and maintain production environments 
 
 
 Coach and mentor junior developers on the team accordingly. 
 
 
 What We're Looking For: 
 
 
 Bachelor's/PG degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or equivalent. 
 
 
 A minimum of 5+ years of strong experience in application development using Java or .net technologies 
 
 
 Proficient with software development lifecycle (SDLC) methodologies like Agile, Scrum, Test-driven development. 
 
 
 Strong command of essential technologies: T-SQL , SQL Server, Oracle, PL/SQL, VLDB, Java, WebLogic 
 
 
 At least a Year of experience in React, micro-services pattern, cloud technologies and managed services preferably AWS, Big Data and Analytics, PostgreSql, NoSql databases, nifi, Airflow, Spark, and Hive 
 
 
 Good experience with analyzing and developing solutions involving relational database technologies on SQL Server and/or Oracle Platforms 
 
 
 Knowledge and experience in developing, tuning, and maintaining large dataset.
