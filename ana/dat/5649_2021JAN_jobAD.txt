 Project Controller 
 Project Controller 
 Belgium

 Duration:  12 months initially - realistically 2-3 years 
 Project Controller Job profile: 
 This is a client-side opportunity for a Project Controller to join one of the world's largest tech firms on their hyperscale data centre construction project in Belgium. 
 This is being offered, initially, on a one-year contract, but will likely be extended on for 2-3 further years throughout the build of the data centre. 
 
 Project Controller Key activities 
 The successful Project Controller candidate's responsibilities will include, but not be limited to: 
 Preconstruction 
 
 Create and establish the Master Project Schedule, based on the Critical Path and key milestones. 
 
 (P6 cost/resource loaded schedule) 
 
 
 Create and establish the Master Project Budget, including soft costs and hard costs, bonds, insurance, contingencies, allowances, etc. 
 
 
 Support Contractor pre-qualification, bid solicitation, bid analysis and recommendations 
 
 
 Support Contract negotiation, typically in concert with the Client's PM and legal counsel, including establishment of all GC "business terms" including mark-ups, fees, insurance, labour rates, escalation, etc. 
 
 
 Provide / Gather cost estimates working with subs and other vendors for scope gaps/changes 
 
 
 Coordinate Value Engineering effort 
 
 
 Produce Cash flow projections on a monthly basis 
 
 
 Construction 
 
 
 Manage Cost / Change Control, including change order review, negotiation, & recommendations, delay analysis 
 
 
 Coordinate Value Engineering 
 
 
 Budget Management and Tracking 
 
 
 Schedule monitoring, tracking and analysis 
 
 
 Invoice / Payment Application processing, tracking and reporting 
 
 
 Tracking of all key documents, including change orders, invoices and payments, lien releases, etc 
 
 
 Close out / Fit Up 
 
 
 Negotiate, resolve and close out any outstanding change orders, quality issues or disputes 
 
 
 Process and track any final invoices, payment applications, retention payments, etc 
 
 
 Prepare and submit final project accounting and budget 
 
 
 Assist the client in scheduling, coordination and management of all Owner-direct vendors, consultants. 
 
 
 
 Project Controller   specification 
 The following will be required of the Project Controller: 
 
 
 Prior experience working on data centre construction projects is desirable, but candidates whose experience includes pharmaceutical, semiconductor, or other high-tech projects will also be considered 
 
 
 Strong P6 knowledge and experience 
 
 
 
 Project Controller Personal Skills and Qualifications relevant to the Role 
 The successful Project Controller will be the following: 
 
 
 Well organised 
 
 
 Adaptable, with the ability to multi-task. 
 
 
 Ability to interact with people at all levels, internally and externally. 
 
 
 Pro-active outlook and attitude.
