 Senior Narrative Designer 
 Shiro Games Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 December 18, 2020 
 Full Time 
 Bordeaux, FR 
 Remote 
 The studio Shiro Games is at the origin of  Evoland ,  Darksburg  and the multi million seller  Northgard . Since 2012, we have been committed to creating games that speak to us personally, with small development teams. We work fast but never resort to crunch. 
 As part of the development of a still unannounced game (medieval-fantasy open world), Shiro Games is now looking for a Senior Narrative Designer. Under the direction of the Game Director, you must imagine and set up the essentials of the game's storytelling, taking into account the concepts of the Universe and gameplay interactions. 
 ‍ 
 Your main responsibilities: 
 
 The design and writing of interactions with NPCs 
 The general level design of the different regions 
 The integration of gameplay mechanisms in this content 
 The structuring and evolution of documents related to the universe 
 
 ‍ 
 Your profile: 
 
 Experience 3 years or more in Game Design and / or Narrative Design with at least video game title marketed 
 Writing skills are essential 
 Excellent level of communication with the team 
 A good level of English 
 
 ‍ 
 Additional Information: 
 
 Position based in our Bordeaux studio, in the heart of a renowned district and close to all amenities (restaurants, bars, transport, etc.) 
 Help provided during the relocation (finding accommodation plus covering the moving costs) 
 Salary according to profile 
 
 ‍ 
 Applications to be sent to  jobs@shirogames.com . 
 ‍ 
 ‍ 
 About Shiro Games 
 Shiro Games  is an independent video game development studio based in Bordeaux, France. Our team is made of talented and passionate developers who strive for excellence and are brave enough to make bold decisions on a daily basis. We're always looking for more great people with good hearts, a will to take on any challenge and the ability to bring a new perspective that will make Shiro Games an even better studio.  **
