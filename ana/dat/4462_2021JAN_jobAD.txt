 Monthly Job Demand Report 
 About This Report 
 The monthly Job Demand Report for Windsor-Essex is created using data collected by Workforce WindsorEssex from national, provincial and locally-significant job boards and career pages; however, between May 2017 and March 2019 the job demand reports used job posting data purchased from CEB Talent Neuron. 
 CURRENT REPORT 
 This most recent report covers the period of December 2020.
