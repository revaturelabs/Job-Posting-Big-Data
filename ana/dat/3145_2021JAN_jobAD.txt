 ABOUT OQC 
 At Oxford Quantum Circuits (OQC) we are building quantum computers to enable life-changing discoveries: from new drug modelisation to longer-lasting battery technology and portfolio optimisation. We are developing a cloud based quantum computer service - Quantum Computing as a Service (QCaaS) - to help our customers improve their operations and trailblaze new commercial and scientific approaches. 
 Our team is composed of 20 people with various backgrounds in quantum physics, nanotechnologies, hardware, and software. We are all bound by the desire to put OQC at the forefront of the quantum revolution and build a technology with practical applications in mind. 
 ROLE OVERVIEW 
 We are looking for an Assistant to support our Chief Experience Officer (CXO) and other members of the Executive team as needed. Our CXO looks after all our operations, from HR and recruitment to communications and marketing; from legals and IP to business development. Our Assistant role is an opportunity to gain experience in these domains and to play an integral role in the day-to-day operations of our start-up. 
 We are looking for a multi-tasker with excellent communication skills and an upbeat attitude who will help ensure that operations run efficiently. The ideal person for the job will be a proactive problem solver with a meticulous attention to detail. You will plan and orchestrate work to help the CXO meet her priorities and enable swift decision-making. 
 RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Manage information flow in a timely & accurate manner to enable swift decision-making 
 Manage the CXO's day-to-day calendar, including making appointments and prioritizing the most sensitive matters 
 Schedule and attend meetings, take minutes and ensure actions are followed through 
 Draft documents (memos, emails, presentations, reports, etc) and research data to support discussions 
 Organise, maintain and improve the office filing system (incl. online platforms) 
 Provide all other secretarial & administrative support as needed (travels, expenses, etc) 
 Support our recruitment efforts (review CVs, schedule interviews, check references) 
 Provide support in market research and analysis 
 Organise team communications and help plan internal events 
 Liaise with clients, partners, contractors and visitors 
 Provide support in organising events and represent the business at networking events when necessary 
 Solving all problems great and small 
 Liaise with teams across the business 
 Replace other assistants as needed 
 
 REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS 
 
 3+ years of administrative assistant experience 
 Experience as a 'virtual' assistant 
 Ability to meet deadlines in a fast-paced and quickly changing environment 
 Proactive and problem-solving approach with good decision-making skills 
 Excellent verbal and written communications skills 
 Outstanding organisational skills and ability to multitask 
 Ability to treat confidential information with appropriate discretion 
 Strong attention to detail 
 Ability to act as gatekeeper and escalate relevant information to executives as needed 
 Ability to work effectively with minimal supervision 
 Strong interpersonal skills 
 Knowledge of interview techniques a bonus 
 
 LOCATION & TRAVEL 
 OQC's office is located in Reading. There will be a requirement for occasional travel throughout the UK and elsewhere abroad for conferences, meetings and engineering visits. 
 **REMUNERATION  ** 
 Attractive remuneration based on experience.
