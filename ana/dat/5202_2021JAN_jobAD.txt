 Senior Front-End Web Developer 
 Channel 1 Media Solutions Inc. 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 About Our Client 
Channel 1 Media (www.channel1media.com) is an award-winning digital marketing agency that specializes in creating exciting interactive online sales tools for over 200 sports teams and venues within the Professional Sports Industry. We are looking for an experienced and talented Front-End Web Developer - skilled in bridging the art of design with the art of programming - to join our growing team! You will be responsible to accurately translate UI/UX design to actual code, resulting in engaging products for our clients. You will work closely with designers to bridge the gap between graphical design and technical implementation, to define how the applications look and operate. Your portfolio must be very strong, showcasing your best web & mobile projects. If you are a passionate individual who meets all the the criteria above AND live in the Greater Toronto Area, please apply! 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Develop cross-platform desktop and mobile responsive websites using both WordPress and other development tools 
 Accurately translate designs from the creative department using standards and accessibility compliant markup 
 Build reusable code and libraries for future use 
 Debug UI on all browsers and platforms including mobile 
 Assure that all user input is validated before submitting to back-end 
 Operate independently or as a member of a team as appropriate for individual projects 
 
 Education 
 
 Bachelor's Degree, computer science or related field 
 College Diploma, computer science or related field 
 Associate's Degree, computer science or related field 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 Minimum 5 years of experience with WordPress, web markup, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript including jQuery 
 Proficient understanding of the inner workings of WordPress, cross-browser compatibility issues, and ways to work around them 
 Proficient understanding of code versioning tools, such as Git 
 Understanding of client-side scripting and JavaScript frameworks like Vue.js or React.js 
 Some knowledge of at least one Back-End programming language such as C# / ASP.NET, Java, PHP or Node.js 
 Experience in optimizing page rendering/performance, animation, deeper level JavaScript 
 Experience with Asana or other project management tools 
 Knowledgeable in Continuous Integrations and Deployment 
 Basic understanding of server-side CSS pre-processing platforms, such as LESS and SASS 
 Proficient understanding of Webpack or similar build tools 
 Experience with connecting to API’s and integrations with other services 
 Experience with 2D/3D rendering APIs (Canvas/WebGL) 
 
 Skills 
 
 Must work from our office location in Toronto, Canada 
 Great attention to detail 
 Work well in a team 
 Perform well under stress 
 Ability to meet deadlines 
 Excellent verbal and written communication skills 
 
 Compensation 
$85,000++ per year 
 Position Type 
Full-Time
