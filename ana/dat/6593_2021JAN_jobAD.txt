 Villa Marguerite 
 __North Rustico, PE 
 Apply Now 
 Villa Marguerite located in the beautiful fishing village of North Rustico, PE is seeking Resident Care Worker/Personal Care Worker for Full Time/Part Time/Casual Employment (36-40 hrs/week)..... We are a 19 bed Community Care Facility for Seniors who no longer wish to live on their own or who need help with their activities of daily living. 
 Some of the responsibilities you will be responsible for will include: bathing/showering, shaving, hair care, assisting with dental hygiene, nail care, medication administration, light housekeeping duties, making and changing beds, laundry, help with serving meals, also supervise meals in dining room, communicate & document any changes in resident health & needs. 
 Qualifications would include valid Standard First Aid & CPR Certificates, Criminal & Vunerable Sector Check 
 Successful applicant must have an excellent work ethic, ability to multi task, excellent communication and documentation skills, friendly, personable, self motivated and responsible. 
 Experience an asset, however we will train successful candidate...... 
 Job Types: Full-time, Part-time, Casual
