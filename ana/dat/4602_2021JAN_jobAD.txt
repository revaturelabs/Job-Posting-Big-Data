 Role overview 
 Join the most cutting-edge and well-funded hardware startup in Silicon Valley as a Data Center Software Architect. Our mission is to reimagine silicon and create computing platforms that will transform the industry. You will have the opportunity to work with some of the most talented and passionate engineers in the world to create designs that push the envelope on performance, energy efficiency and scalability. We offer a fun, creative and flexible work environment, with a shared vision to build products to change the world. 
 As Data Center Software Architect, you are responsible for unifying our software development teams through technical direction. In external engagements, you will be leading our software engagements with customers and partners. You will define software architecture and requirements for data center solutions, engage in customer programs, assist in building an ecosystem partnership program, and help build our world class engineering team. You will be responsible for ensuring that we deliver the highest quality and feature robust software solutions. You will have an opportunity to engage broadly with our customers and third parties on enablement of new platforms, and optimizations in our microarchitecture. 
 Minimum qualifications: 
 
 BS / MS in an engineering discipline (or equivalent professional experience) with 10 years experience working with server ecosystems, open source, etc. 
 Highly proficient in computer architecture concepts such as data coherency, PCIe, NVDIMM, virtualization and ARM based servers 
 Work experience in delivering high performance server class compute architectures and platforms 
 Experience in collaborating with customers and enabling platform design 
 Self-starter who takes initiative and brings collaborative technical leadership 
 
 Preferred qualifications: 
 
 10 years+ of experience in building software stacks for server class platforms 
 Experience in working ‚Äúupstream first‚Äù with the Linux kernel community 
 Deep knowledge of server platform specifications such as SBSA, SBBR, PCIe, UEFI, ACPI and TPM 
 Strong grasp of the Arm ServerReady compliance program 
 Good knowledge and experience with ODP reference model 
 
 Role and Responsibilities: 
 
 Lead the definition of future server class SoCs and platforms and collaborate with silicon designers and software teams on features for compatibility, power, performance and scalability 
 Engage with key customers on data center programs and partnerships 
 Drive the software enablement of platforms through the open source community 
 Assist in definition and participate in the development of an ecosystem partnership program with major industry partners 
 Establish engagements within the Open Source community, and ultimately build an ‚Äúupstream first‚Äù Open Source developer program that builds our reputation for understanding the community and doing things ‚Äúthe right way‚Äù from day one
