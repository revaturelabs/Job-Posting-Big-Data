 Full-Time Controller

 Job Description 
 S Group is comprised of the Corporation and its subsidiaries, regional cooperatives. S Group provides grocery and consumer goods, service station and fuel trade, tourism, hospitality business, automotive, automotive accessories, as well as agricultural trade in services. 
 In addition, S-Bank offers banking services to its customer-owners. S Group is owned by two million cooperative members, or customer-owners. S-Group's retail sales (VAT 0%) in 2011 was 11.5 billion euros. 
 We are working in the S-group is already more than 42 000 S Group offers a modern and evolving work environment in a big house with its advantages. Economic SOK SOK Corporation is responsible for financial planning and follow-up, the S Group's financial operations, as well as the development of common financial and payroll administration services rendered. 
 Job description: 
 
 Internal accounting and reporting for customers 
 Budgeting process participation 
 Separate orders we expect 
 A suitable business training (Bachelor of Business Administration / KTM) 
 After a few years of work experience in internal or external accounting tasks 
 Good Excel skills 
 Accuracy and independent mindset 
 Positive attitude and service orientation 
 
 We value experience in SAP and Cognos software. Good management of the Office programs is also an advantage. 
 We offer: 
 
 a varied and interesting job description 
 Opportunity to develop your skills and common ways of working 
 for highly skilled team and a large support organization 
 the S Group's employee benefits
