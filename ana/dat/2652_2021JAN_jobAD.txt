 Entale is an exciting new way to listen to and discover new podcasts. Through the use of advanced AI and a cutting-edge publisher platform, we dynamically show listeners pictures, links, articles, social media posts and other relevant and eye-catching content that means they can delve deeper into podcasts as they listen via our mobile app. 
 This Spring we're launching Collections: a unique way to save and share podcasts and content, which will have a huge impact on how our users can find the next thing to listen to. 
 As a venture-funded startup, we are out to disrupt the world of podcasting, by bringing this experience to as many listeners as possible. We are seeking a passionate part-time content manager to support the growth of our app. 
 The Role 
 
 Create and manage our featured collections across a huge variety of topics and always be on the hunt for new and interesting shows, episodes, snippets and links to include 
 Build an engaged community around our users by sending newsletters to explain new features, encouraging early feedback and extracting key customer insights to feed into product development 
 Manage our website and social media channels, using them to communicate our unique approach to podcasts 
 Work directly with the product team to design new customer experiences from first hearing about Entale, to downloading the app, to sharing their first collection 
 The role is a fixed term 6 month contract and is part-time (4 hours per day) 
 
 Requirements 
 
 1-2 years in an editorial content or content marketing role 
 Outstanding written communication skills 
 Eye for design and creating on-brand content 
 Superior analytical skills - you know how to tell what's working 
 Experience with mobile apps a plus 
 Experience with paid advertising platforms a plus 
 You need to love podcasts and be curious about a huge range of topics!
