 Sales Representative (HR Services) - Lagos

 Description 
 What your responsibilities are going to be: 
 Find new customers: generating leads and direct sales for the local market. Creating a network of stable clients that use the platform on a current basis. 
 Marketing activities: Implementing the marketing strategy with two objectives - creating awareness for WorkInAfrica.com among potential clients and bringing candidates on the portal. 
 Administrative activities: Tracking contract signing with the clients, sending invoices and making sure the invoices are paid. 
 Managing the platform locally: you will be responsible for setting-up the clients on the platform, daily management and first level support. 
 Strategic input: We want you to be part of the business and have an impact at all levels, including the strategic one. 
 Other info 
 What will you get in return: 
- Specialised training and constant support in your activity 
- International exposure 
- Motivational incentive scheme 
- Opportunity to work with an enthusiastic team of professionals 
 VERY IMPORTANT ! 
No financial investment is needed from your side. You will join the network based on your desire to take part in the growth of a new, disruptive way of doing recruitment in your country and you will be rewarded based on your performance. 
 Apply 
 Requirements 
 What are we looking for: 
- 1-2 years of experience in selling services 
- very good English language skills 
- strong motivation for developing a career in sales 
- excellent communication and negotiation abilities 
- proactive and results oriented attitude 
- dynamic, smart and motivated individuals 
- good PC skills, use of Internet knowledge, MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) 
- very good phone sales abilities 
 Nice to have: 
- experience in fund-raising for student NGOs 
- experience in selling HR services, online advertising, or previously worked in telesales 
- business academic background
