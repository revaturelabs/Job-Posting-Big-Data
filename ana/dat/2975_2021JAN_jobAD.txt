 Registered General Nurse 
 An amazing new job opportunity has just arisen for a committed RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Southport, Merseyside area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 Our clients are a family run care group providing quality residential and nursing care for the elderly. 
 They provide a full range care from residential to nursing care needs for both long term and respite periods 
 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Ensure the highest possible standards of care, assessing residents' needs and wishes, and developing services to enhance their quality of life
 
 
 
     Champion appropriate independence and personal choice; developing, reviewing and updating care plans to meet our residents physical, social and psychological needs
 
 
 
     Oversee all aspects of medicine management on your shift - ensuring medicines are appropriately received, stored and administered, in accordance with company policies and current legislation
 
 
 
     Build the positive reputation of the Business; liaise professionally with visitors and other external stakeholders
 
 
 
     Ensure all elements of resident experience are positive, from admission to discharge.
 
 
 
     Participate knowledgably and professionally to all inspection visits, ensuring your team is ready at all times to do the same
 
 
 
     Ensure compliance with all legal, regulatory and best practice guidelines - identify, investigate and resolve risks proactively
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     You must have excellent communication skills
 
 
 
     Excellent written skills
 
 
 
     Have previous experience working within a nursing home or elderly care setting
 
 
 
     Self-motivated, resilient and well-organised
 
 
 
     You will be a team player but able to work on your own initiative
 
 
 
     You will be dependable, able to work well under pressure and flexible to meet the needs of the residents and the home.
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will be offered an excellent salary of £16.75 per hour. 
 This exciting position is a Part Time role for 12 hours every Saturday Night. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits: ** 
 
 
     Comprehensive induction and on-going training
 
 
 
     Career development opportunities
 
 
 
     Excellent work environment
 
 
 
     Free parking on site
 
 
 
     Free uniform
 
 
 
     5.6 weeks of paid annual leave
