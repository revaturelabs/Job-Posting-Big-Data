 Role overview 
 We at the Messaging Team  , own everything under the messaging domain at Truecaller - building, transforming, optimizing existing and new features to make messaging more reliable and secure. Currently, we are building a business messaging experience that will help users to communicate with enterprises efficiently. 
 We are in search of someone who has an interest in system architecture, but a passion for getting things done. Willing to work with smart people and have fun building something great. Your challenge will be to build a scalable and reliable system while facing quickly growing global traffic. You will also be coding new features and have an active role in the definition of the backend architecture; which includes designing microservices and researching new alternatives and technologies together with the platform team. 
 What we expect: 
 
 7+ years working experience work with Java, at least 2+ years experience with Scala 
 Solid experience with microservice architecture and patterns. 
 Experience working in a complex tech environment 
 Experience with non-relational databases 
 Experience with Akka 
 Good understanding and working experience with high load and low-latency distributed systems 
 
 What you will work on: 
 
 Building new features and have an active role in the definition of the backend architecture. 
 Effectively break down and scope of that work into workable chunks for 2 or more backend engineers. 
 Design core backend software components. 
 Build team cohesion and help them when they are stuck. 
 Conduct design and code reviews 
 Analyze and improve efficiency, scalability, and stability of various systems.
