 It may be cold and dreary outside this first day of December, but we rounded up some of the hottest jobs in the Triangle anyway! 
 
 Fidelity  seeks a  Design Researcher  to incubate new business models! 
 Work for  BASF  as a  Marketing Communications Specialist ! Creativity needed! 
 Are you a computer pro?  Sensus  seeks a  software engineer  to build game-changing software. 
 Duke University seeks a  Program Director, Special Events  for  Nasher Museum . 
 Can you handle multiple tasks at once?  Liquidia  seeks a  Project Manager . 
 Siemens , a global technology company seeks a  VP of Customer Service. 
 TrialCard  seeks a  Pharmaceutical District Manager, Inside Sales Team . 
 INC Research  seeks an  Associate Director of Corporate Communications .   
 Lulu , free print & eBook publishing company, seeks for a talented  UX Designer . 
 Duke Clinical Research Institute  seeks an  Assistant Director, Clinical Operations , experience as a healthcare professional a plus.
