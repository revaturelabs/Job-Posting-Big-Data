 Registered General Nurse 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN Nurse to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Shipley, West Yorkshire area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading healthcare providers 
 This special nursing home is a home for young adults with disabilities. 
 It cares for people who have a variety of specialist needs including amputees, people with brain injuries Multiple Sclerosis (MS), Motor Neuron Disease, Huntington's Disease, Epilepsy and many other complex conditions 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Accurately assess the needs of residents and identify priorities of care
 
 
 
     Administer all medication and treatments within the NMC guidelines and company's medication policy
 
 
 
     Ensure that residents care plans are completed and maintained in conjunction with residents, relatives and other health care professionals
 
 
 
     Report any ill-health amongst residents and make request for GP/professional visit where necessary
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Provide both residential and nursing care to older people who have physical disabilities.
 
 
 
     Able to show empathy and warmth
 
 
 
     A bubbly and outgoing personality
 
 
 
     Dementia experience
 
 
 
     Able to write care plans
 
 
 
     Assist in assessments
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £17.50 per hour and the overtime rate is £20.50 per hour. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time position for 35 hours a week, working throughout Day Shifts. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     28 days paid holiday
 
 
 
     Smart uniforms
 
 
 
     Free CRB checks
 
 
 
     Four days induction and NVQ training
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a current active NMC Pin
