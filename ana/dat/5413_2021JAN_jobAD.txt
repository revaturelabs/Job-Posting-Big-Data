 HSSE Business Partner 
 
 Location 
 
 Manchester, Greater Manchester 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Built Environment 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £45000.00 - £55000.00 per annum + car, pension, bonus 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Stephen Brandsma 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 stephen.brandsma@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH22243_1608642160 
 
 Published: 
 
 about 1 month ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2021-01-21 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 We are working on an exclusive basis with a world-leading transport logistics company, helping them recruit a high-calibre Health, Safety, Security and Environment Business Partner based from the North West area. 
 Reporting to the Head of HSSE as part of a national team, this role will provide independent advice, guidance and support to the service managers in the specified region to ensure the effective and efficient management and continuous improvement in Health, Safety, Security and Environment. As the successful HSSE Business Partner you will be focusing on frontline support to embed new tools, the updated safety management system and drive adoption of a more Human Factors based approach in support of management HSSE training. 
 As the successful HSSE Business Partner you will ideally have a proven track record working within the road, rail or maritime logistics sectors and knowledge of the niche requirements these sectors have along with experience working in a unionized environment. Other sectors may be considered but rail, road or maritime logistics experience is ideal. You will likely be a lead auditor and be able to demonstrate that you are committed, motivated and posses highly effective people skills. 
 In return the successful HSSE Business Partner will receive a highly competitive salary of £45-55,000 dependent on experience. In addition there is a company car or car allowance on offer along with access to an exceptional pension and private healthcare scheme. Our client also offers a bonus of up to 10% with the position. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
