 Medical Device Reprocessing Technician 
 Oakville Endoscopy Centre 
 __Oakville, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We are a friendly and busy medical outpatient endoscopy clinic based in Oakville, Ontario. 
 We are looking for a part time or full time  medical device reprocessing technician . The ideal candidate is someone who has experience with infection control setting, and knows how to follow all the rules and regulations. Must be a quick learner, responsible, reliable, friendly, mature, and able to work independently. 
 Job Description 
 
 Removing the scope from procedure room and sterilizing the area 
 Cleaning and sterilizing all scopes during the day 
 Cleaning and sterilizing scope station after each use 
 Sterilizing the water bottle assemblies 
 Checking and ensuring all needed supplies are on the premise 
 Performing opening and closing cleaning routine for reprocessing area 
 
 Required Experience 
 Experience in the MDR field is an asset 
 Job Types: Full-time, Part-time 
 Salary: $18.00 /hour 
 Licence: 
 
 Medical Device Reprocessing Certificate (Required)
