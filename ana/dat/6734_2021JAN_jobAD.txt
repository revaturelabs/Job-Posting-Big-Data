 It's the last day in June! Therefore, we are going out with a bang. Check out all our HOT JOBS this week. 
 Burts Bees  seeks a  Sales Planning Team Manger ! Work for a company with the highest standards. 
 Biogen  is hiring a  Project Engineer  to handle all aspects of a portfolio of Capital Projects. 
 Have you ever heard of an  Innovation Evangelist ?  Metlife  is hiring one to drive innovation into the culture of MetLife. The Innovation Evangelist is a unique leader who will be responsible for driving a variety of innovation efforts across the globe. 
 Are you a master of data?  Red Hat  needs you as a  Business Analyst ! 
 ReverbNation  makes killer tools for artist and musicians and is hiring an  Interface Designer  to create simply, yet elegant solutions to interface problems. 
 Liquidia_Inc  a local biotech company known for its PRINT technology seeks a  Director of Non-Clinical Development . 
 Fidelity  is hiring a  Financial Analyst  for Fidelity Charitable. LordCorporation  seeks a Sr. Tax Analyst to join their innovative & diverse team.  http://ow.ly/OZpm7   Apply now! 
 HealthGrades  helps consumers find the right doctor and right hospital, for the right care and seeks a  Marketing Coordinator . 
 Lord Corporation  seeks a  Sr. Tax Analyst  to join their innovative and diverse team. 
 Deloitte , provides industry-leading audit, consulting, tax, and advisory services to many of the world's most admired brands, and seeks a  Sales Operations Manager - Life Sciences . Apply today!
