 SAP Implementation Specialist - Project Manager 
 SAP Implementation Specialist - Project Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 London, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Temporary 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £375 - £400 per day + + bens 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Fiona Price 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 fiona.price@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 FRP22149_1606142173 
 
 Published: 
 
 2 months ago 
 
 Duration: 
 
 6 weeks 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-11-23 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Our client, is a successful, global business, with a small UK Office but with a US parent, who are currently in need of a SAP Implementation Specialist, who is immediately available, to come on board for about 6 weeks to head up the final stages of this project. 
 This role is requiring a Project Manager, with a finance background, who is hands-on, who will lead the final stages of this exciting project, to implement SAP. 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group have been chosen exclusively to source a talented Project Manager to finalise this last 6 weeks of this project, to implement SAP. 
 Your profile as the successful SAP Implementation Specialist - Project Manager will include: 
 - Being immediately available and able to commit to the 6 week role 
 - Offer a finance background, combined with a passion for systems 
 - Previous and recent SAP implementation knowledge (ideally SAP BY Design or SAP Hana) 
 - A forward-thinking and personable approach to your work 
 - Be able to work closely with the US Head Office 
 - Have focus on concluding projects and to get them through this last 6 weeks 
 - Be able to work remotely (this role will be home based at all times) 
 - Be able to be the key interface with the ERP consultants, finance, systems and Head Office functions 
 - Be engaging with a positive attitude 
 - Be able to design reporting, training and help wherever needed 
 This is a superb, and unique role to be hands-on, in this exciting project. You must be able to start straight away and be able to commit to this 6 week assignment, that has a high potential of extension. Please do get in touch for additional details and a full job spec. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
