 Company Description 
 How about flexible hours that let you do your thing, whatever your thing is. The opportunity to grow. Professionally, personally and financially. How about the chance to be part of a company that’s primarily run by independent business owners and that’s active in your community. That’s helped more than 300,000 Canadian families with sick kids through Ronald McDonald House. If that sounds good to you, then we’d love to hear from you. 
 Job Description 
 We're looking for energetic individuals to join our team! We are looking for crew members who are eager to learn, can work in a fast paced environment, is able to work in a team setting along with working independently. Ideally our part time crew members are available to work until midnight throughout the week, and have an open availability on the weekends. 
 Qualifications 
 We will train all crew members in full, customer service experience is a plus. 
 Additional Information 
 Any interview for this position will be held over a ZOOM video call, all prospective candidates will be notified of a time, date, and link to join the call!
