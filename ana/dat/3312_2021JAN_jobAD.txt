 We're looking for software engineers that want to write great code for real customers 
 Role overview 
 Company Description: 
 Pelotech helps enterprise clients leverage Kubernetes and other cloud technology to solve core business challenges. We help them deploy distributed, highly available, services. 
 We help the worlds largest companies build globe spanning technology products and provide expert guidance on scaling technology solutions at all levels. 
 In support of this ambition we: 
 
 Cherish and champion values and practices that create trust, collaboration, and measured improvement; 
 Work with folks that do the same. 
 
 We are not a consultancy that talks at you, nor a "body shop" contract company without an outside perspective. We teach software acceleration by working alongside our customers. 
 Job Description: 
 Join:  We're looking for software engineers that want to write great code for real customers consistently and love to teach their team how to do the same. You are an experienced player-coach in creating amazing software teams, have proven technical chops in current CI/CD, immutable infrastructure, micros-service technologies. 
 You: 
 
 Can be the primary customer point of contact 
 Are not phased by ambiguity and guide others through it 
 Relentlessly learn new technology that improves the speed of which you can achieve your goals. 
 Value empathy and strive to build solutions that teams can understand. 
 Are effective at guiding and counseling your team's managers. 
 
 You have: 
 
 A passion in working full-stack, from coding cloud infrastructure to building distributed systems and front end web applications. 
 A Passion for building products that are beautiful outside and inside â€“ from their user experience to their robust and elegant design. 
 Data and metrics-oriented mindset: you want your work to be measurable and impactful. 
 Customer empathy: you understand your customersâ€™ needs and requirements. 
 Strong software development skills and computer science fundamentals. 
 A broad knowledge of entrepreneurial and work management frameworks such as Lean Startup, Design Thinking, Kanban, Scrum 
 Value loosely-held strong-opinions, sharing them and having them challenged. 
 An inquisitive mind and enjoy chasing down problems and opportunities across technology stacks and domains. 
 
 Even better: 
 
 A history of contributing to open source 
 A fondness for pair programming 
 
 Qualifications 
 Must have: 
 
 Strong skills in several programming languages and an understanding of when to use which.
