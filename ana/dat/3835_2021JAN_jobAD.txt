 Transcripción 
 Published on the May 10, 2018 in Writing & Translation 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 writing-translation / others-6 
 USD 50 - 100 
 Sigma Technologies Global is a tech company located in Spain specializing in pattern recognition and natural language processing technologies. We are currently looking for responsible, independent native-level Danish and Norwegian speakers to provide transcription and validation services. 
 The transcription work is done from home on an online platform that is available 24 hours a day Monday to Sunday. We regularly have projects available in Danish and Norwegian that last approximately 2-3 months depending on our clients. 
 On our current project: 
• Your task will be to listen to short utterances, validate and correct the corresponding transcription according to the manual provided. 
• The hourly objective is 200 transcriptions minimum. 
• You can do more work than your objectives. 
• The pay per validated transcription is €0.04- 0,045 gross all included (vacations, compensation, etc). 
 Requirements: 
• A secure/private internet connection, 
• A computer with headphones, 
• Native-level oral and written comprehension in Danish/Norwegian, 
• The ability to write in Danish/Norwegian without spelling mistakes, 
• High typing speed (the ability to touch type is a plus), 
• Motivation to achieve objectives, 
• Pass an online test of 50 audios (transcriptions).
