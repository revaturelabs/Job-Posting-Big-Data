 About this Role 
 This is the first dedicated position of its type charged with growing Chainlink, and it has tremendous growth potential into a senior management role as we rapidly expand our coverage of any/all blockchains. Enabling a deep integration with many different blockchains is a big part of Chainlinkâ€™s ability to act as the middleware standard that will drive the adoption of blockchains in general, both by enterprises and various startup teams. You will work directly with the senior management team to evaluate which chains to integrate, oversee integration, and represent Chainlink in each respective community, and ensure that Chainlinkâ€™s usefulness for each additional blockchain is maintained. We are a fully distributed team and have the tools and benefits to support you in your remote work environment. 
 Your Impact 
 
 Work directly with the companyâ€™s founders to form and execute a larger blockchain integration strategy for Chainlink
