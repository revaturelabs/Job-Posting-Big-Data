 We're expanding! Mahalo is a disruptive online food & drink distributor looking for an outstanding Sales Manager to join our close knit team. This role is a remote, work from home position with flexible hours to suit your lifestyle. 
 Our clients are the loveliest people: owners of independent farm shops and delis across the country who all need help filling their shelves with great food and drink products. We're looking for someone jolly who loves food and championing independents. 
 What will you be doing? 
 
 Working closely with our Head Of Sales to proactively grow turnover 
 Generating new leads and finding ways to continually secure new customers 
 Looking after existing customers and maintaining strong personal relationships 
 Managing incoming enquiries and supporting the onboarding process 
 Getting customer feedback and relating this back to the team 
 Managing our CRM database and keeping information on our customers up to date 
 
 What are you like? 
 
 A confident communicator on the phone, with strong persuasion skills 
 A super people person, happiest building relationships and engaging with new people 
 Proactive self starter and able to troubleshoot independently 
 Highly organised and responsive, with a methodical approach to sales 
 Tech savvy, comfortable using virtual phone systems, email and social media 
 Goal driven and focused on meeting sales targets 
 Full of entrepreneurial spirit and focused on fast paced growth 
 
 What kind of experience have you got? 
 
 At least 2 years experience in a customer facing sales role, ideally in FMCG 
 Demonstrable experience of achieving commercial targets 
 Proven track record of driving the entire sales cycle from lead generation to close 
 Good understanding of CRM systems, VOIP, G Suite, Slack and other apps 
 Experience working with small businesses, ideally in FMCG 
 
 What 's great about this role? 
 
 Opportunity to be part of a growing team and actively shape growth 
 Working with small independent businesses and emerging food brands 
 Working remotely, hours are flexible to suit your lifestyle 
 Free samples of exciting products that are totally new to market 
 
 The finer details 
 
 We are open to candidates looking for full time and part time roles though we require a minimum of 24 hours per week 
 Competitive salary plus bonus 
 Private healthcare insurance
