 We are seeking an experienced Scheduler for our Edmonton Industrial District office located in Edmonton, AB. This position will transition to a project-site based location. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Assist in the planning and scheduling process through various construction phases (preconstruction, construction, system completions, etc.); 
 Retrieve, consolidate and analyze information from various sources; 
 Collaborate with field supervision on look ahead planning; 
 Implement subcontractor schedules into the master project schedule; 
 Apply schedule scope changes in alignment with contract requirements; 
 Identify schedule delays, impacts and variances to the baseline; and 
 Communicate plans and schedules internally and externally through effective graphs and reports. 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Minimum 3 years of industry experience; 
 1-4 years of dedicated planning and scheduling with direct hire trades and subcontractors; 
 Position based in Edmonton office to start with transition to camp based project; 
 General understanding of planning and scheduling principles, best practices and techniques; 
 Basic knowledge of Oracle Primavera P6 and advanced knowledge in Microsoft Excel; 
 Utilize information from construction drawings, specifications, contracts and proposal documents; 
 An Engineering Technology Diploma or Engineering Degree preferred but not mandatory; 
 Capacity to prioritize work under strict timelines and be adaptable to changing needs; 
 A keen eye for detail; 
 Effective listening and oral communication skills (including spoken, written and visual); 
 Self-starter that shows initiative; 
 Follows up on issues to work towards solutions; and 
 Comfortable working with a variety of people including estimators, field supervision, subcontractors, project management and clients 
 
 Working Conditions: 
The ability to work in a project-site.
