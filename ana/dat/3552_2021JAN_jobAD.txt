 Deputy Manager 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Deputy Manager to work in an exceptional care home based in the Cockermouth, Cumbria area. 
 As the Deputy Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special care home is a purpose built care home which supports people with physical and dementia need who require assistance and care 
 As the Deputy Manager your key duties include: 
 
 
     Capable of making decisions in line with company policy and procedure whilst having the service users best interests at the heart of the decision making process
 
 
 
     Required to line manage a team of staff and have knowledge
 
 
 
     Understanding and experience of relevant legislation ensuring they comply with the requirements of CQC and other statutory regulators
 
 
 
     You will take pride in providing the highest standards of care to all residents and manage the Service to the highest standards
 
 
 
     Ensuring the people who use our services can be supported to live as independently as possible and achieve their goals
 
 
 
 An ideal Deputy Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Experience in a similar role that has the passion and drive support the site into becoming an outstanding service
 
 
 
     Excellent people management skills
 
 
 
     Strong clinical knowledge and experience in change management
 
 
 
     Significant experience in leading a large team of Registered Nurses and Support Workers
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Manager will receive an excellent salary of £18.50 - £19.00 per hour. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 38.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Enhanced disclosure cost coverage
 
 
 
     28 days annual leave inclusive of bank holidays
 
 
 
     Online benefits and cashback rewards
 
 
 
     Extensive tailored training packages (Including internal & external training) & support with CPD
 
 
 
     Gym Membership, dental insurance, travel insurance, cycle to work scheme, eye care vouchers and simply health
 
 
 
     Pensions (Standard Life & auto enrolment scheme NEST)
 
 
 
     Career progression opportunities within the adult care division and the company
 
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN, RMN or RNLD with a current active NMC Pin
