 Job Title:  Quality Assurance Inspector 
 Job Duration:  Temporary to Hire 
 Pay Rate:  $12-$16/hour BOE 
 Hours:  8am - 4:30pm 
 Location:  Crystal Lake, IL 
 Job Description: 
 
 Approves production products by confirming specifications 
 Conducting visual, electrical or measurement tests 
 Communicating failures to appropriate personnel 
 Other duties as assigned 
 
 Job Duration: 
 
 Minimum of 2-years of experience in an inspection role within an IPC-610 compliant electronics manufacturing environment. 5-years or more desired 
 Able to use microscopes, calipers and other measurement tools 
 Able to read and interpret product drawings, blueprints and written specifications 
 Ability to visually discern small product features and recognize defect conditions 
 Must be able to read and communicate in English 
 Demonstrable working knowledge of IPC-610 standard is required. Certification is a plus 
 Ability to lift up to 30-pounds and maneuver for short distances 
 Basic computer skills desired, but not required
