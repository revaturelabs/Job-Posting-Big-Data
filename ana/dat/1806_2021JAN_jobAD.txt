 For one of out boutique consulting clients specialising in Business Intelligence, Digitalisation / Industry 4.0 & Business Process Management we are currently searching for a  Head of Big Data Analytics Competence Center (m/f). 
 --------------- 
 Your profile: 
 
 Completed studies in computer science or a similar field 
 Multiple years in a disciplinary leadership position 
 Extensive practical experience in the analysis of large amounts of data as well as the application of machine learning algorithms 
 Good knowledge of data analysis, data visualization toolds, query languages ​​as well as (big data) databases 
 Fluency in German and English 
 Willingness to travel across German-speaking Europe 
 
 --------------- 
 Your tasks: 
 
 Development, expansion and management of the Big Data Analytics Competence Center 
 Acquisition of new clients, and further support and development of existing customer relationships 
 Recruitment of new analytics experts to continue the growth of the department 
 Operational implementation of won projects (technical and technical conception, realization, management and controlling) 
 Act as the internal interface to the management team, reporting regularly to senior partners 
 
 ---------------
