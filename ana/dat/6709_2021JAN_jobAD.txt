 Caregiver Job Vacancies Singapore 
 Posted on  August 13, 2012  by  caregiver 
 One of the POEA duly licensed recruitment agencies, whose offices are located in Ermita, Manila and Benguet, is in need of  Caregivers cum Domestic Helpers  for placement in  Caregiver Job Vacancies Singapore . These caregiver job vacancies Singapore were made available to interested applicants thru the POEA approval of its Job Order last August 6, 2012 allowing the recruiting agency to recruit 50 applicants to fill the said caregiver job vacancies Singapore. 
 If you wish to be included in the list of possible candidates to fill the said Caregiver Job Vacancies Singapore, proceed as soon as possible to any of the offices of OFW Recruitment Agency and personally submit your comprehensive and updated resume.
