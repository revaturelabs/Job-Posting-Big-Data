 Gleeson Recruitment Group are supporting a well known retail brand based in Nuneaton who are recruiting for a qualified accountant (ACA/ACCA/CIMA) to join them as a Commercial Finance Business Partner on a permanent basis 
 Main Duties: Finance Business Partner, Nuneaton 
 
 Drive the impact and understanding of financial information through insightful reporting and interpretation to influence decision making and promote business performance across the Business. 
 Finance Business partner / Decision Support for all areas of the Business. 
 Present industry, competitor, consumer and economic trends to provide context. 
 Advise on key business planning assumptions, risks, opportunities and correction plans. 
 Support future strategic planning and target setting. 
 Responsibility for production of rolling forecasts and annual Budgets. 
 Partner respective Business areas from a Finance perspective. 
 Develop KPI analysis both financial and operational, revenue and profitability analysis 
 Evaluate capex investment plans 
 Building a greater understanding and helping to drive the profitable success of the overall business. 
 Provide insight and analysis in assisting the team to ensure targets and goals are met 
 ROI analysis by channel 
 Continuous performance management and Budget optimisation with stakeholders 
 Monthly reporting schedules prepared for the Corporate board. 
 Instrumental in the compiling of annual budgets in line for your area of the business 
 Ensuring scheduled reports are produced on time and efficiently. Promote continuous improvement 
 Assist the Senior Finance Business Partners (Retail, Digital, Trading, Commercial, International) with ad-hoc analysis projects and insight 
 
 If you are a qualified accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) looking for a highly commercial Finance Business Partner role within a fast paced and complex business based in Nuneaton please do get in touch 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
