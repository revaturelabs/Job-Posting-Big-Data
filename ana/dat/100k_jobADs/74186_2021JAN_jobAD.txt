As a recognized technical information technology (IT) expert in evaluating available tools, methodologies, and processes for feasibility in improving the OFMS effectiveness and efficiency in meeting the requirements, functions, and services of its automated financial systems. The employee provides authoritative advice and assistance to OCFO senior management on IT software and/or hardware that will enhance the financial programs and services provided by the OFMS organization.The duties include:Identifying opportunities to improve the quality and performance of financial management systems, and leverages common standards and shared service solutions at the USPTO.Refining functional financial systems requirements and directs the preparation of, or independently prepares technical specifications, statements of work, task orders, and other acquisition related documentation necessary to effectively obtain and administer automated information system projects.Ensures enforcement of IT security policies, principles, and practices for all production systems.Participates in activities related to the Certification and Accreditation process as well as the Annual Security Assessment.Participates in Agile methodologies.Defines project/task requirements through appropriate planning, implementations, reporting on, and evaluating projects and tasks.Travel RequiredNot requiredSupervisory statusNoPromotion Potential13RequirementsConditions of EmploymentYou must be a U.S. Citizen or NationalYou must meet the definition of specialized experience and IT competencies.Required to pass a background investigation and fingerprint check.Must be registered for Selective Service, if applicable (www.sss.gov)Financial Disclosure Statement (OGE Form 450) is required.•A probationary period may be required.QualificationsYou must meet the United States Office of Personnel Management's (OPM) qualification requirements (including specialized experience and/or educational requirements) for the advertised position. Specialized experience for this position includes:Experience serving as an system analyst, in the development of new or existing automated systems with experience in Agile and/or Waterfall software development methodologies, for a multibillion-dollar company or agency.Experience functioning as an system analyst in the operation and maintenance of systems including enforcing IT security policies, principles, and standards.In addition to meeting specialized experience requirements you must have the following IT-related competencies:Attention to Detail-Strives for completeness when performing assigned tasks, with minimal errors.Customer Service-Responds to business partner or customer needs as they pertain to your assigned area of work.
