The fiber network has spare capacity that broadband service providers can lease to provide high-speed internet access to their customer in Anaheim. Here’s the link to view the fiber optics lease agreement: Sample fiber optics lease agreement and fiber lease rates.Wireless NetworkMajor cell phone companies (AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and Sprint) are working to improve service for their customers in Anaheim. As part of these improvements, they are replacing some of the streetlight poles to allow installing their wireless equipment inside an enclosure at the top. These installations are referred to as Small Cell Sites.
