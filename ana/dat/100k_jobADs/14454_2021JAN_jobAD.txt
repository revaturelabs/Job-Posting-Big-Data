Outpatient care also includes the Family Health Centers at NYU Langone, one of the largest Federally Qualified Health Center networks in the nation.Also part of NYU Langone Health is the Laura and Isaac Perlmutter Cancer Center, a National Cancer Institute designated cancer center, recognized for its basic laboratory, translational, and clinical research. Its five primary research areas include cancer genome dynamics, cancer cell biology, melanoma, tumor immunology, and epidemiology and cancer control.Since 1841, NYU Grossman School of Medicine has trained thousands of physicians and scientists who have helped to shape the course of medical history, and again ranked among the top 10 in the nation for research on the 2020 U.S. News & World Report “Best Graduate Schools” rankings.
