Applications from eligible candidates are invited by the DRDO GTRE for recruitment to 150 posts of Graduate / Diploma and ITI Apprentices for the recruitment year 2021. Candidates having B.E. / B.Tech Degree or Engineering Diploma or ITI Diploma in Mechanical Engineering / Civil Engineering / Electrical & Electronics Engineering or the branches / discipline mentioned below can apply online for this recruitment.Name of Exam: DRDO GTRE Graduate / Diploma and ITI Apprentice Recruitment 2021.Name of Organization: Defence Research and Development Organisation – Gas Turbine Research Establishment (DRDO-GTRE)Post Name: Graduate Apprentice, Diploma Apprentice and ITI Apprentice.Total Vacancy: Total vacancies notified by the DRDO GTRE are 150.
