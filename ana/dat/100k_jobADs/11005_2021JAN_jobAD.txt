Its a 3 years duration diploma after matriculation with science subjects, which helps you a lot in finding a job immediately after completion of your studies. DAE is all about planning, maintenance, evaluating, designing, application, production and implementation. It is a professional and vocational training based diploma which equips you with the skills badly needed in job market. DAE is equivalent to FSc PreYou may also get admission in B.Tech after DAE. It is highly recommended for DAE diploma holders to get admission in B.Tech as it will increase your chances of getting reasonable job in your field. DAE is abbreviation of Diploma in Associate Engineering. DAE is the best route for those students who want to become engineer but their marks are not good Matric. Such students should prefer DAE on FSc Pre Engineering. Still we can nor rule out the importance of college. Chose a college which has best laboratories and faculty. Provincial boards of technical education conducts the exams of DAE and grant diplomas to the successful students. DAE diploma awarded by Skill Development Council SDC is not recognized by majority of industries.Eligibility CriteriaCandidates must have passed the matriculation exam with science subjects. Matric tech students are given preference. Public colleges grant admission on merit base only. Specially marks in Physics, Chemistry, English and Mathematics are considered for calculation of merit.Career & ScopeThere is great demand of DAE diploma holder associate engineers not only in Pakistan but also abroad.Every year large number of DAE diploma holders gets job in different industries of Middle East countries. This diploma also makes you a self employed person. You will hardly see an unemployed DAE diploma holder in Pakistan. Try to get some internship during your studies or work without stipend in your technology for getting some practical experience.
