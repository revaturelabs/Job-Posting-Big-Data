We help institutions, intermediaries and individuals around the world invest money to meet their goals, fulfil their ambitions, and prepare for the future.We have around 5,000 people on six continents. And we've been around for over 200 years, but keep adapting as society and technology changes. What doesn't change is our commitment to helping our clients, and society, prosper.The baseWe moved into our new HQ in the City of London in 2018. including MQ.• Proven and demonstrated skills in solving big scary problems in the digital world• Understands the drivers and motivators behind decision making and the need to influence in driving the right outcomes for the business• Great communicator both written and verbal including highly polished presentation skills to technical and executive audiences• Have a steady, calming, logical and methodical approach to solving difficult challenges• Highly analytical and able to focus on many different aspects in parallel whilst building a larger conceptual framework/worldview• Pragmatic and
