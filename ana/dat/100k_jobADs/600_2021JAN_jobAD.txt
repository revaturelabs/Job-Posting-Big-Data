We are seeking qualified candidates to operate production equipment for a growing automotive supplier in Mount Sterling. This company is globally recognized as a leader in the manufacturing of HVAC and HEX units for automobiles.Duties and Responsibilities:· Fabrication· Assembly· Final Finish & Inspection· PackagingRequirements:· Previous experience in manufacturing facility a plus, but not required· Dependable with a good attendance record· Work for extended periods while standing· Occasionally lift/move up to 25 poundsBenefits:· 4- or 5-day work week, depending on shift and department· INCREASED SHIFT DIFFERENTIAL FOR 2ND SHIFT· Affordable health, life, dental and vision insurance available· Weekly Pay· Direct deposit· 401(k)· On-Site Fitness Center & Gym· Temp to hire with great benefits when converted.
