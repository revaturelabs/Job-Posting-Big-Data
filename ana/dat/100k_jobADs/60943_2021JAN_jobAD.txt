Where these vacancies are present in various departments such as project manager (co-ordination, scientific research planning & management, scampi project, tilapia project, Dtsp Bmc, assistant project manager (TT/extension), assistant project manager (pathology, genetics), technician (genetics lab, pathology lab, various projects, technology transfer & training), accountant and project manager (works) etc. so in order to employ all these vacancies with eligible and appropriate candidates Rajiv Gandhi Center for Aquaculture has given the employment notification and welcoming applications from all interested and eligible candidates. Therefore aspirants those who are willing to work in this organization and those who have enough educational qualification such as applicants must have passed Diploma/B.E/B.Tech/Graduation Degree/MFSC./M.Sc, M.Sc/M.Tech or its equivalent qualification from any recognized university can apply through offline. Such as applicants have to attend for the personal interview which is going to held from 6th January 2016 to 9th January 2016.
