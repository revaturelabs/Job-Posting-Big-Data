It is one of the ingredients that really lives up to the hype - it is truly an excellent all-round ingredient!Jojoba Oil is referred to as an oil, but it is really a liquid plant wax coming from the Jojoba plant (simmondsia chinensis). It has been around an used for hundreds of years in folk medicine. Native Americans use Jojoba oil for treating skin sores and bruises.Today Jojoba Oil has a wide variety of uses - both for skincare and haircare. It is commonly used to treat dry skin, chapped skin, sunburn, and acne. It also helps to moisturize, soothe skin, and unclog pores.Anti-Aging Properties of Jojoba OilJojoba Oil helps to hydrate and seal in moisture within the skin. As we age, our skin gradually loses moisture - Jojoba Oil helps to combat moisture loss and create a shield to keep moisture in and irritants out.An important consideration - Jojoba Oil will not make skin more oily, in fact it helps reduce oiliness! Again Jojoba Oil is actually a liquid plant wax, and it helps to regulate sebum production. This in turn also helps reduce acne production.Jojoba Oil helps to smooth out wrinkles, soothe fine lines, wrinkles and naturally slow down other signs of aging. It is truly one of the most all-round effective ingredients, which is why it is found in so many great products!Skin TypesJojoba Oil is appropriate for all skin types.
