Bradley & Myles WarrenTAPA/News/Industry Listing/Job Posting/Job Posting: Call for Technical Director – Future Theatre Festival, Seven Siblings Theatre	Job Posting: Call for Technical Director – Future Theatre Festival, Seven Siblings TheatreSeptember 26 2017 CategoryIndustry Listing, Job Posting.The Future Theatre Festival is a two week long curated selection of new work developed with Seven Siblings Theatre.All performances are within the mandate of Fantastic Realism and are written by dynamic and imaginative Canadian playwrights.Responsibilities:• Coordinating a 2-hour initial lighting and sound setup at our venue• Recording and adjusting lighting cues for each show during tech slots• Ensuring that show tech slots move smoothly and effectively• Assisting each stage manager with Sound Qs as necessary• Running lights and acting as technician for all three shows and special events at The Future Theatre Festival• Coordinating show storage and maintaining a professional performance space• Assisting with the space flip between performances• Facilitating a smooth strike and setup for our closing night eventRequirements:• Graduation from a relevant post-secondary program or extensive practical experience in performance design and tech• Must be able to efficiently light an immersive performance space• Experience monitoring the condition of equipment including lighting, sound, and rigging equipment• Experience with independent theatreThis non-union contract runs from Oct 14th to 29th.To be considered, please submit a headshot and resume to sevensiblingstheatreco@gmail.com. The deadline for applications is October 6th at 5pm. Early submissions are encouraged!In exchange for their time, the Future Theatre Festival Technical Director shall receive a $500 honorarium.Posted by: Seven Siblings TheatreFor more information: http://www.sevensiblingstheatre.ca/ftftickets/Comments are closed.Subscribe to the Industry Listings* indicates requiredEmail Address *First NameLast NameAdd your listingDo you have something to post to the TAPA Industry Listing page?
