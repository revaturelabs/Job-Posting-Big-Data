Meet Healthcare Careers Ireland at Jobs Expo DublinHealthcare jobs? Meet Healthcare Careers Ireland at Jobs Expo Dublin	April 7, 2017 /0 Comments/in Jobs Expo Dublin, News /by JobsExpoLooking for jobs in the healthcare sector? Don’t miss your chance to meet Healthcare Careers Ireland at Jobs Expo Dublin on Saturday 22nd April at Croke Park.About Healthcare Careers IrelandHealthcare Careers Ireland is a live recruitment fair connecting skilled healthcare professionals with companies and recruitment firms. Their live, online events bring attendees together with employers, HR and personnel professionals from top organisations across Ireland and around the world. As a result, healthcare professionals can explore an array of career opportunities.How their live recruitment events workHealthcare Careers Ireland uses the latest in interactive chat technology. Attendees register for the event and upload their CVs. Candidates can share their backgrounds as well as their work experience. They can also direct questions towards the companies that interest them. In addition, they can make online appointments to chat to employers during the event. All candidates need is an internet connection and a browser.All Healthcare Careers Ireland’s live recruitment events are free for all attendees seeking new career opportunities. These allow jobseekers to connect with local and international organisations without needing to travel. This is particularly beneficial to jobseekers outside major urban areas, candidates looking to relocate, or anyone too busy to attend a traditional career event. Therefore, Healthcare Careers Ireland offers an easy and effective way for healthcare professional to interact with potential employers.About Careers UnlimitedHealthcare Careers Ireland has grown out of the successful Jobs Expo, Ireland’s leading employment and recruitment fair, and is run by Careers Unlimited. Careers Unlimited is an Irish company which specialises in recruitment and career enhancement. With their many years of experience connecting companies and candidates, they wanted to create a smart, efficient and innovative careers event.
