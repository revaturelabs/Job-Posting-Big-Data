"Jobs - APA-KY JobsHomeEventsNewsKY PlannerJobs/RFPsResourcesAboutLeadership >Executive CommitteeRegional RepresentativesCommitteesMembershipSponsorsContactLinkedInTwitterFacebookAdministrator for the Office of Planning & Zoning - Bullitt County8/10/2016Bullitt County Fiscal Court is accepting applications for an Administrator for the Office of Planning and Zoning. Duties include administration and enforcement of all zoning regulations of Bullitt County and statues as required by KRS Chapter 100, assist customers with zoning information, attend zoning hearings, review permits and site plans. The Administrator will also serve as Flood Plain Coordinator and be in charge of GIS Mapping. Applicants must have experience in Planning & Zoning or related field, GIS experience and possess a valid driver’s license with a clean driving record. A background check will be performed. Applications can be obtained from the Bullitt County Judge Executive’s Office, 300 S. Buckman Street, Shepherdsville, Monday thru Friday between the hours of 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Deadline to submit an application is 4:00 p.m. on Friday, August 19, 2016.
