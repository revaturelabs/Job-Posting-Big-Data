Our team has a deep appreciation for quality design, and we understand the work required to get there. We value creativity, sharing ideas openly, and having fun while we work. Most of all, we’re motivated by the desire to create product experiences people and teams love.You will play a critical role in the design and development of the Teams apps. You will work across web, desktop, and mobile. Design Producers are the glue that keeps our design studio running smoothly. You will participate in the end-to-end design process, scheduling and driving work from ideation and experimentation through to interaction design, visual design, copy writing, motion design, executive review, hand off to the engineering team, and quality control during implementation. In short, you will make sure the right designers are working on the right stuff, at the right time, at the right fidelity, and with the right visibility.**Responsibilities****Key Responsibilities:**+ Maximize the Design team’s impact. Manage design backlogs and sprints in a highly visible way, to give stakeholders confidence that their needs are being met, and to help Designers know what to focus on.+ Own the inward, outward, and upward communications of the Design team. Craft communications and meetings appropriately based on the audience, from Business stakeholders, to Engineering, to Product and Company Leadership. Coordinate design work across multiple locations and time zones.+ Continuously improve the design process. Optimize project management practices, design workflow tools, and communication methods to increase the quality of the Design team’s work.+ Advocate for the Design team’s point of view when working with partners.
