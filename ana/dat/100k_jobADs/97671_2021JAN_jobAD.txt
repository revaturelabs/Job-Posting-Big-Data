Our program provides a rigorous program with high support, using classroom time for discussion of original documents and historiography. We are committed to a global approach at every level, believing that to prepare students for a 21st century world, there needs to be an appreciation of societies across place and time.ResponsibilitiesThe teacher will report to the Interim History & Social Sciences Department Head and the Head of the Upper School, and join a highly collaborative, innovative, and experienced faculty.
