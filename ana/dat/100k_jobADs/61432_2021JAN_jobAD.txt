Hopper Fillers are vital in ensuring the correct production of tea, while maintaining its integrity. Hopper Fillers main objective is to ensure the continual flow of production by ensuring the \"hoppers\" are always filled with tea on each machine in production.Position Responsibilities:Check blend Item# and Lot# against the work order and previous blend in the bin.Place stickers on the blue bins.Refill all the supplies on L#1, 2 & 3 and check for blend every 30 minutes. L#4 & 5 are to be checked every 1 hour. And Zone B is to be checked every 15 minutes.Inform Line Supply personnel of material shortages on the lines.Responsible to maintain GMP of all lines.Responsible for cleaning the top of the machines during changeover.Must empty the blue bins during changeover.Responsible to take out filter from the machines.Ensure the In/out supplies during the changeover.Responsible to empty all the garbage/Tin containers.Responsible to manage breaks before/after changeover.Responsible for general housekeeping throughout and at the end of the shift.Must adhere to ALL safety rules, safe working practices/procedures and Good Manufacturing Practices.Report ALL injuries and illness to your Supervisor immediately.Performs ALL other duties as assigned by your Supervisor.Knowledge and Skills:Required to lift up to 50 kg bags of product with the assistance of push cart.Must be able to read, write and comprehend English.Maintain good attendance per Company's Attendance Policy.Self-starter with strong organizational skills, problem solving skills and troubleshooting skills.Must be able to take a leadership role and communicate/cooperate with others within a team environment.Ability to work independently and as part of a team.High level of integrity and work ethic.Working Conditions:Standard warehouse environment in food industry.Able to meet the physical demands of the position including, but not limited to, standing and/or sitting, some bending, twisting, repetitive movements with fingers, wrists and shoulder joints and reaching.Required uniform includes hairnet and/or beard net, Steel-toed footwear, and smockGood personal hygiene is expectedJewelry is not permitted.Disability AssistanceTreeHouse Foods is an Equal Employment Opportunity Employer and offers opportunities to all job seekers, including those with disabilities.
