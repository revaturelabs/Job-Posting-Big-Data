Wittenberg Veterinary Clinic, Companion Animal in Wittenberg, WI, is seeking self-motivated dedicated, enthusiastic professional to join their team as a part time or full time Associate Veterinarian.Wittenberg, Wisconsin has a hometown feel with the proximity to larger metropolitan areas. Wittenberg is a unique smaller town with an artistic flair. The local community, including our clinic, has participated in the \"Walls of Wittenberg\" with artists painting scenes: modern and historical, tranquil and exciting on the walls of local businesses and schools. There are local community days, festivals, concerts and several nearby casinos providing something for everyone!We are an expanding small animal practice with 2 doctors that specializes in providing high quality, compassionate and convenient care for our patients. We recognize the importance of the relationships between humans and animals and we see our clients and patients as extended family. We offer a complete array of services including medicine, surgery, dentistry, x-rays, and ultrasound.The purpose of this position is to maintain animal health, diagnose, and treat diseases and injuries. The Associate Veterinarian is to make every effort to help improve the quality of care given to the patients and uses initiative in carrying out assignments independently, but refers deviations, problems, and unfamiliar situations to the associate doctors for decision or help. Candidates of all experience levels considered. Additional opportunities may be available at our location in the Wausau area, Metro Animal Hospital.
