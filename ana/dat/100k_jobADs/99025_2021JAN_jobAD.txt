You will now get jobs alerts.Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) was established on 27th October 1986. IWAI undertakes projects for development and maintenance of Inland Waterway Terminal infrastructure on National Waterways. Inland Waterways Authority of India is the statutory authority in charge of the waterways in India. IWAI has its headquarters in Noida, UP. The authority builds the necessary infrastructure in waterways (rivers, canals, backwaters and creeks), surveys the economic feasibility of new projects and also looks after administration and regulation.
