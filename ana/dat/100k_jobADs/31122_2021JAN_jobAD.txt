Analyst for Customer Quality IntelligenceworkFastansættelseDo you have solid experience with mechanical engineering, and would you like to work in cross-cultural teams to develop great designs for state-of-the-art audio technologies?Join our Mechanics DepartmentAt GN, we make life sound better, and through our Jabra brand, we are leading major technology shifts with intelligent audio solutions. Joining our Mechanics Department in Ballerup, you will become part of a global team consisting of 40 colleagues who collaborate with colleagues across the organization to create new products.Translate user requirements into mechanical designAs Senior Mechanical Design Engineer you will make new mechanical product concepts. You will be involved in the process from idea generation to product development. Doing so, you will work closely with R&D partners, industrial designers and product management and ensure that the concepts can be manufactured and fulfill all the requirements and targets. You will be responsible for activities such as mechanical stack-up, component design, risk assessment, design reviews and prototyping/testing. You will also be part of technology studies securing continuous product innovation.Collaboration is keyThroughout the design process, you work with a wide range of stakeholders, including internal customers and colleagues, suppliers and manufacturing partners. All of them will have their requirements on the mechanical concept.“The challenging part will be to reach the right compromise between design details, cutting-edge technology and user-friendly products, all within agreed deadlines,” finishes Olof Simonsson, Senior Manager, Mechanical Engineering.Experienced engineer who enjoys cross-cultural team workYou hold a B.Sc. or a M.Sc. in mechanical or design engineeringIdeally you have 7+ years of experience within design of plastic parts for medical or electronic consumer devicesYou have high experience working in cross functional teams including ID/HW/AudioYou are a highly experienced 3D CAD user, we use PTC CreoYou have experience in Design for Manufacturability (DFM) and Design for Assembly (DFA)You can present and communicate ideas and concepts both verbally and visually, e.g. by free hand sketchingYou speak and write English fluentlyYou are ready to travel 15-20 days per year to collaborate with colleagues and business partners primarily in ChinaAs a person, you are open minded and ambitious with strong a mindset to take on ownership and responsibility. You master the balance between abstract high-level focus, high attention to details and explorative processes. You have the ability of seeing a problem from more than one side and you are not afraid of killing your darlings. Moreover, you enjoy working with people from different cultures, sharing findings at all levels and establishing common ground.Would you like to know more?To apply, use the ‘Ansøg’ link no later than 23. February 2020.
