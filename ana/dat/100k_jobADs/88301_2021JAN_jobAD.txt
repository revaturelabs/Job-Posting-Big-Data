stock and replenish supplies and equipment as required.Participate in own professional development by maintaining required skills validation and attending educational offerings.Support the development of other staff and formal learners.Perform sterile dressing change for wounds over 48 hours old, IV fluid assistance activities, oral/nasogastric feeding and urinary catherizations/irrigation.What you will need:Current Nurse Aide I Registry listing with the N.C.
