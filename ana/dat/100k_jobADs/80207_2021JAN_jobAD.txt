2018	Want to be part of a company that promotes a work-life balance and help families raise healthy kids?Lunchtime Solutions, Inc., a leader in the food service management industry, is seeking a talented “Food Service Director” to become a key contributor and manager of the Hastings Public School food service operations team in Hastings, Nebraska.Come join our team and the opportunity to make a difference! We offer a schedule with no nights, no weekends, and no holidays. We also offer competitive compensation, an retirement savings 401(k) plan, paid holidays, paid time off, and SIGN ON BONUS!Our mission is to provide healthy school breakfast and lunches to students throughout the Midwest. We’re not your typical school breakfast and lunch program. Our focus is to provide healthy meal options at the school districts. We take pride in what we do, have passion for the industry, and focus on customer service.The Food Service Director works closely with school administration to provide the best lunches and best customer service in the industry. Your responsibilities include hiring your own rock star staff, being a cheerleader for their development and success, and representing our Company in the district.Knowledge of the school food service industry is extremely helpful, but not required. A bachelor’s degree and/or 2 years of relevant school nutrition program experience is preferred. Qualified candidate will require computer skills. You must be able to effectively communicate with your team and lead them to success.Qualified applicants should apply online at: www.LunchtimeSolutions.com/careers or email their resumes at hr@lunchtimesolutions.comLunchtime Solutions Inc. is an Equal Opportunity Employer and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, age, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, sex, marital status, disability, or status as a U.S. Veteran.
