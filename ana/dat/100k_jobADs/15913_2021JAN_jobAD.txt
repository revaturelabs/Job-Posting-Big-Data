We are seeking a specialist in Roman history, archaeology, or culture with the ability to teach Latin.PhD is required by July 1, 2014. Applicants should send, by e-mail, only a letter of application and a curriculum vitae to Ms. Elizabeth Reyna (ereyna AT tulane.edu). The file name of all e-mail attachments should begin with the candidate’s surname.The search committee will commence its initial screening of applications on April 7, 2014. We will interview select candidates by Skype. However, the advertising and search process will remain active until the position is filled.Please direct any inquiries to Prof. S. Lusnia (slusnia AT tulane.edu), Department of Classical Studies, Tulane University, New Orleans, LA 70118-5698; phone: (504) 865-5719; website: http://ift.tt/1h1VRER.
