In fact, we’re not a clinic at all. IndeVets is a community of veterinarians working together to make vet med better.WhY We'Re DiFFeRenT?We partner with animal hospitals in need of staffing help. We are big on community.You choose your own hours and where you want to practice—small private practices, large corporate groups, shelters, ERs — and customize your schedule to suit your life. Work when you want, take off when you want. Stay at one place a while, or diversify. The choice is always up to you.Our team is veterinarian led and here to support you at every step in your journey.
