CommentShareTweetWhatsappPinterestLinkedinTelegram0 Flares Filament.io 0 Flares ×CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 notification has been issued by CRIJAI : Central Research Institute for Jute and Allied Fibers which is a elementary body of ICAR – Indian Council of Agriculture Research invites applications from eligible and qualified candidates for filling up various posts of Technical Assistant, Field/ Farm Technician and Lower Division Clerk.Recruitment details of CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 :Technical Assistant : 01 postField/ Farm Technician : 06 postsLower Division Clerk : 06 postsTotal number of fresh vacancies launched in CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 : 13 postsMinimum Eligibility Standards to apply for CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 :For Technical Assistant Post : Candidate should have completed his/her bachelor’s degree in agriculture from a recognized university.For Field/ Farm Technician Post : Candidate should be passed matriculation with at least one year certificate in Agriculture from a reputed institution.For Lower Division Clerk post : Candidate must be passed in 10+2 examination from a recognized board and should have typing speed of minimum 35 wpm in English or 30 wpm in Hindi.Age limit mandatory to apply for CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 :For Technical Assistant and Farm/Field Technician : 18-30 years.For Lower division clerk : 18-27 years.Dates to remember to apply for CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 :Candidates have to send their dully filled applications to the address given in the notice within 30 days of the issue of notification.Notification Issue date : 08 December 201445 days for candidates belonging to far flung areas.Application Fee payment necessary to apply for CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 :Rupees 500/- only from candidates of General Category through Demand Draft which must be drawn in favour of – ICAR Unit – CRIJAF Payable at Kolkata.Candidates of SC/ST/PH category are exempted from payment of the application fee.Complete Procedure to apply for CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 :Candidates have to visit at official website of CRIJAF and download official notice of the recruitment.Print application form given in the notice and fill up with care.Affix recent two passport size photos on application form and sign then attach self attested copies of all certificates required as per eligibility.Keep in envelope and must write : APPLICATION FOR THE POST OF …………. on envelope and Advertisement number as well and send to the given address.Address to send application form of CRIJAF Lower Division Clerk and Assistant Recruitment 2015 :ICAR – Central Research Institute for Jute and Allied Fibers, Barrackpore, Kolkata – 700 120, West Bengal.Click here to visit at official website : http://www.crijaf.org.in/SideLinks/QuickLinks/Recruitment.htmlClick here to download official notice and application form : Notification-CRIJAF-JobsFiled Under: Government Jobs
