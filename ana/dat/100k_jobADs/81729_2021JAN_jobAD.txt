Where these vacancies are present in various departments such as economics, commerce, English, Hindi, mathematics and environmental studies. To fill up all these vacancies with eligible and suitable candidates Satyawati College has issued the employment notification and welcoming applications from all interested and eligible candidates. Thus applicants those who have enough eligibility criteria and sufficient educational qualification such as applicants must have passed Master’s degree level in a relevant subject from any recognized university and candidates must have cleared the National Eligibility Test (NET) which was conducted by the UGC/ CSIR or a similar test accredited by the UGC can apply through offline. Such as applicants have to download the application form in a prescribed format and fill it with necessary details and send the filled application from to the below mentioned address on or before the last date of 9th January 2016. Other important details like age limit, educational qualification, selection process, application fee, application process and important dates are given below.Satyawati College Recruitment-2016
