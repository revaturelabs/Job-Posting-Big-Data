The IT/GIS Department manages the City’s technology hardware and software infrastructure, enabling departments to deliver timely, efficient services to residents and customers. This department provides IT services and support to more than 400 users in City departments and affiliated organizations, including Spectra Venue Management, Okanagan Hockey Group, Penticton Vees, Interior Health Authority, RCMP and Tourism Penticton. These users are spread across 16 buildings and connected through a City-owned fibre optic network.Reporting to the Business Services Supervisor, the GIS Analyst II – Developer will be part of a highly motivated team of GIS Analysts with a passion that extends beyond the job. Our purpose is to provide the systems and services necessary for our City departments to do their work efficiently, with access to all the information available, so that they can make the best decisions possible and provide quality customer service to our community. We are looking for a highly skilled person to join our team and take over complete management and maintenance of all our GIS infrastructure and enterprise systems. A person experienced in scripting and application development, who can support and build new tools and widgets. A person who also takes initiative to go above and beyond, is customer service oriented, and is passionate about bringing solutions to the table.Key Responsibilities:Ensure enterprise systems are
