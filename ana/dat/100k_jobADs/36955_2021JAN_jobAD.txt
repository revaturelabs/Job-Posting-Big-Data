Our associates make the world more productive by improving the efficiency and reliability of the machinery that keeps industry in motion.Position Summary: This purpose of this position is to create a detailed daily production schedule by balancing the orders to meet customer delivery dates in the most efficient manner which includes planning for the correct materials, in the required quantities, on the required day, at the required locations. Will work to ensure the work centers are utilized in most efficient manner. This position must provide continuous improvements to the role and related processes.The planner schedules planned orders and releases Production orders to operations. The planner works with shop floor leadership to ensure that delivery dates are met and on contingency plans as required to address any potential deviations to meeting the daily/weekly production schedule. In order to release work orders, the planner must release purchase orders to suppliers following the guidelines set by supply management. Similarly, the planner will request delivery from suppliers to meet the customer due date. If the internal or external customer delivery date is at risk, the planner will notify those affected and work with operations leadership, supply management, and / or engineer to resolve the situation.Planners will work together across product lines and component processes to resolve conflicts and constraints. Assist operations team to ensure accurate inventory including a leadership role in the physical inventory or cycle count process. The planner will participate in continuous improvement activities to reduce lead time, improve material flow, and improve cost. The planner will analyze data and make recommendations for Total Replenishment Lead Times, safety stock levels, minimum order quantities, maximum order quantities, etc.Essential Job Duties:Reviews ERP system messages and takes action as appropriate.Aligns work orders with the production schedule.Participates in the schedule attainment process.Performs capacity evaluations and leveling within guidelines set by the manager.Supports operations team as an expert on inventory transactions.Acts as a leader when supporting the physical inventory process.Supports lean initiatives through development of the Kanban process, visual shop floor controls, and lean manufacturing principles.Participates in the quoting process by offering projected lead times for make to order products and ensuring adherence to lead times for make to stock items.
