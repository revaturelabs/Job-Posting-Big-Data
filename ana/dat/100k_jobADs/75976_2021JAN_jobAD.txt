For more information, go to www.goodyear.com/corporate.Program DescriptionThe company, headquartered in Akron, Ohio, is looking for talented leaders to significantly strengthen its global leadership depth and play an integral role in the current and future success of the organization. Students entering an internship with the Enterprise Competency Center of the Project Management Office (PMO) will be provided with a meaningful and challenging twelve- to eighteen-month assignment (on a part-time or full-time basis) at our corporate headquarters in Akron, Ohio. As a PMO intern, you may be responsible for:Assisting with the development of new training material for onsite and eLearning modulesKnowledge Management: Analyze and organize Project data to leverage lessons learned for future projectsAssisting with the administration and maintenance of associate training historyMaking updates to the PMO SharePoint siteAssisting Project managers with ongoing workshops and trainingIn addition to challenging, engaging, and interesting work assignments, interns have the opportunity to meet and interact with other interns, including interns in other functional areas and from different schools. Educational and social events are coordinated for during and after work hours to help interns learn more about Goodyear. Several Goodyear leaders present various topics to help interns begin to think about significant business issues and their career options.
