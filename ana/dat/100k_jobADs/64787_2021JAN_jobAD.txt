The OCM Manager's mission is to create converging path that bring together change management and project management to achieve the business objectives of the change.The OCM Manager ensures projects (change initiatives) for Unisys new & existing customers' meet objectives on time and on budget by increasing employee adoption, reducing resistance and risk.. This person will focus on the people side of change, including changes to business processes, IT systems and technology, job roles and organization structures. The OCM Manager will create and implement change management strategies and plans that include: Engagement & Adoption Plans, Leader Action Plans, Communications Plans, Training Plans, Sustainment Plans that are informed by Stakeholder Impact Assessments, Training and Communication Needs Assessments, and Readiness Assessments. The OCM manager will work to drive faster adoption, higher ultimate utilization of and proficiency with the changes that impact employees.
