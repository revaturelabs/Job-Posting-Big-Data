And we are looking for an MBA intern to help us grow.In this role you will provide financial oversight, analytical insights, business planning and forecasting support for teams across Waymo. We believe learning is a two-way street: leveraging your knowledge while providing you with opportunities to expand your skill-set. Interns are an important part of our culture and our recruiting pipeline. Join us at Waymo for a fun and rewarding internship!In this internship, you’ll:Conduct deal modeling and analysis, including consideration of strategic alternatives, contract review and revision of key financial termsModel P&L and cash flows for various company-wide business linesMaintain the company’s budgeting and forecasting tools and processes for various engineering, operational, and other business teamsEngage with business partners to provide strong financial oversight for business decision support including items such as deal review, PO approvals and TVC requestsWork with cost center owners to develop and maintain rolling monthly and annual forecasts and understand sources of variances and uncertaintyPrepare executive presentations for budget, forecast and business analysisAt a minimum, we’d like you to have:Stellar business acumen with strong financial modeling skills and familiarity with financial statementsExcellent professional written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills required.
