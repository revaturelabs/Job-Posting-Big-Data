Brands use both printed and digital designs to market their products. A Graphic designer is assigned with the work task of creating visual concepts, by hand or by using the software, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform or gratifying consumers. They are also responsible for developing overall layout and production designs for advertisements, magazines, brochures etc. Moreover, there are other duties which should be performed by a designer like they are required to present the design to clients or the art director, update the changes recommended by the clients into the final design, determine the message the design should portray etc.How many types of graphic designs are there?Graphic design is the process of visual communication and problem solving through the use of typography, imagery, color etc. There are eight types of graphic designs which are visual identify graphic design, marketing & advertising graphic design, user interface graphic design, publication graphic design, packaging graphic design, motion graphic design, environmental graphic design, art and illustration for graphic design.What education qualification is required in Graphic designer job?To become a graphic designer, a candidate needs to have IT and drawing skills, excellent communication skills etc. Moreover, a bachelor's degree in graphic design or other art and design-based subject.
