Northwest Arkansas references a unique group of municipalities unlike anywhere else in the country with a trade area of just over 500,000 people and the 22nd fastest metro area in the US. The area is home to 3 Fortune 500 companies: Tyson Foods, Wal-Mart and JB Hunt and SEC member, the University of Arkansas. For more metro minded amenities, the area has numerous shopping districts including the Pinnacle Hills Promenade and Northwest Arkansas malls.
