In addition, the successful candidate will be required to provide a weekly research summary of the sectors covered and high conviction stock picks.A highly collaborative Team player, he/she will also work closely with other Equities Research Analysts based in Singapore and the rest of Asia ex-Japan in regional collaboration projects to share investment insights. He/she should also be enthusiastic in mentoring junior colleagues.Clear presentation and active participation in Group-wide discussions via conference calls, emails, calls & chats on a daily basis are essential.Job RequirementsThe ideal candidate must have a passion in equities research and investing and possess strong fundamental research, financial modelling, forecasting and valuation skills. He/she should also be an expert in using Bloomberg, Microsoft Excel and Word. He/she should be exceptionally meticulous and recognize the importance of fact-checking and doing reality checks.The candidate must also be a highly pro-active, conscientious, responsible and disciplined team player who can complete all assigned tasks impeccably within tight deadlines. He/she should also be mature enough to work independently and propose effective solutions to address potential challenges.Strong inter-personal skills with the ability to coordinate and collaborate with colleagues from different countries, cultures and level of work experience are essential.Excellent writing and communication skills.At least 7 years of Equities Research work experience covering the Asia ex-Japan and ASEAN listed equities is required. Prior work experience in major buy-side asset management firms or sell-side equities institutions would be ideal.
