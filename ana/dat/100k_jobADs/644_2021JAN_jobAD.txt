We want to have more skilled colleagues in our R&D unit and we want to strengthen the team with an agile leader! Therefore, we are looking for you who love to coach, facilitate, lead development teams, drive change and think that the consulting role is exciting and developing.Apply today if this sounds like something for you!What is the role like?In the role as an agile leader, you will drive agile methods and support development teams to reach their full potential in customer projects. With your technical expertise in system development, you are a bridge between developers and product owners. If you are passionate about system development, you can combine the role of agile leader with working as a developer in a team. Our customers are in all different types of product development companies and we work with everything from the latest telecom technology to connected and autonomous vehicles. With us, you get the opportunity to develop and learn new things all the time. You set the bar yourself for how high you want to reach.Your backgroundYou have a technical background but now want to focus on create effective teams and change the way they work.We also see that you:Has documented experience of agile methods such as Scrum, Kanban or SAFeHas worked as a developer for a few yearsGreat interest in leading people and team developmentInterest in keeping up to date on new trends, ways of thinking and methods in the fieldYou speak and write Swedish and English fluentlyWe believe that you have a strong drive, are curious, take initiative and have a will to develop the way you work. You are also humble in your own competence and you care about your colleagues, at the same time as you are responsible and make the most of every opportunity.How is Sigma as an employer?We are proud of our strong corporate culture, so your personal qualities are very important to us. We have both business sense, customer orientation and technology interest in the blood, and of course an ability to quickly take in new information and transform it into creative solutions.We respect everyone and are happy to take part in the experiences of others while sharing our own. We believe that diversity promotes creativity and helps us grow both as individuals and companies.Sigma IT Consulting is the largest company in the Sigma Group and we work according to the words Passion and Execution. For us, this means that we love what we do and we get it done. We also want to be part of creating a better tomorrow for all of us. Together we can do this.Our vision permeates everything we do: EXPECT A BETTER TOMORROWApplicationPlease send in your application below. We cannot handle applications that are sent through email. We act now and drive the digital revolution forward. We see the possibilities that lie in challenging the status quo and together with our clients creating the technology of the future. It is about cutting-egde technology combined with the ability to dream. To work together with people that create a wow! That is how we make a difference.Sigma is a value-driven company with high goals where decisiveness and engagement are key influences. You are close to those who make the decisions and you are able to be involved in these. When you work with us at you will have a employer that has a strong position in Sweden but also internationally and where you have big opportunities for further development.Sigma IT consists of more than 1500 specialists within strategy, technology and communication. We are present in 8 countries and at over 20 cities in Sweden. The head office is located in Gothenburg. We are a part of the Sigma Group, that is Sweden's largest privately owned Tech Consultancy company with 5000 employees in 14 countries.
