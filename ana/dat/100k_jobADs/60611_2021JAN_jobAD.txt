Bradley & Myles WarrenTAPA/News/Industry Listing/Job Posting/Job Posting: ARTISTIC INTERNSHIPS, THEATRE GARGANTUA	Job Posting: ARTISTIC INTERNSHIPS, THEATRE GARGANTUAMay 15 2017 CategoryIndustry Listing, Job Posting.Theatre Gargantua is seeking TWO emerging artists who are serious and committed to pursuing a career in the performing arts.Our paid mentorship program is designed to provide an opportunity for emerging artists to expand their skills and experience through observing and participating in Theatre Gargantua’s creation cycle. Specifically, participants will attend all rehearsal and creative meetings while simultaneously assisting in areas of production (e.g. set, lighting, costumes, stage management, and director assistance). Through this program, interns will develop new skills that will be useful in future artistic endeavours.The program will begin in June and continue through performances of the premiere of latest cycle of work at Theatre Passe Muraille this fall. This position is full time during rehearsals and production (approximately ½ week in June and 8 weeks in the fall) with some commitments in between. There is also the opportunity to assist with our SideStream Cycle which will premiere at SummerWorks Festival.Successful applicants will have a background in theatre and an interest in multi-disciplinary work and/or the collaborative creation process. Applicants should be prepared to make a commitment to the full length of the project, including attendance at all rehearsals and performances and for all creative meetings.To Apply:Please send a resume and cover letter detailing your interest in the program and the company as it relates to your future career aspirations.Please send application to jacquie@theatregargantua.ca citing “Artistic Internship” in the subject line (no phone calls please.)The deadline to submit applications is Friday May 26nd at 5pm. Interviews will be held the first week of June.Theatre Gargantua is an equal opportunity employer who encourages applications from candidates with disabilities, Aboriginal candidates, and candidates who are members of a visible minority.Theatre Gargantua’s mentorship program is generously sponsored by TD Bank Financial GroupPosted by: Theatre GargantuaFor more information: http://www.theatregargantua.ca/join-us/emerging-artists/artistic-internships/Comments are closed.Subscribe to the Industry Listings* indicates requiredEmail Address *First NameLast NameAdd your listingDo you have something to post to the TAPA Industry Listing page?
