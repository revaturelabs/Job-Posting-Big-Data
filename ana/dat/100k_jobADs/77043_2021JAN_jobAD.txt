ASTA exists to support string and orchestra teachers and players. Our members range from budding student teachers to artist-status performers. We are a trusted source for teaching resources, professional education, eLearning, local and national events, a career center, insurance programs, and access to a vibrant and collaborative strings community. The ASTA 6 person team is a small, fast paced, highly collaborative environment, seeking someone who shares our values: Teaching & Learning. We are lifelong learners and share our knowledge openly. Diversity & Inclusion. We value every person for the differences of identity, ideas, and interests they bring, and we actively work to engage with those differences. Community. We harness the power of collaboration between people. Integrity. We will be honest and aligned with our values - always. Passion. We approach our work with focus and intentionality of commitment. Excellence. We will always strive to do our very best work in service of our community. SCOPE OF ROLE Come join the team. ASTA is seeking a Manager, Membership and Component Relations to join the membership team to actively drive impact across our community of over 7500 string educators, collegiate students, and performers. This is an exciting time and the Manager will join an energetic and passionate team as we to create solutions, community and resources that meet the needs of string educators. Reporting to the Director, Meetings & Membership, the manager will administer member service programs including implementing new services focused but not exclusively on membership recruitment, and renewal along with, identifying the content, scope and cost of membership programs. The Manager is responsible for the association management system including data analytics and reporting, online community, and the liaison for the ASTA chapter program. The Manager will work with the Director of Meetings and Membership and Membership Coordinator to build and administer membership, chapters and the online community programs to increase engagement and with the goal of ensuring a positive experience for all individuals and ultimately a growth in membership.
