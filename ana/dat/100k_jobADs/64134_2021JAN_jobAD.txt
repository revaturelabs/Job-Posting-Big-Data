Ltd (APCPL)Mode of Apply OnlineLast Date July 7, 2020APCPL Recruitment 2020 Details:Name of the Post: Engineering Executive TraineesTotal Posts: 25 [UR-17, OBC-6, SC-2]Discipline wise Vacancies:* Electrical - 8* Mechanical - 12* Control & Instrumentation - 5Age Limit: Upper Age limit is 27 years as on last date of online application in APCPL (i.e. 01.06.2020). The upper age limit is relaxable as per Govt. rules.Qualification for APCPL Engineering Executive Trainees 2020:Full time Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering or Technology/ AMIE with not less than 65% marks (55% for SC/ST), as per respective Institute/University norms and have also qualified GATE-2019 are eligible to apply. A candidate with the prescribed degree identified for the disciplines as given below can only apply for the post of ET in the respective discipline.1. Electrical Engineering: Electrical/ Electrical & Electronics/ Electrical, Instrumentation & Control/ Power System & High Voltage/ Power Electronics/ Power Engineering.2. Mechanical Engineering: Mechanical / production/Industrial Engg. / Production & Industrial Engg. / Thermal / Mechanical & Automation / Power engineering.3. Instrumentation Engineering: Electronics & Instrumentation / Instrumentation & Control/ Electronics Instrumentation & Control.Selection Process for APCPL (ET) Recruitment 2020:Eligible candidates who have qualified in Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE)-2019 and Based on the GATE-2019 marks and requirement, candidates will be short listed for further selection process (GD and Interview). The final merit list will be prepared based on 85% weightage of GATE-2019 marks, 5% weightage of GD marks and 10% weightage of Interview marks.Application Fee:Candidate belonging to General/ OBC category is required to pay a non-refundable registration fee of Rs. 150/-. The SC/ST/PwBD category registration fees NIL.Important Dates:* Commencement of Online Registration in APCPL Website: 04.06.2020* Last date for online registration in APCPL Website: 03.07.2020How To Apply:The candidates, who have appeared in GATE 2019, are only eligible to apply and should indicate GATE registration number, the candidates need to apply online for APCPL ET 2020 on the website www.apcpl.co.in from 04.06.2020 to 03.07.2020.Apply Online: Click Here [from 04/06/2020]Detailed Advertisement: Click HereOfficial Website: https://apcpl.co.inTags: All India Govt Jobs, Aravali Power Company Pvt.
