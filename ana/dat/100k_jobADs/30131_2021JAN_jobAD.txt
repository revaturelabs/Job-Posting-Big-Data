Affordability, adventure, friendly people, universal healthcare, low crime, inclusive culture, and beautiful scenery.Find everything you are looking for as a Small Animal Veterinarian in our AAHA-Accredited practice. Lots of people say they’re compassionate but, the results are in and, our experienced team at Grey Bruce Pet Hospital truly is. Only 6% of hospitals in Canada are accredited by AAHA, and our 4 veterinarian, Small Animal hospital is one of them. We celebrate best practices and are not shy to try new things, most recently introducing stem cell therapy and digital thermal imaging to our roster. Our vets work alongside our 7 Registered Veterinary Technicians and, together with our other support team members, we run a busy and successful hospital. We use cloud-based software (Ezy-Vet) and our nearest referral centre is conveniently located within the Ontario Veterinary College. Our building has served us well for many years but we are currently in the planning stages for construction of a new hospital – something to look forward to!Being an AAHA-Accredited practice means we are constantly striving to be better, with clear protocols and progressive culture. With a fear-free mindset and emphasis on client relationships, we are lucky to have a compliant client base that benefits from our extensive scope of services.Our veterinarians work collaboratively and have regular doctor’s meetings to ensure consistency and open communication. In support of your medicine, you will have an in-house lab, digital x-ray, therapeutic laser and ultrasound available. Along with theriogenology, rehabilitation and chiropractic services in-house, one of our vets rounds out our Western medicine with Traditional Chinese medicine and acupuncture for the times when your clients wish to pursue these methodologies.You will be scheduled for 36-40 hours over 4 days as well as working one in four Saturday shifts. There will be a mix of appointments and surgeries. Each of our veterinarians is responsible for one on-call shift every 4-6 weeks as part of a rotating call system shared within our community.Our ideal candidate takes pride in their leadership and communication abilities. Although a minimum of 2 years of experience is preferred, a newer graduate will also thrive due to our supportive culture and experienced Veterinarian mentors in the clinic. If you show up with a can-do attitude and a love of surgery and medicine, we’ll set you up for success. For the right individual, we would absolutely consider the possibility of buy-in. Either way, it’s incredibly important to us that you have a voice and take an active role in the evolution of our hospital, because it will be your hospital too.Ideally you like to be organized and are diligent about record keeping and timeliness. You are team-oriented, kind-hearted and passionate about helping people and their pets. Should you be interested, mentorship in Chinese medicine, acupuncture, Vet Stem, rehab or reproductive services would be available to you. Our Dr. Boyd is marvelous, and a wonderful teacher!Moving from the U.S. to Canada has the appeal of travel and adventure, without the drawbacks of language barriers or disorienting culture shock. The rumors are true, we do say “eh?” and apologize often, but we think that just adds to our charm! Canada is a peaceful country and, Owen Sound is a perfect mix of rural and urban. It is only a 2-hour (scenic) drive from the big city of Toronto (with direct flights to most major American cities) and, the largest urban community in its county (with all of the amenities including the regional hospital). Owen Sound boasts incredible value for money with property and housing being readily available and affordable. It is the type of place that allows you to live on an acreage and still be at work in 10 minutes! Located on the southern shore of Georgian Bay it has an expansive harbor, stunning beaches, waterfalls, ski hills, parks, and world-renowned shipwreck scuba diving. First settled in 1841, the thriving downtown core is steeped in history (for Canada!!) – giving it a truly village ambiance. Canada celebrates diversity, culture, equity, the arts, sports, adventure, and inclusion. You will be free to authentically be yourself and feel welcomed into our community with open arms and hearts.Our clinic values our Veterinarians, and we prove it by supporting your personal, financial, and professional growth through:Continuing Education (CE);Competitive Salary;Extended Health Benefits package (Medical, Dental, & Vision premiums paid by clinic);Professional Services coverage (chiropractor, massage therapist, physiotherapist…);Employee Assistance Program;Paid Licensing & Membership Dues;Professional Liability Insurance coverage;Paid Vacation time;Paid Sick & Personal Days;Generous Veterinary Discounts (75% off services for your own pets, and an extended family and friends discount);Uniform Allowance;Eligibility for a discretionary Performance Bonus;Attractive Signing Bonus for Exceptional Candidates;Relocation Allowance & Assistance;Comprehensive Immigration Assistance provided by experienced immigration lawyer on retainer for out-of-country applicants.If you are looking for a progressive environment where each individual is heard, and recognized for their unique contributions, we'd love to talk.We are an equal opportunity employer and are committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and diverse workplace.
