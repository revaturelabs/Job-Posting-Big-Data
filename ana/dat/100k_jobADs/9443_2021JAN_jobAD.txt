Check out the accomplishments of our Women in IT group! http://bit.ly/MedtronicWomeninITA Day in the Life Principal Master Data Management (MDM) Technologist works in collaboration with the MDM team and stakeholders to implement, maintain, and enhance data models and solutions used to define and measure quality of data domains. The MDM Technologist applies innovative and best practice techniques for modeling, developing, enhancing, and deploying database, and integration solutions into MDM. The MDM Technologist will support existing MDM solutions and play a key role in new data integration, establishing new data domain initiatives that support organizational priorities.The individual will need to work closely with data and solutions architects to develop and build a scalable MDM solution which encompasses multiple domains: customer, consumer, payers, and providers. Individual will also be responsible for guiding partners on best practices, implementing and designing future domains and supporting day-to-day MDM needs/enhancements.Responsibilities may include the following and other duties may be assigned.Play a lead role in designing and building new data domains.Partner with implementation teams to deliver comprehensive MDM solutions and strategiesBring together large, complex and sparse data sets to meet functional and non-functional business requirements.Deliver all functional aspects of an MDM implementation – installation, configuration, and deployment.Analyze and resolve technical and configuration issues.Work closely with integration, DBAs, application owners, and business SME to help design and implement technical solutions.Partner to develop, drive, and execute the long-term data vision and strategy for the MDM platforms by working with multiple teams and stakeholders across the company.Provide detailed documentation, end user training and knowledge-transfer servicesTranslating requirements into actionable project initiatives with associated metricsCommunicate Broadly: Create and proffer both verbal and written briefings to key stakeholders to explain complex problems and situations in their language. Then turn around and communicate to the developers in their lingo for execution.Develops, documents, and configures systems specifications that conform to defined architecture standards, address business requirements, and processes.Involved in planning of system and development deployment as well as responsible for meeting hardware and software compliance and security standards.Evaluates interface between hardware and software, operational requirements and characteristics of overall system.Establishes and documents system design, parameters and formats, ensures hardware and software systems functionality, performance and compatibility, and coordinates and/or modifies system parameters in terms of configurations, such as existing and projected computer capacity and capabilities.Actively identifies system functionality or performance deficiencies, executes changes to existing systems, and tests functionality of the system to correct deficiencies and maintain more effective data handling, data integrity, conversion, input/output requirements, and storage.May document testing and maintenance of system updates, modifications, and configurations.May act as a liaison with key technology vendor technologists or other business functions.Must Have: Minimum RequirementsBachelor’s degree7+ years of experience with a bachelor’s degree or 5+ years of experience with an advanced degree5+ years of MDM experience, at least 2 with Informatica MDMExperience in designing and architecting integrations between Informatica MDM and other systems using standard connectors, publish-subscribe, and other mechanismsImplementation experienceNice to HavePriority consideration will be given to local applicantsHealthcare experience (payer or provider)Advanced Oracle and SQL scripting and analysis expertiseMaster Data Management Developer, Professional CertificationAbout Medtronic Together, we can change healthcare worldwide.
