other activities related to communications.Responsibilities:Manage campaigns: coordinate with the relevant departments to gather the ongoing campaigns’ results and winners’ interviews, verify the results and announce the winners on social media and the website.Manage various email campaigns, including template designs, calls to action and import content used in email blasts.Produce paymentsKeep well informed of competitor marketing initiatives and promotions.Liaise with the relevant sub-teams within the marketing department to report the requirements of the requestors.Arrange regions, help to book venues and coordinate the delivery of merchandise.Provide Quality Assurance of all resources and materials to the Sales and BD teams.Troubleshoot and proactively identify ways to improve procedures and cooperation between Sales & Marketing teams.Report to the marketing operations manager and CMO and assist with any other localized tasks as and when required.Your role will be required to have:Minimum 2 – 3 years of experience in a Marketing position.Excellent knowledge of the English Language both written and oral.Strong organizational, project management and prioritization skills.Ability to work quickly and accurately.Self-motivated with trouble-shooting and problem-solving skills.Attention to detail is critical.Extremely proactive.Always act professionally despite highly pressurized environment.BA or MA in Business, Marketing, Communications, or any relevant field.Highly analytical and able to derive meaning from data through A/B testing and email optimization.Ability to work with tools such as Google Analytics, CRM, email platforms.Will be considered an advantage if you have:Knowledge of HTML and CSS for e-mail template building.FX
