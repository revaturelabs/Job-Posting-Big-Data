Coaches and manages the SQE team ensuring the competences and capabilities are in line with the business needs.* Participates in the Supplier Selection process for the quality aspects, to ensure supplier quality.* Maintains and sets supplier quality aspects for new / improved products ensuring a.o. manufacturability aspects at suppliers.* Contribute to sourcing decisions identifying required supplier capability and quality level.* Identifies gaps between required and current supplier capabilities, and drives quality improvement plan / risk mitigation accordingly.* Creates, releases and continuously drives the improvement of tools, equipment and manufacturing processes and related Quality systems to meet the product specifications.* Identifies cost saving opportunities with the supplier.* Implements design and manufacturing process changes at supplier level.Knowledge and Experience:* A minimum of 3-5 years of direct experience in healthcare compliance and/or Medical Device Manufacturing* BA or BS in engineering discipline required. * Experience working in the Product Development Lifecycle process* Work with critical components/assemblies suppliers* Experience auditing Suppliers to ISO 13485 Standard* Experience driving process improvements with suppliers based on event and/or data driven decisionsSkills and Abilities:* Strong written and verbal communication skills* Excellent knowledge and experience with software programs such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint* Strong organizational, process, and interpersonal skills* Experience with project management in a matrix business organization* The ability to facilitate clarity where ambiguity may exist in complex business situations* Ability to lift 25lbs with or without accommodationsFUJIFILM SonoSite, Inc. offers a fantastic compensation package, including benefits, and a 401k program.
