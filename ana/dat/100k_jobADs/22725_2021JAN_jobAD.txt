This exam is for the posts of Assistant Engineer cadre . The candidates those who are eagerly waiting to take the exam can now download the call letters in online from the website. The examination is scheduled to be held on 8th November 2015 at various centers.TSNPDCL has already issued an employment notification for the recruitment of candidates for the Posts of Assistant Engineers . Lot of applications were received by the interested and eligible persons. The candidates have to take the examination and qualify for the exam to get the recruitment. The complete information is placed in the official website. So candidates are advised to visit the website before going to write the exam.The Online examination will be conducted at various centers across the state on 8th November 2015. The candidates those who are short listed have to attend for the next process of recruitment. The candidates can follow the below mentioned procedure to download the call letters in online.Procedure to Download the Call Letters for Assistant Engineer Exam 2015:1. Visit the official website of TSNPDCL2. The home page will be displayed on the screen3. Then go to the careers page4. Select the link named as “ Download Admit card for AE Exam Link “5. Then enter the details like Registration Number and Date of Birth .6. After entering the details, click on “Login” button7. The admit card will be displayed on the screen8. Candidates are advised to take the printout of it and save it for further reference.Admit card is an important document to be produced at the time of exam. No candidate will be allowed to sit in the exam hall without admit card. So candidates are advised to download their admit card before going for the exam.
