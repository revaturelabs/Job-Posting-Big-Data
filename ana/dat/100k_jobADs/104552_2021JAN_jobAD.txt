Often receiving excellent reviews, the team show their commitment to the future of medical education. The unique community based Hepatitis C program in conjunction with the hospital based service was highly commended in the National Quality & Care awards in 2014.Radiological delivered paracentesis was embedded in 2017 and an “IBD Hub” has been established in recent months enabling the team to virtually monitor this chronic cohort of patients.The department runs an established EUS list along with various other advanced endoscopy areas.Gastroenterology Team:The team consists of 9 consultants, 2 SpRs and a large support team of both clinical and non-clinical staff.Candidate Specification:Requirements for this position are:MRCP UK or equivalentIELTS or OETAdvanced endoscopy experience including ERCP, EUSExtensive experience in the delivery of therapeutic gastroenterology and diagnostic colonoscopyPrevious consultant experience, ideally within the NHS but those without will be considered.Please note, overseas candidates not on the specialist register will be offered
