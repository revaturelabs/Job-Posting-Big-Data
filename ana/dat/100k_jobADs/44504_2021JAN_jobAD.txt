notification to recruit the candidates for Agriculture Development Officer. A total number of 43 vacancies are released for recruitment. However, the vacancies are distributed among various categories. The last date to apply for the recruitment is 5th February 2021. The candidates who wish to apply for the recruitment should know the basic details like eligibility criteria, selection process, salary details etc.The candidates must be between 18 - 32 years of age. However, age relaxations are applicable to the reserved category candidates.The candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree from the university which is recognized by the government.The candidates will undergo 2 stages of the selection process - written examination followed by the interview.The candidates who are selected will get a salary of INR 56,100 - 1,77,500/-.Get the complete details regarding the recruitment from the below article.Arunachal Pradesh PSC ADO Recruitment OverviewRecruiting Body APPSCFull-Form Arunachal Pradesh Public Service CommissionOfficial Website Click HerePost Agriculture Development OfficerNumber of Vacancies 43Notification Release Date 28th December 2020Last Date to Apply 5th February 2021Admit Card Date Will be NotifiedWritten Exam Date Will be NotifiedResult Will be NotifiedCheck Arunachal Pradesh PSC ADO NotificationArunachal Pradesh PSC ADO VacancyA total number of 43 vacancies are released for recruitment. The candidates must get 33% of marks in each subject and 45% of marks out of total marks. The candidates will be shortlisted in a ration of 1:3.Viva Voice:The candidates who are qualified in the written examination are called for the Viva Voice process. This is conducted for 50 marks. The candidates must produce all the original documents required at the time of interview.Arunachal Pradesh PSC ADO EligibilityAge LimitThe candidate must be between 18 - 32 years of age as on 05.02.2021. However, the upper age limit is relaxable to the candidates belonging to reserved category.Educational QualificationThe candidates must have a Bachelor’s Degree from the university which is recognized by the government.
