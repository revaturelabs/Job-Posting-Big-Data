Following the first hotel in Asakusa, the second hotel is designed to maximize the charm of the local Ginza area.The new Gate Hotel Tokyo creates a sense of unity with the outside environment with four outside terraces unique in the Ginza area. Additionally, in most of the spaces, the seating area is set by the window to time-lapse the cityscape and feel the neighborhood.
