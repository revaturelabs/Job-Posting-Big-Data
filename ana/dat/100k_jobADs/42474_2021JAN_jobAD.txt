The hospital is staffed 24x7 and provides preventative, sick and emergency care to the surrounding area. This modern facility is a fully computerized hospital outfitted with the most up-to-date equipment available. Hospital campus includes separate large animal haul-in, small animal hospital and ICU, and Boarding.About the area: Our location offers a number of exciting activities for the outdoor enthusiast. The area's many parks and game-lands provide excellent opportunities for horseback riding, camping, hiking, and hunting. The Ohio River and many lakes provide a wonderful scene for boating and fishing opportunities as well. If you prefer the excitement of city life, downtown Pittsburgh is just 40 minutes away. Pittsburgh is a modern, cultural city offering dozens of dining, shopping and entertainment opportunities. The city is also home to three professional sports teams and several major universities. Looking for adventure? Pennsylvania’s Laurel Highlands and West Virginia are both just a short drive away. Both areas offer exciting outdoor opportunities, including some of the best white water rafting in the country. In the winter, downhill skiing is easily accessible in the Laurel Highlands and also in Western New York (less than 2 hours north in the Snow Belt).
