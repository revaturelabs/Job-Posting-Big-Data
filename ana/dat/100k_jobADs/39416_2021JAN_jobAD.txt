Manchester Airport is the fourth busiest airport in the United Kingdom, with over 20 million passengers passing through its terminals every year. Therefore quite a few airport security officers are needed within the airport, and applicants from all backgrounds are welcome.In order to obtain one of the airport security jobs in Manchester, candidates must satisfy a few physical requirements. Firstly they must have good vision. This is of course very important, as security officers must examine passports and boarding passes as passengers move into restricted areas of the airport. It is important that security officers be in a position to detect any irregularities with these documents. Similarly, security officers in Manchester Airport may also be assigned to X-ray machines. It is also essential that these officers be capable of detecting any suspicious items displayed on the screens of their scanning equipment.Those who apply for the airport security jobs in Manchester Airport must be able to stand for long periods of time. Occasionally security officers may be required to patrol the terminals, and therefore candidates must be capable of walking for a number of hours. They must also be able to bend and straighten easily, so that they can lift heavy items of luggage and conduct body searches.In addition to satisfying these physical requirements, candidates for airport security jobs in Manchester must pass a background check before they can be accepted for these positions. Training will be provided and both male and female security officers are required.
