Our hospital is housed in a modern, 4,000 sq. foot building that is outfitted with all of the equipment, technology, and amenities necessary for providing our patients with exceptional care throughout their lives. There are four spacious exam rooms, two advanced diagnostic laboratories, a fully equipped surgical suite and cutting edge dental treatment room, both featuring the latest in veterinary medical technology, as well as separate dog and cat kennel areas.The staff at Carlsbad Animal Hospital is comprised of a team of highly skilled, dedicated professionals who share the same goal of doing whatever it takes to help each patient we serve to enjoy as many happy, healthy years as possible and to exceed our clients’ expectations with every interaction. We always go the extra mile when it comes to making our clients and their pets feel welcome and showing you how important they are to us. Learn more about us at www.carlsbadanimalhospital.com.Aside from our commitment to improving the lives of animals and the people who love them, we also feel strongly about giving back to the community. We work with many different local rescue organizations such as English Springer Spaniel rescue, Southern California Rescue and much more. We also work in conjunction with the FACE Foundation, a nonprofit organization that offers financial assistance for certain kinds of medical conditions. Our military is very important to us so we make regular donations to Camp Pendleton Marine Animal Shelter, including food donations, blankets, and other supplies. It is important to us that the shelter pets get what they need too.At Carlsbad Animal Hospital we take the health and well-being of our employees seriously, offering flexible working conditions and encouraging a well-rounded work/life balance. Our compensation package includes a competitive salary, quality health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off, generous continuing education allowance and additional paid time off, numerous discounts through our partners and reimbursement for membership dues and license fees.Carlsbad Animal Hospital is a part of the PetVet Care Centers network; where each network hospital is unique and connected to its community.
