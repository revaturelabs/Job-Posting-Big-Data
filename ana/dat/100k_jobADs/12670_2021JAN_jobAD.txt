Those looking to work as Undergraduate Research Assistants should be able to complete duties such as assisting faculty members, performing library research, collecting and analyzing data, preparing spreadsheets, updating websites, and handling clerical tasks. Successful resume samples for this role showcase the following assets: a good academic record, organizational and communication skills, teamwork, problem-solving orientation, research skills, and attention to details.
