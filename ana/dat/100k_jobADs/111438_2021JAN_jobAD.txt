For the recruitment of 61 vacant posts. Where these vacancies are present in various departments such as. Lower division clerk, technical assistant (field farm technician), technical assistant (workshop), technical assistant (laboratory technician) and technician. So in order to employ all these vacancies with eligible and appropriate candidates. NDRI has given the employment notification. And accepting applications from all interested and qualified candidates. Thus aspirants those who are willing to work in the government sector in the state of Haryana. And those who have sufficient educational qualification such as applicants must have passed 12th class, diploma in electronic and communication/ civil/ mechanical/ electrical/ from any recognized board / university. Can apply for these vacancies through offline. Such as aspirants have to post the filled application form to the below mentioned address. On or before the last date of 16th February 2016. Other important details like age limit, educational qualification, selection process, application fee, application process and important dates are given below.NDRI Haryana Recruitment 2016 details:Total number of available posts: 61 postsName of the post:Lower Division Clerk: 05 postsTechnician: 08 postsTechnical Assistant (Field Farm Technician): 19 postsTechnical Assistant (Laboratory Technician): 15 postsTechnical Assistant (Workshop): 14 postsAge limit: Applicant’s age should be between 18 years to 30 years as on the last date of 1st January 2016. Age relaxation would be applicable to all S/ST/PWD candidates.Educational qualification: aspirants must have passed 12th class or intermediate, diploma in electronic and communication/ civil/ mechanical/ electrical/ from any recognized board / university.Selection process: National Dairy Research Institute will employ the candidates based on the performance in the written exam, tying test and personal interview.Application fee: aspirants have to pay application fee of 100/- through online mode.Pay scale: selected candidates would be given a salary of Rs. 5200-20200/- + Grade pay Rs. 2800/- per month.How to apply: all interested and eligible candidates those who are willing to apply. For these vacancies are advised to post the filled application form to the below mentioned address. On or before the last date.Postal address:To the Sr. Administration Officer, E-III (M) Section, ICAR-National Dairy Research Institute, Karnal – 132 001, Haryana.Important dates for NDRI Haryana Recruitment 2016:Last date for the submission of application form: 16th February 2016.Official Notification available hereCategories Haryana Post navigationNBCC Recruitment 2016-32 MT Civil Posts nbcc.comICAR–NRRI Recruitment 2016-69 Technical Assistant PostsLeave a Comment Cancel replyCommentName Email WebsiteSave my name, email, and website in this browser for the next time I comment.Trending PostsSSC 10th Class Duplicate Certificate Online Download Memorandum at bseap.orgBy Mahee ReddyEmployment News of This Week January 2021 Job HighlightsBy Mahee ReddyTelangana (TS) Govt Jobs 2021 – TSPSC Daily UpdatesBy Mahee ReddyEngineering Jobs Mechanical Engineering Jobs and Civil Engineering JobsBy Mahee ReddyGovt Jobs in AP 2021 – This Month January Job HighlightsBy Mahee ReddyHSSC Recruitment 2016-369 Posts Apply at hssc.gov.inBy Mahee ReddyGovt Jobs in UP 2021 – Sarkari Naukri in UP Daily UpdatesBy Mahee ReddyLatest Govt Jobs in Bihar 2021 -Daily Updates in BiharBy Mahee ReddyCopyright © 2014 -2021 - Government Jobs Blog | About Blog | Contact | Privacy | Terms & Conditions"
