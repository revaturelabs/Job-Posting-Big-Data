You will also manage and oversee MFS Investment Management's strategic global technology partners engaged to provide flexibility and scalability to our full-time information technology staff.The Technology Partner Management Services are provided to different areas across GTIO (Global Development, Strategic Investments and Distribution Initiatives, and other groups as needed). Partnership with Finance and key GTIO stakeholders to understand needs while delivering and focusing on continuous process improvement.In partnership with stakeholders across MFS, define, develop and report a standard set of Project, Program metrics that highlight trends, challenges, and progress. Track supplier performance through standard scorecards/KPIs to ensure IT suppliers are held accountable to quality, efficiency and cost effective delivery of services. Track supplier volumes and ensuring billing and true-ups are in alignment with agreed terms. Evolve metrics and reports as new needs emerge.Oversee and manage resources and associated deliverables. Direct management responsibilities of employees, co-ops, and contractors including cross-functional resources.Serve as the primary point of contact for vendor onboarding, knowledge management transition and receiving and resolving escalations around issues and concerns related to assigned vendors. Integrate the IT Vendor oversight governance framework with MFS Vendor management practices with appropriate SLAs developed or updated. Proactively plan for operational impacts on vendor and partners with internal business stakeholders and vendor to ensure successful performance management.Work closely with MFS Legal team in reviewing IT Services contract to make sure MFS interests are protected.
