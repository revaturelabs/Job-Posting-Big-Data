Patrols network grounds on a continuous basis and offers assistance and information to the general public. SHIFT: 12 Hour day or night shiftDUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: * Performs campus surveillance by vehicle or walking to ensure security and watches for situations that could result in injury or loss due to potentially unsafe conditions. * Screens all vehicles at the Network entrances and monitors activity at or near assigned post. Maintains a sense of vigilance for undesirable, dangerous, or counterproductive activities or individuals. * Monitors camera activity, fire protection systems and other devices from a control room and dispatches calls as necessary. * Relays critical information to other security personnel as necessary. * Provides accurate and timely written or verbal information/instruction to all vendors, visitors, pilgrims, or anyone conducting business with the Network. * Reviews and has personal knowledge of the Safety and Security daily postings and instructions. * Initiates emergency 911 calls as appropriate and directs emergency vehicles to proper location on site. * Administers First Aid, CPR, basic emergency assistance to employees and guests as needed. * Completes incident and accident report forms as necessary. * Transports guests to and from the Birmingham airport. * Communicates with and assists other Network departments whenever special instructions for guests or visitors are required. * Directs traffic for special functions and some live show events. * Participates in department training and drills as necessary. Job Requirements:QUALIFICATIONS:* Experience in security, law enforcement, military, EMS or related field.* Possess valid Alabama State Driver's License.* Possess valid State of Alabama pistol permit or be able to obtain upon hire.* Ability to qualify with a firearm.* Possess up to date First Aid and CPR certification or be willing to obtain within 90 days of hire date.* Basic knowledge of OSHA & NFPA codes and standards.* Possess basic computer skills.* Strong verbal and written communication skills.* Ability to maintain confidentiality around sensitive items and security issues.* Attention to the needs of others; must be courteous while also being direct.* Possess a keen sense of observation of physical surroundings.* Must be able to work overtime if necessary.* Physically able to perform the duties, including the ability to lift up to 50 lbs. * Must be able to work in one place for long periods of time in all weather conditions.* Must be able to work under a command-type structure and follow instructions regardless of the form of communications, e.g., 2-way radio, telephone, voice, etc.* Must be willing to work assigned 12 hr day or night shiftREQUIRED EDUCATION:* High school diploma or GEDADDITIONAL INFORMATION:Benefits include: Medical, dental, FSA, vision care, life insurance, short & long term disability, retirement plan with employer match, CDU Educational assistance, paid time off, and 11 paid holidays.
