You work with our sales team to identify our ideal customers and prospects. You engage in outbound prospecting and lead generation. You are the first contact many of our customers will have with Astadia beyond our website or an email. You will often introduce our clients to who Astadia is – our professionalism, our character, our drive, our knowledge, and our nature.
