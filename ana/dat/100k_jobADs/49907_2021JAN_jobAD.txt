The car manufacturer has also put on hold its automated driving alliance with Daimler’s /zigman2/quotes/205332368/delayed DE:DAI +3.62% Mercedes-Benz.Car sales have plunged in recent months and the industry’s major players have halted production as a result of the Covid-19 crisis. New car sales in Germany fell 50% in May compared with the previous year, with Volkswagen, BMW and Daimler sales all halving.
