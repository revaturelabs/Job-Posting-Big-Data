"Arkansas paper mill reducing capacity, cutting 79 jobs - News - Times Record - Fort Smith, ARSectionsNewsSportsEntertainmentLifeObituariesE-EditionJobsCarsHomesClassifiedsLocal PhotosSiteArchiveArkansas paper mill reducing capacity, cutting 79 jobsThe Associated PressFridayOct 4, 2019 at 10:39 AMLITTLE ROCK — A paper mill in southwest Arkansas is reducing its capacity and cutting 79 jobs.South Carolina-based Domtar Corp. announced Thursday that it was permanently shutting down one of its paper machines at its facility in Ashdown. The company says that it will continue to operate one paper machine and employ approximately 725 people at the facility.The company says the mill also operates one of the world’s largest fluff pulp machines.The company also announced it was shutting down a paper machine at its mill in Port Huron, Michigan, a move the company says will affect 22 employees.
