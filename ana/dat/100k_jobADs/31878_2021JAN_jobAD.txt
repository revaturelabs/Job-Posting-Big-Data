She led Visit San Antonio since 2011, first as executive director and then as president & CEO. Matej serves on the board of directors for U.S. Travel Association and was named to the Top 25 Women in the Meetings Industry by Meetings & Convention Magazine. Her more than two decades of experience in the tourism industry includes stints at Visit Dallas, Starwood Hotels and Resorts, and Hyatt Hotels.
