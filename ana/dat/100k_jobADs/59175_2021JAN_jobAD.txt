(DCI), established in 2004 in Prince William County, Virginia, is a structural engineering design firm specialized in the design, inspection and management of infrastructure such as bridge and highway structures. DCI has designed projects either as a prime or teamed with large design firms as a sub-consultant. DCI engineers have been involved in a number of large projects in the DC region including VDOT Transform I-66 Outside the Beltway, VDOT’s Woodrow Wilson, VDOT Route 28 PPTA and DDOT’s 11th Street and South Capitol Street Corridor Bridge Projects. DCI has also designed small projects for cities, authorities and private clients including DC Government, City of Fairfax, Fairfax County Park Authority, and Metropolitan Washington Airport Authority (MWAA). The projects have varied from vehicular and pedestrian bridge designs, retaining walls, signal pole foundation analysis and design, Interstate sign attachment, structural inspections and evaluations. DCI has experience in structural rehabilitation and replacement bridge projects.
