MSABD is a state-of-the-art $53 million investment in a next-generation facility for research, teaching, and outreach related to meat science, food safety, and value-added molecules or materials derived from animal co-products.The successful candidate will have broad knowledge of the meats industry, established professional networks, and demonstrated leadership.
