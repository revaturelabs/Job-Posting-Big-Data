Central London and Victoria station are a 30 minute train ride away. The Hospital consists of 2 Outpatient areas, plus a Bupa Health Assessment Centre. There are 2 ward areas with 29 single rooms, a 2 bedded emergency recovery unit with a 16 bedded day care unit and five single rooms. There are also 3 Operating theatres – 2 with laminar flow.Duties and responsibilities:Based on our Pathology reception desk you will be responsible for the receipt of all samples before ensuring they are processed within the correct area. This is a busy team that requires high volumes of data entry and will involve plenty of time on the phone as you speak with stakeholders ensuring the timely receipt and delivery of results.Who we’re looking for:A good administrator who is organised and accurateKnowledge of Pathology would be a benefitGood computer literacyExcellent customer service skillsBenefits:We offer employees a competitive salary as well as a comprehensive benefits package which includes but is not limited to:35 days annual leave inclusive of bank holidaysEmployer and employee contributory pension with flexible retirement options‘Spire for you’ reward platform – discount and cashback for over 1000 retailersFree Bupa wellness screeningPrivate medical insuranceLife assuranceSave an average of £50 per month with our free onsite car parkWe commit to our employees well-being through work life balance, on-going development, support and reward.Spire Healthcare is a leading independent hospital group in the United Kingdom and the largest in terms of revenue. From 39 hospitals and 8 clinics across England, Wales and Scotland, Spire Healthcare provides diagnostics, inpatient, day case and outpatient care.For further information about this role or for an informal conversation about the range of career options available with Spire please contact Richard Marsom on richard.marsom@spirehealthcare.comFor us, it’s more than just treating patients; it’s about looking after people.Closing date: In order to streamline our recruitment process, we reserve the right to expire vacancies prior to the advertised closing date once we have received a sufficient number of applicationsTo stay safe in your job search we recommend that you visit SAFERjobs ( ), a non-profit, joint industry and law enforcement organisation working to combat job scams.
