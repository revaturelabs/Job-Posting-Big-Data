Job, College JobArya Vidyapeeth College Recruitment 2021Last date: 15 daysApplications in prescribed format with bio-data, photocopies of marksheets, testimonials and other relevant documents are invited from eligible candidates for the following Sanctioned Posts of Assistant Professors in Arya Vidyapeeth College, Guwahati, Assam.Name of Post: Assistant ProfessorNo. of Posts: 04 PostsSubject wise vacancy:Mathematics: 2 [ Unreserved, Roster Point No. 03; Unreserved, Roster Point No. 04]Hindi: 1 [ Unreserved, Roster Point No. 46]Assamese: 1 [ Reserved for OBC / MOBC, Roster Point No. 99]Educational Qualification and Selection Procedure:As per Govt. Office Memorandum No. AHE.407/2017/54 dated 25/11/2020 with the NET/ SLET / SET as mandatory eligibility conditions. Candidates having PhD degree in accordance with the UGC (minimum standard and procedure for award of PhD degree) Regulation 2009 shall be exempted from the requirement of minimum eligibility conditions of NET/ SLET/ SET.The candidates must have minimum 55% marks ( or an equivalent grade point scale) at the Master’s degree level in the concerned subject. A relaxation of 5% marks may be provided to SC / ST/ PWD candidates. A relaxation of 5% marks may be provided to PhD holders who have obtained their Master’s degree prior to 19th September 1991. The candidates have to acquire qualifications as on the last date for submitting applications. No additional qualifications / published papers/ books chapter etc. obtained after the last date of application shall be considered.Age Limit: The age of the candidates should not be more than 38 years on 01.01.2021 with a relaxation of 5 years for SC / ST/ PWD candidates and 3 years for OBC /MOBC candidates.How to apply for Arya Vidyapeeth College, Guwahati RecruitmentApplications in prescribed format ( available in DHE Assam’s website and College website https://www.avcollege.ac.in/ ) with complete biodata and self attested copies of all testimonials from HSLC (10th standard) onwards must be accompanied by a non-refundable Demand Draft of Rs. 2000/- (Rupees Two Thousand ) only drawn in favour of Principal, Arya Vidyapeeth College, Guwahati-16 payable at Guwahati. Applications must reach the Principal, Arya Vidyapeeth College, Guwahati-16 within 15 days from the date of publication [DOP: AT, 12-01-2021] of this advertisement.Address: To, Principal, Arya Vidyapeeth College, Guwahati-16.Application Form: Click HereAdvertisement Details: Click HereShare This:Facebook Twitter Google+ Pinterest Linkedin WhatsappCollege JobBy thejobinassam at January 13, 2021Email ThisBlogThis!Share to TwitterShare to FacebookShare to PinterestTags Assam Career, Assam Govt.
