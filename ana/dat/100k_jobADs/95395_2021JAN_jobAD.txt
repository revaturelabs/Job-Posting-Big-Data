The 501(c)(3) nonprofit was founded in 2009 and is located on the Taube Koret Campus for Jewish Life, a multi-generational site where families and individuals connect, learn, and play. Serving as a central gathering place for the Jewish community throughout Silicon Valley, the Oshman Family JCC strives to create inclusive, joyful, and meaningful experiences that enable members to explore new ways to integrate Jewish values and traditions into modern life.The OFJCC offers membership to anyone interested in joining a community center dedicated to nourishing their body, mind, and spirit. Its programs feature experiences \"for all ages and interests,\" which are provided in state-of-the-art facilities, including classrooms, a cultural arts hall, fitness centers, and more. Across these programs, the Oshman Family JCC supports over 250 preschool families, 9,000 fitness-center members, and more than 15,000 weekly visitors. Furthermore, its cultural arts programs are visited by more than 23,000 patrons every year, while over 20,000 residents in the region receive its promotional materials. These OFJCC programs are sustained with support from over 1,100 active donors and corporate partners, including established companies such as Accenture.As an employer, the Oshman Family Jewish Community Center looks for candidates who support its vision to become \"the architects of the Jewish future.\" Team members enjoy opportunities to participate in the JCC's active collaboration efforts with local nonprofits, national organizations, and tech companies. Because the Oshman Family JCC wants its employees to \"be well,\" it offers benefits packages that may include health coverage, retirement plans, \"discounts galore,\" paid time off, and more.
