Once you file your ITR, the income tax department does the assessment and refund, if any, gets credited directly into your pre-validated bank account. If you have been waiting for your tax refund and are not aware of how to check the status, then you are not the only one. There are many people who don’t know how to check their income tax refund status.How to check the status of your income tax refundOnce you file your ITR and claim a tax refund, you can track your income tax refund status online at www.incometaxindia.gov.in or www.tin-nsdl.com.Log on to any of these two websites and click on ‘Status of Tax Refunds’ tab, enter your PAN and the assessment year (AY) for which your refund is pending.Status of tax refund: Through an e-filing websiteVisit the income tax e-filing portalLogin with a user ID, password, DOB/ date of incorporation, and captchaGo to ‘My Account’ and click on ‘Refund/Demand Status’Select ‘Income Tax Returns’ from the drop-down menu and click on submitClick on the acknowledgment numberA new webpage will appear on your screen showing complete details of your ITR filingStatus of tax refund: Through the NSDL website1. Visit the website https://tin.tin.nsdl.com/oltas/refundstatuslogin.html2. Enter your PAN details3. Select the relevant assessment year for which you want to check the refund status.4.
