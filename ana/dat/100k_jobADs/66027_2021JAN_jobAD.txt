The team provides stakeholder engagement support to projects across a variety of sectors including transport, energy, water and waste as well as residential and commercial property development.This role will focus on two key areas:1. Assisting with the delivery of a range of stakeholder engagement services, with a focus on engagement and consultation for infrastructure development projects;2. Assisting with the development of new business proposals and marketing activities to help win new projects and support the growth of AECOM’s stakeholder engagement services team.Day-to-day, the role will predominantly involve working closely with the other members of the stakeholder engagement team but will often involve working as part of a much larger team to deliver projects. Some travel will be required to other AECOM and client offices or project locations. Some home working to maximise work efficiency and reduce time lost to travel will be included in the working arrangements.The role will report directly to a member of the stakeholder engagement team. There will also be a need to report to other AECOM team members on specific projects. There will be on-the-job learning to develop stakeholder engagement expertise and opportunities to develop other skills via AECOM and external training.Job ResponsibilitiesDay-to-date delivery of stakeholder engagement services on a range of projects including:research to support stakeholder and political audits/mappingdrafting text for various communications materials such as booklets, response forms, websites and social media posts and consultation reportslogistical support for engagement and consultation eventssupport to manage stakeholder enquiriesanalysis of responses to consultationsSupport in identifying new business opportunities for the provision of stakeholder engagement services and associated proposal development including:management of the team's new business trackerdrafting text and helping to co-ordinate submission of proposals for new businesssupport in the ongoing proactive promotion of the AECOM stakeholder engagement service including drafting of marketing materials for the team such as case studies and presentations.RequirementsMust demonstrate an interest in stakeholder engagement and communications, helping organisations explain proposals
