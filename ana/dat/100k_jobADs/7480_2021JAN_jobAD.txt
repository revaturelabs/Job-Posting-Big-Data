The city of Poughkeepsie benefits from rich cultural diversity and from convenient commuter rail access to New York City.Position IntroductionThe Asian Studies Program at Vassar College invites applications for a Visiting Assistant Professor or Visiting Instructor of Korean Language for the 2021-2022 academic year.AA StatementVassar College is an affirmative action and equal opportunity employer with a strong commitment to increasing the diversity of the campus community and the curriculum, and promoting an environment of equality, inclusion, and respect for difference. Candidates who can contribute to this goal through their teaching, research, advising, and other activities are encouraged to identify their strengths and experiences in this area. Individuals from groups whose underrepresentation in the American professoriate has been severe and longstanding are particularly encouraged to apply.Position DescriptionRequirements for this full-time position include a Ph.D., ABD, or MA in Korean language, linguistics, pedagogy, or other related discipline and the ability to teach all levels of Korean language. Experience in Korean language teaching is necessary. The successful candidate will teach five courses, including elementary, intermediate, and advanced Korean Language classes and supervise students\\' independent work.How to ApplyPlease apply online at https://employment.vassar.edu/postings/1689Candidates should submit:-a cover letter-curriculum vitae-copies of syllabi of courses taught or proposed-three letters of recommendation.For inquiries, email Christopher Bjork, the Director of Asian Studies Program (asianstudies@vassar.edu).Review of applicant materials will begin immediately and will continue until the position has been filled.Special Instructions to ApplicantsTo ApplyAll applicants must apply online at: https://apptrkr.com/2113814Copyright ©2017 Jobelephant.com Inc.
