Assist, further to Decision of the World Heritage Committee, in the overall coordination at WHC to ensure the holistic approach across the regions with regard to the Periodic Reporting process, through specialized assistance in the preparation, design and update of guidance and training materials and communication tools in the required languages for the Third cycle of the Periodic Reporting exercise; - Provide guidance and support to each of the Regional Units at WHC and to stakeholders involved in the respective regions undergoing the Periodic Reporting exercise, on content and technical matters, including IT aspects throughout the different phases of the cycle, including assisting in the launch, reporting process, preparation of the synthetic report on the basis of the approved analytical framework, and participating in training sessions on the technical use of the online Periodic Reporting tool; - Ensure support on specific technical matters related to the back-end of the online Periodic Reporting tool, including the management of the questionnaire settings and databases, the technical specifications related to user access, cache applications, log errors, prefilling and postfilling of data, editing of questionnaire contents in HTML as well as the development of the Periodic Reporting statistical tool with regard to data exports, accessibility via the online platform and preparation of country and regional datasets; - Collect and analyse technical requests and needs in the area of the Periodic Reporting Questionnaire, including the online tool, and advise users, as required, on the possible solutions; - Provide inputs to relevant documents, including for the statutory meetings of the World Heritage Committee and the General Assembly of the States Parties to the World Heritage Convention and carry out necessary IT and organizational tasks to follow-up and implement the recommendations and decisions of the statutory bodies with regard to the Periodic Reporting exerciseAccountability (C) -
