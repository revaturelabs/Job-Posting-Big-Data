Rentals are customized with brand-new Ford vehicles and experienced English-speaking chauffeurs. AVIS also offers car rental and leasing that meets all types of customers requirements for business trips, leisure or field work, with or without chauffeurs.RMA Cambodia has a financing arm, RMA Financial Services, offers financing solutions to flexibly meet the needs of corporate and retail customers for the purchase of their own automotive and heavy equipment, agricultural machinery and warehouse solution equipment.RMA Cambodia is also a provider of heavy equipment products and solutions in construction, road building and maintenance, and agriculture, namely JCB, Mitsubishi FUSO and John Deere.
