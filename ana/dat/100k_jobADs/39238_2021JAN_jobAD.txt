The Graphics Cache Hierarchy Verification Engineer will be responsible for the pre-silicon RTL verification of graphics memory subsystem units including Caches, Memory Management Unit, Interconnects and Link interface units. This includes deep understanding of the micro-architectural details of their block and how it works within the broader GPU design. A strong computer architecture background, and a solid foundation in verification methodology is required.Key QualificationsKey QualificationsAt least 3-5 years of meaningful experience including: Experience developing unit or cluster level test environmentsExperience defining and executing unit level test plans.
