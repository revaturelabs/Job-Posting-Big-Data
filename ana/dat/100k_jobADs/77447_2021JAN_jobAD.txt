HomepageStarter/Ranger (Mountain Course)Greets golfers at No. 1 & No. 10 tees to provide a brief synopsis of the Mtn Course offerings, incl. layout & course rules. Enforces speed of play and assists with opening/closing procedures. Req: HS diploma (GED) & 3 mo related customer service experience. Valid Driver’s License. Good communication & customer service skills, with knowledge of the game of golf and the Lake Tahoe area also helpful. Rate: Starts $10.50 - $11/hr DOE.
