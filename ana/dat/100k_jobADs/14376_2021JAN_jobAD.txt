The IT Infrastructure Officer will also manage end-user and direct activities to resolve hardware and software problems in a timely and accurate fashion.RESPONSIBILITIES/ DUTIES:♦ Support and monitor entire IT ecosystem (infrastructure, security, network, database, software) to ensure that all operations services are running without faults;♦ Manage the delivery of IT Services that meet agreed service;♦ Ensure that documentation of our IT ecosystem is maintained and created where needed♦ Identify, propose and address areas of improvement in the IT systems and infrastructure;♦ Configuring and installing various network devices and services (e.g., routers, switches, firewalls, load balancers, VPN, QoS)♦ Performing network maintenance and system upgrades including service packs, patches, hot fixes and security configurations♦ Manage and ensure effectiveness of security solutions, including firewalls, anti-virus solutions, and intrusion detection systems♦ Undertake other duties as may be required from time to time.♦ Service desk supportOther Duties♦ Assist CBC management with other tasks as required;♦ Take initiative in doing other task appropriately;
