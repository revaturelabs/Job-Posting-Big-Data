2017in: JobLeave a comment2 Viewsshare0 0Job at Chemonics International: Social and Behavior Change Communications (SBCC) Specialist (Deadline: 20 April 2017)Chemonics International, a U.S. based international development consulting firm, is seeking a social and behavior change communications (SBCC) specialist for the anticipated USAID-funded Feed the Future Rwanda Increased Protein for Dietary Diversity (Orora Wihaze) activity. This five-year, $10-25 million project will sustainably increase the availability of, access to, and consumption of animal-source foods in order to improve the dietary diversity scores of women of reproductive age (15-49 years), especially pregnant and lactating women (PLW), and children between the age of 6-23 months in poor rural households.Social and Behavior Change Communications (SBCC) SpecialistAdvanced degree in a relevant subject matter area.At least seven years of experience designing and implementing social and behavior change interventions.Experience developing and implementing SBCC strategies in agriculture and nutrition, ideally in RwandaExcellent communication and writing skills in English. French and Kinyarwanda preferred.Application Instructions: Please complete the application and submit CV, cover letter, and contact information by April 20, 2017 through this form. No telephone inquiries, please. Finalists will be contacted.
