Business DevelopmentDirector, Public Sector Business DevelopmentBasking Ridge, NJ, United StatesFull-Time StrategyJob # 550078Date posted - 2021-01-19ApplyShare this JobWhat you’ll be doing...The Director of Business Development (BD) will lead a team of leaders and business development professionals responsible for driving significant growth in Public Sector’s largest opportunity accounts in a cost-effective manner.The Director (player/coach) will lead their team to:- Top Account Relationship ManagementAdviseto senior client stakeholders, co-creating connectivity, security, and IT strategy with CIO/CTO of Top AccountsCultivate and maintain relationships with senior stakeholders at Top Accounts (e.g., CIO)Lead Public Sector’s top 100 to 150 ranked accounts overall business relationship and other nominated strategic engagementsDevelop and execute an overall client business plan to achieve Public Sector’s client objectives and nurturing senior executive and board level relationships as required for plan deliveryBuild trust and credibility at all levels within the client organization and internally within Public Sector and, thereby, identify business client priorities and ensure Public Sector has a key role in achieving them; responsible for shaping and driving all opportunities to a successful conclusion- Opportunity Identification and QualificationDevelop strategies to drive upsell and cross-sell revenue in a top account, by identifying, qualifying, and managing execution of new growth opportunitiesDevelop a deep understanding of existing technology infrastructure and customer needs and challengesAssess feasibility of addressing different needs given available suite of products and identify and prioritize opportunities for account growth- Top Account StrategyLead identified opportunities through qualification process; develop business cases for high-priority opportunitiesLiaise with Top Account, Finance, and Sales teams to influence bid parameters of opportunities and determine relative ROI of bid- Contract & Bid Management SupportParticipate in cross-functional review sessions of proposal (e.g., technical fit, compliance, value proposition) to ensure competitiveness of bid within broader account strategyCo-create contract vehicle strategy for relevant agency with capture and contracts/proposals teamDevelop 1-year, 3-year, and 5-year account plans for a Top Account, keeping in mind Verizon’s changing product suite and market landscapeLead the development of relevant market research, customer segmentation, and competitive analysis to inform sales strategiesOrchestrate and coach internal sales and solutions teams in implementing strategyWhat we’re looking for...Bachelor’s degree and/or 10+ years of relevant work experience8+ years in business development and/or capture roles in the technology industry5+ years of direct supervisory experienceIdeally science/engineering/commercial degree qualifiedDemonstrable experience in achieving results in a complex, distributed organization and of providing complex, multi-departmental, IT, or Telecom services to government clientsExperience leading major IT transformation efforts involving organization and process re-designExperience managing and influencing client relationships at the senior executive levelKnowledge of various forms of technology – intermediate to advanced user of MS applications, Visio, PowerPoint, Excel, and internal applicationWhen you join VerizonYou’ll have the power to go beyond – doing the work that’s transforming how people, businesses and things connect with each other. Not only do we provide the fastest and most reliable network for our customers, but we were first to 5G - a quantum leap in connectivity. Our connected solutions are making communities stronger and enabling energy efficiency. Here, you’ll have the ability to make an impact and create positive change.
