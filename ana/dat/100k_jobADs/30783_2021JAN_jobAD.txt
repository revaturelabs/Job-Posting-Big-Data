Check us out: Madison Animal Hospital - Veterinarian in Madison, MEMadison, Maine is a beautiful small town nestled on the banks of the Kennebec River in the scenic foothills of Maine’s western mountains. Madison is a classic small town that offers a taste of the arts and outdoor adventure. Lake Wesserunsett and the Kennebec River along with miles of scenic trails are prime for excellent camping, fishing, hiking, biking, golfing, snowshoeing, and cross-country skiing. Sugarloaf Mountain, the second largest ski area in the East, is a short drive (40mi) away and offers excellent skiing as well as four season recreation including golf, mountain biking, ziplines and more. The Appalachian Trail (37mi), Acadia National Park (100mi) and Katahdin Mountain in Baxter State Park (115mi) are popular destinations. Maine’s picturesque rocky coastline, lighthouses, and seaside villages are about a 1.5hr drive away.
