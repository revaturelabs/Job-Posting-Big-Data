Being the most excellent institute within HZNU in terms of scientific research capability and facility, Division of Health Sciences consists of following 6 schools: School of Basic Medical Science, School of Pharmacy, School of Public Health, School of Nursing, School of Clinical Medicine, School of Stomatology. Moreover, it owns 1 directly affiliated hospital, 12 non-directly affiliated hospitals and more than 70 teaching practice bases. Currently, Division of Health Sciences enrolls more than 3400 students.About our staff:There are about 350 full-time employees, 37% of them have overseas education/research experience, and 78% of them hold doctorate degrees. One member of Academy of Sciences of China undertakes full-time work, and two member of Academy of Sciences of China work part-time. There are also a number of outstanding personnel working in Division of Health Sciences, including the recipients of the National Outstanding Youth Fund, the winners of the National New Century Million Talents Project, chief scientists of the National Major Scientific Research Program, winners of the National Science and Technology Progress Award, winner of He Liang & He Li Award, and winner of Wu Jieping Innovation Award.Scientific Research Achievements:Division of Health Sciences ranks worldwide among the top field of “Preventive Treatment of Diseases”, “Anti-aging”, “Vegetative State and Conscious Scientific Research, “Epigenetic Mechanism of Cancer” , “Cardivascular Disease” etc. In the past three years, Scientific Research Teams in Division of Health Sciences have undertaken more than 70 national-level scientific projects.Division of Health Sciences has been seeking exceptional scholars in the following research fields from all around the world: basic medicine, clinical medicine, stomatology, public health and preventive medicine, nursing, public management, biology, chemistry, genetics and pharmacy. If you are interested in these research fields, please feel free to contact us at any time.Faculty Positions are open at four distinct ranks: Full Professor, Associate Professor, Assistant Professor, and Lecturer.In addition to the competitive salary and social insurance, the university also provides merit pay, position allowance, house purchasing subsidies, and settling-in allowance to qualified talents selected. These benefits are designed according to the university oversea talents recruiting & incentive policy and the details can be found in the document entitled “Recruitment of High-level Talent Plan of Hangzhou Normal University 2020”. (https://rsc.hznu.edu.cn/c/2020-10-10/2459300.shtml)Each new faculty member will be provided with a sufficient research start-up fund, ample laboratory space, excellent equipment, and full research support. Please send the following application documents in PDF format to yxyrcyj@hznu.edu.cnREQUIRED DOCUMENTS· Cover Letter· Curriculum Vitae - your up-to-date CV with publication list· Research Summary and Research Proposal (3-5 pages)REFERENCE REQUIREMENTS· Please arrange 3 reference letters directly by referees to the above email address.For more information, please visit:http://yxy.hznu.edu.cn/homepage/Review and selection process of applications will be conducted by the Academic Search Committee and will start upon receipt of your complete application package. For the outstanding scholars selected, we will arrange the on-campus interview at candidates’ convenient time.
