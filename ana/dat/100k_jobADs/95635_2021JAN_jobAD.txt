We will work collaboratively with the right candidate to define their coverage across areas that may include digital payments, e-commerce, retail technology and fraud prevention.What's in it for you: Are you passionate about fintech, commerce or retail trends? Are you curious about how these sectors are evolving as a result of innovation and emerging technologies? Do you enjoy writing, leveraging data and collaborating with clients? The objective with this role will be to develop expertise in your field and provide support for the broader team coverage, with the intent to develop sufficient expertise to enable you ultimately to work independently with minimal supervision. You will also be asked to attend industry events and, as appropriate, provide media commentary in order to build influence for 451 Research.The Team: As a Customer Experience & Commerce Analyst, you will join a team of accomplished industry analysts that are experts on the evolution of the customer journey. As a member of the team you will assist and, in some cases, lead a variety of our commerce and payments research efforts, including briefing with technology vendors, writing reports, analyzing survey data and presenting to clients. Your primary responsibility will be to cover payments and commerce trends and technology vendors .Qualifications:A successful candidate should have a minimum of 2-5 years' applicable experience in a journalism, research or analysis role.
