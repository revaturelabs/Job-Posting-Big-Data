This position will be responsible for coordinating and executing demand and supply planning activities across both Harry’s and our retail partner’s supply chains. As this role will work across internal and external teams, it requires strong collaboration skills and attention to detail to ensure efficient and accurate product distribution.Primary responsibilities include executing replenishment and operational strategies across the collective Harry’s and retailer Supply Chain. This Customer-facing position is responsible for maximizing Store In-Stocks (via Replenishment Management), Execution and forecasting of New and Discontinued items, Event planning, enhanced Reporting, and inventory Analytics. The CPFR Analyst will use information obtained from the retailer while applying Supply Chain planning information as input into Harry’s Forecasting process. Proficient use of retailer data sharing tools combined with collaboration between the retailer, Sales, and Harry’s internal operational teams will be used to drive efficiencies with predictable product flow throughout the entire supply chain. The Forecast and Replenishment Analyst serves as a collaborative bridge between external Replenishment departments, and internal stakeholders across S&OP, Supply Chain, and Fulfillment.
