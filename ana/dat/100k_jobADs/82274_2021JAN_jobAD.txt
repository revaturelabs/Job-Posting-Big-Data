We are seeking a leader who has demonstrated the ability to set a content strategy, gain executive and stakeholder buy-in, then drive consistent execution of a company-wide content agenda through various marketing/selling functions at scale, including executive communications, demand generation, GTM enablement, and public/analyst/investor relations. You also have a keen and innovative sense for how to pivot content to meet the evolving needs of customers and the market, particularly against the backdrop of increasing online content noise and fatigue. You recognize key content consumption trends and work to stay ahead of them by trying new approaches, channels and form factors. You have the ability to set a content governance model in place to provide guardrails that enable content production and delivery across the company at scale, while enabling us to consistently and effectively stay on message, brand and tone. Lastly, you have your finger on the pulse of the sales technology competitive landscape, and you ensure that we are producing and delivering a content narrative that is differentiated and compelling for customers.
