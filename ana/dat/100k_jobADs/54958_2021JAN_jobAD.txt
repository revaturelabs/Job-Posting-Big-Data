Helmholtz Leadership AcademyScienceAdministration & ManagementTechnical InfrastructureJob VacanciesHomeJobs & TalentWhat Sets Us ApartWhat Sets Us ApartThe Helmholtz Association promotes talent according to the following principles:Scientists are especially supported from the time of their doctorate to their first professorship.Equal opportunities, internationalization, and transfer of technology are interdisciplinary topics of consistent focus.Wherever possible, scientific and administrative talents are promoted together.We place particular value on professional management. Alongside professional training, we provide and emphasize organizational and management skills. To this end, the Helmholtz Leadership Academy was created.The Helmholtz Association and the individual Helmholtz Centers complement each other’s work in this area. Their shared goals and standards are outlined in strategies and guidelines.The Helmholtz NetworkIn addition to the development programs realized through the Helmholtz Academy and the Helmholtz Mentoring Program, the Helmholtz Association also offers flexible development and peer learning opportunities to its leaders and aspiring leaders in research and management.The Helmholtz NetworkHelmholtz Mentoring ProgramThe Helmholtz Association strongly encourages and supports the personal and professional development of its staff. From 2018 onward, the well-established Helmholtz Mentoring Program will be available in a revised format open to participants of any gender.Helmholtz Mentoring-ProgrammHelmholtz Career Development Centers for ResearchersThe Helmholtz Association places strong emphasis on systematic career support and guidance.
