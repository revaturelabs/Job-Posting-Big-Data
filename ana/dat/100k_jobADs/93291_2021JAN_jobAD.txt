Our admin teams support Social Care and Education staff and we are looking to increase our pool of bank staff in order to be more flexible as the demand for support changes in this exciting service. You will make sure that everything is in place to guarantee that all runs smoothly for the processes and teams you support.Excellent interpersonal skills are essential along with a high level of written and oral communication.
