At Amazon Prime Video we are leaders in the DTC (direct-to-consumer) space and our mission is to provide deep and diverse content for all our global customers to enjoy. We are changing the way people watch movies and TV shows, with over 200,000 titles available on-demand across a multitude of devices. As Program Manager, you will play a critical role in the Partner Integration Management team and will be integral in transforming the way our partners deliver content to Prime Video. The Global Partner Integrations Management (PIM) team's vision is to make Prime Video the easiest and best platform for partners to distribute their content to customers. We envision that all partners, regardless of size, capability, location or language are able to reach customers, on time and with the highest quality, in a manner that is self-service and friction free. In this role, you will use your technical knowledge and critical insights to educate and evangelize Amazon Prime Video's specifications and requirements. You will work closely with our tech teams and engineering to influence and develop capabilities that drive scale in content ingestion, delivery, and publishing. You will contribute in developing mechanisms to create standardization and structure to the partner's journey, enabling partners to deliver content on time and defect-free. You will play a critical role as Prime Video's gatekeeper, holding our partners to a high-bar in performance and quality. This role will require you to develop solutions to complex and ambiguous business challenges. You'll combine analytical rigor and thought leadership to foster improvements across partner engagement, partner onboarding, and improve the technical video asset and metadata delivery mechanisms.We're looking for a best-of-the-best leader to own program management, stakeholder management, dive-deep into digital supply chain tasks, execute and deliver successful projects and programs that impact our customers. We are dedicated to growing the unique needs of our partners and our customers alike.
