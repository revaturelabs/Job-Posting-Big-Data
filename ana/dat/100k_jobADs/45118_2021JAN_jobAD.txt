community and youth focused charity in Devizes, Wiltshire is seeking a Head of Finance & Business Development.The role will be offered as a Fixed Term Contract for 22 months, with the possibility of extension subject to funding.The role represents a chance to work for a fantastic organisation who provide wide ranging and essential services to the local community.The successful applicant will be a budgets and forecasts-Lead on all tax & VAT duties-Company secretarial duties-Oversee the IT department-Lead a business development project on the charity’s outdoor activity centre-Profiling and advising on income and cost optimisation and commercialisation of the charity’s activities and spending, including investments-Property management duties covering the main office and activity centre – tenancies, health & safety etcCandidate requirements:-Fully-qualified accountant (ACA, CIMA, ACCA)-Demonstrable experience in a similar role at senior level in a comparable organisation-Leadership and staff management experience-Detailed knowledge of charity accounting – Charities SORP, restricted, unrestricted and designated funds, and trading subsidiaries-Track record of managing and reviewing
