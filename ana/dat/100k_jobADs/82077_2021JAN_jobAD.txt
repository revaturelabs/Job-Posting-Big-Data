Within Nestlé’s worldwide research and development network, the Nestlé Product Technology Centre FOOD with the central seat in Singen, Germany, is responsible for the innovation and renovation of culinary products. The NPTC Food Singen is the home of food and is bringing culinary art to a level that can be practiced at home or used in a professional kitchen. NPTC FOOD transforms science into technologies that deliver tasty and balanced meals with the highest freshness perception and convenience.Position SnapshotLocation: NPTC Food, Singen, GermanyWorking Time: full-timeContract: unlimitedStart Date: immediatePosition SummaryTo support our Technical Competency Unit´s (TCU) Process Engineering Team, we are currently looking for a very experienced Senior Factory Engineering Specialist (m/f/d) to start as soon as possible.A day in the life of a Senior Process Engineering Specialist:As a Senior Factory Engineering Specialist at NPTC Food you will be responsible to drive process innovations, support our factories around the world and build-up people competencies.Hence, there are several interesting activities waiting for you:Provide professional engineering & technical leadership in the TCU to ensure that all engineering activities enhance business objectives, factory operation, maintenance and performance.Support I&R and investment projects to achieve the project-defined capability and assure that the invested resources bring the return of investment expected with a flawless execution.Design & develop end-to-end processes and equipment concepts for culinary products with strong focus on optimal Total Delivered Cost and CAPEX efficiency under consideration of performance, safety, quality and environmental aspects.Lead or support benchmarking, networks, innovation & improvement & activities, develop and improve Equipment and Operations Standards, and provides technical assistance to the markets.Set strategy, coordinate and interact with key suppliers and internal customers on identifying the right machines and/or lines for the right applications.Lead people competency development in the field of engineering through training, mentorin and/or coaching and establish Factory Engineering network within the culinary category.What will make you successful.MSc or equivalent degree from a (Technical) University, preferably in Process, Packaging, Mechanics or AutomationProfound understanding of product category value chainVery deep successful experience in factory environment including Factory Engineering Management responsibility and/or Major CAPEX project executionProven experience in successful execution of major Investment projects is a pre-requisiteVery good language skills (Fluent English is a must; good German & Spanish skills would be a plus)Flexibility for business travels where necessaryGrowing together sustainably.NPTC Food wants to be a really good partner for you. With our offer for flexible working time and several work-life balance initiatives, we want to make sure that you can find a good harmony between your professional and personal life. If you are also looking for an exciting, challenging and rewarding position in a great international atmosphere with other benefits like a company pension fund and individual offers for further development we would love to hear from you.Ready for the next step?Please apply online. Your contact person for this position is Jacqueline Zeuner.Do you have any questions?
