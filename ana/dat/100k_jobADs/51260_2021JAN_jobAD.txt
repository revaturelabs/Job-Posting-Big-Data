Maintain work stations as necessary to optimize appearance and efficiency. Remove used and replace with all new linens, towels and necessary products and supplies. Clean all areas of kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living rooms, hallways and closets as set forth in housekeeping standards. Climbing stairs, walking, and carrying of supplies. Must be able to constantly lift at least 20 lbs and be on their feet most of the time. Walking on slippery (snowy) or uneven surfaces, standing for long periods, bending, exposure (limited) to wind and cold. Driving may or may not be required.
