JF&CS is an Equal Opportunity Employer.Email Me This JobPosition Summary:Project NESST (Newborns Exposed to Substances: Support and Therapy)/FIRST (Families in Recovery Support) Steps Together, provides parent-child therapy and peer support to address the impact of substance use and trauma on parent's mental health, the early parent-child relationship, and infant development. NESST helps pregnant and new mothers meet the dual challenges of parenting and recovery from substance use disorder so they can form healthy relationships with their developing babies. NESST/FIRST Steps Together is a program of the Center for Early Relationship Support (CERS). CERS provides support groups, home visiting, family support and clinical services for families and training and professional development for professionals focused on supporting the earliest parent-child relationships, maternal and infant mental health and child and family well-being.Project NESST/FIRST Step Together's Maternal Recovery Specialists are responsible for providing support, education, treatment planning, referrals and case management to pregnant and new mothers whose newborns have been substance-exposed. The Maternal Recovery Specialist reports to the NESST/FIRST Steps Together supervisor for individual and group supervision.Your workday will include:Working in collaboration with assigned clinician
