The Pentagon is the headquarters for the United States Department of DefenseThis Federal job opportunity offers rewarding and exciting careers with excellent benefits.Qualification SummaryThe idea candidate Leads studies and participates as study team member for campaign analysis of potential future wars, analyzing system solutions to complex and challenging USAF problems. AF/A9I is the premier AF operations analysis team examining operational-level warfighting, and the preparation for such wars, for the Secretary of the AF, the Chief of Staff of the AF, and other senior AF leaders. A9I ensures the future Air Force, & Joint Force, can be relied on to project power and achieve national defense strategy objectives.Employee asked to display the highest quality critical thinking, problem solving, and analytical thinking regarding future military performance and developing practical solutions. Will support the development, execution, and processing of mission- and campaign-level combat modeling & simulation (M&S) representations, databases, and analyses of US, allied, and adversary forces executing warfighting concepts of operation. Primary tools include M&S, mathematical programming, parametric and non-parametric statistics and data analysis/visualization, and applied military history.
