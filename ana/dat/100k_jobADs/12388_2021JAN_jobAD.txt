This position is also accountable for communicating/presenting analytical findings supporting major business projects and drives to implement recommendations.Essential Responsibilities:Works with LOB Strategy and execution team and other customers as appropriate. Design analytic approaches and analysis to address business questions or issues. Lead presentation and consultation of interpreting analyses of data, providing recommendations in support of critical long-term regional membership and margin goals.Evaluates on a regular basis, model to assess reasonableness of input data and assumptions and predictive accuracy of output, and applies learnings to improve model performance.
