The best part is you will not need any previous experience as full training will be provided. You will also have the reassurance of a job guarantee (£18K-£30K) within 20 miles of your location upon completion.Whether you are working full time, part-time or unemployed, this package has the flexibility to be completed at a pace that suits you.The traineeship is completed in 4 easy steps, you can be placed into your first role in as little as 3-6 months:Step 1 - IOSH - Managing SafelyYou will begin by studying the IOSH Managing Safely online course. Here you will learn about the basics of health and safety. The course is delivered online in its entirety through video tutorials, quizzes and presentations. You will be given access to your own learning portal which allows you to study from anywhere. Upon completion of the course modules, you will sit a short, multiple choice quiz which will result in your first qualification in the industry.Step 2 - NEBOSHYou will need to decide whether you would like to study the NEBOSH General, Construction or the Fire Safety course, depending on the path that you intend your career to follow. You will have an expert tutor on hand if required. NEBOSH as a governing body sets such high standards that it's therefore the most sought after qualification not only in the UK, but across the globe. Becoming NEBOSH certified truly sets you apart from the rest of the field.Step 3 - Risk AssessmentsThe third step focuses on the practical application of what you have learned during your studies. You will write a series of risk assessments that need to be up to a workplace standard. This is not only to ensure that you have retained all that you have learned, but is also a requirement to gain your NEBOSH certification.Step 4 - NEBOSH ExamsThe final step will be to sit the official NEBOSH exams, which we will book for you once you are ready. The exams can be sat in one of the official NEBOSH testing centres, or online. (Online only currently available for general certificate).Once your exams have been passed, you will be ready to move onto our recruitment stage and start your new career within the world of Health and Safety.Your Job Upon CompletionOnce you have completed all of the mandatory training and exams, we will place you into an entry level health & safety role as either a advisor, officer or coordinator, where you will be guaranteed a starting salary of £18K-£30K. We have partnered with a number of large organisations strategically located throughout the UK, providing a nationwide reach of jobs for our candidates.Responsibilities:• Maintain and manage health and safety polices, safety training and document control systems• Maintain, update and create standard operating procedures and assist in the implementation of continuous improvement safety projects• Complete regular reviews of risk assessments for all work equipment and operations• Ensure that all accidents are documented, investigated and recommend improvements• Conduct safety inspections, fire drills and fire alarms are correctly reported, safety inspections, risk assessments and lone working procedures are managed, and employees are aware of their responsibilities• Order and monitor PPE stockAt a one off cost of £945, or 8 monthly payments of £137, this represents a great opportunity to start a rewarding career in health & safety and have a real career ladder to start climbing. If you are not offered a role at the end of the training we will refund 100% of your course fees.Read through the information? Passionate about starting a career in coding?
