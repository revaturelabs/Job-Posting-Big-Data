Family accommodation may be available within the School grounds.Full Time, PermanentStart dateAutumn Term 2021Not suitable for newly qualified teachers (NQT)Apply by 15 Jan 2021Job posted 1 Jan 2021Share through emailShare through twitterShare through linkedinShare through facebookNot the right job for you?If you are a strategic thinker with a passion for high quality education and pastoral care and you see yourself working with the Headmaster to drive continuous improvement within a successful and historic prep school, this may be the opportunity you have been searching for. Our current deputy head retires at the end of the Summer term 2021 and this newly designed role will provide an opportunity for a dynamic and motivational leader who cares about delivering to a high standard within a caring environment. You will share our vision - to be a school which embraces a love of learning for every child through a combination of academic rigour and outdoor adventure.As a key member of the Senior Leadership Team, the successful individual will have exceptional organisational and administrative skills and a high level of intellect which will enable the smooth day-to-day management of the School. This combined with proven effective and efficient leadership experience and a high degree of emotional intelligence will enable the development and implementation of evolving pastoral strategies including behaviour management, welfare and personal development of all pupils. Excellent communication skills are vital to continually develop positive, professional relationships with a wide range of stakeholders.Having knowledge of prep school systems and a minimum of five years teaching experience, the candidate will also have knowledge of, or an interest in, boarding, including the National Minimum Standards. In particular, they will understand the importance of excellent pastoral care and welfare on a child's well-being and development and how to implement that knowledge in a practical and compassionate way to help develop happy, resilient young people.The successful candidate will have high standards. Integrity and fairness will be important to them, but they will not rest on their laurels. They will continually be looking to improve themselves as well as others whilst overseeing the well-being and welfare of staff too.Teaching Personal Development to children in years 5 - 8 will be an integral part of the role. You will also oversee the further development and provision of a wide-ranging extra-curricular programme including a stretching programme for Post CE pupils.Twyford School parents and staff say that Twyford School is a truly special place where they and their children make friends for life.The School is set on the edge of the South Downs National Park close to Winchester, on a beautiful 23 acre site. The traditional Queen Anne house and Victorian Chapel are complemented by a range of excellent modern teaching areas including a purpose built music school and recently the expanded DT and Art facility combines tradition with the future. Extensive playing fields and exceptional sports pitches including an Astroturf and training facilities are enhanced by a large modern sports hall and indoor swimming pool and recently refurbished all weather netball and tennis courts allow for a perfect blend of innovative indoor and outdoor learning experiences.We look forward to hearing from you.The closing date is 12 Noon on Friday 15th January 2021. Longlist interviews will take place on 25th and 27th January 2021.
