At the same time, tech is more and more complex and industry-specific. How to share with business leaders sharp points of view on digital solutions, technologies, and market positioning? How to help them seize the opportunity of technology? Let’s take up the challenge with the Group Portfolio team.As a business analyst and growth hacker, you will be in charge of developing game-changing tools to empower conversations with C-level.To achieve this objective, you will address different activities based on design thinking’s best practices:Understand the issues of C-suites across one specific industry (internal and external research, etc.)Explore potential solutions that could answer their needs and leverage our offers (ideation workshops, etc.)Define an impactful solution and derisk its development (description of the value proposition, definition of the customer journey, feasibility analysis)You will report to one of the senior members of the Portfolio team and have access to all the resources of the Group Portfolio
