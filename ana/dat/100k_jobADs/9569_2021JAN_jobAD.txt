Where these vacancies are present in various districts of Jammu and Kashmir, so in order to employ all these vacancies with eligible and appropriate candidates JKAPD has given the employment notification and inviting applications from all interested and eligible candidates. Thus aspirants those who are willing to work in this agriculture production department and those who have sufficient educational qualification such as applicants must have graduation degree along with diploma in computer applications with basic computer knowledge and applicants must have minimum experience can apply for these vacancies through offline. Such as applicants have to read the recruitment notification completely and download the application form in a prescribed format and fill it with necessary details and send that filled application form to the below mentioned address on or before the last dates of 10th January 2016. Other important details like age limit, educational qualification, selection process, application fee, application process and important dates are given below.JKAPD Recruitment 2016 details:Total number of available posts: 62 vacant postsName of the posts: Data Entry Operator (DEO)Age limit: Aspirant’s age should be between 18 years to 40 years as on the last date of submission of application form and age relaxation would be applicable to all reserved category candidates.Educational qualification: Candidate’s must have graduation degree along with diploma in computer applications with basic computer knowledge and applicants must have minimum experience of 1 year. Operation of large and small scale electronic data processing systems.Pay scale: selected candidates would be given a salary of 10000/- per month.Selection process: Jammu and Kashmir Agriculture Production Department will employ the candidates based on the performance in the skill test and personal interview.How to apply: all interested and eligible candidates those who are willing to apply for these vacancies are advised to download the application form in a prescribed format and fill it with necessary details and send that filled application form to the below mentioned address on or before the last date.Postal address: Government of Jammu and Kashmir Agriculture Production Department of concerned districts.Important dates for JKAPD Recruitment 2016:Last date for the submission of application form: 10th January 2016.Official Notification available hereCategories Jammu & Kashmir Post navigationAAI Junior Executive Recruitment 2016-200 Posts aai.aeroTANGEDCO AE Recruitment 2016-375 Posts tangedco.gov.inLeave a Comment Cancel replyCommentName Email WebsiteSave my name, email, and website in this browser for the next time I comment.Trending PostsSSC 10th Class Duplicate Certificate Online Download Memorandum at bseap.orgBy Mahee ReddyTelangana (TS) Govt Jobs 2021 – TSPSC Daily UpdatesBy Mahee ReddyEmployment News of This Week January 2021 Job HighlightsBy Mahee ReddyGovt Jobs in AP 2021 – This Month January Job HighlightsBy Mahee ReddyEngineering Jobs Mechanical Engineering Jobs and Civil Engineering JobsBy Mahee ReddyHSSC Recruitment 2016-369 Posts Apply at hssc.gov.inBy Mahee ReddyGovt Jobs in UP 2021 – Sarkari Naukri in UP Daily UpdatesBy Mahee ReddyLatest Govt Jobs in Bihar 2021 -Daily Updates in BiharBy Mahee ReddyCopyright © 2014 -2021 - Government Jobs Blog | About Blog | Contact | Privacy | Terms & Conditions"
