I have the answer.Wade Thiel, a senior creative writing major at UIndy, disproves the unemployed-guy-who-lives-in-his-van stereotype of English majors by already having a job lined up. (James Figy)I really enjoyed writing this article. It didn’t hurt that I got a lot of advice about the job search and keeping options open. Although thinking about finding a job and what the future will hold is a little scary right now, writing this article made me feel a little bit better.And even though I approached it from a practical standpoint, a lot of people said that following your dreams, responsibly, is important.
