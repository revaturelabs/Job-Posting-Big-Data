You will be working with an interesting mix of clients including housing associations, local councils, private developers, private estates and commercial organisations. The workload is mainly on residential/domestic properties providing a full range of building surveying services including project management/contract administration (projects £100K to £500K in value), DDA adaptations, litigation work and general building surveys. Candidates will ideally have experience of residential surveys, writing specifications, contact administration, party walls and general inspections.
