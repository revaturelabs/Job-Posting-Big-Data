For getting full information related to this Jobs 2020 read Below.Name of Exam: RECPDCL Recruitment for Engineers & Executives Exam 2020.Name of Organisation: REC Power Distribution Company Limited (RECPDCL), subsidiary company of Rural Electrification Corporation Limited (RECL).Name of Post: Assistant Engineer (Technical) and Assistant Finance Executive Finance.Total Vacancy: 09 VacancyImportant Date:Last date of Online Application: 27/05/2020Application Fees: All candidates are exempted from payment of fees.Educational Qualification & Experiences: Post wise qualification and experience’s details are given below –For Assistant Engineer (Technical): B.E/B. Tech (Electrical Engineering) Preferably with 60% Marks or 1st Division. AndEssential Experience: Experience in Execution & Implementation of Power Distribution Projects up to 66kV voltage level, U/G cabling preferably under Govt. schemes like IPDS, DDUGJY, R-APDRP etc. Should have good understanding of SLDs, GTP/Electrical Drawings, IS/IEC codes/ Pre-dispatch inspection of Electrical equipment, Quality Assurance.Desirable Experience: Should have knowledge of implementation of Feeder Segregation, Conversion of bare LT line with Aerial Bunched cables etc.For Assistant Finance Executive Finance: Chartered Accountant/Cost Accountant/MBA (Finance)Essential Experience: Experience in Finance & Accounting of ledger Books, Establishment Accounts, Preparation of Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss Accounts, Income & Expenditure Statements, State & Central Taxation including Income Tax.Desirable experience: Should have working experience in the accounting and handling of assignment of a project independently at least for 2 years in any of the power distribution company.Age Limit: Upper age limit is 35 Years which is relaxable for reserved category candidates as per GoI Rules.Salary/Pay Scale: Candidate will get Rs. 54,000/- pm.Selection Process: Shortlisted Candidates shall be called for Personal Interview to be conducted at Noida, Gautam Budh Nagar, Uttar Pradesh or Skype/Zoom Call.Category: Fixed Term Basis recruitment.Important Link:Apply OnlineDownload NotificationOfficial websiteNow You can get daily Latest Employment Update in Whatsapp: Click HereHow to Apply Online:Firstly, Download and Read RECPDCL Recruitment 2020 Online Form Notification.Then Click on online form.Then Fill up complete Information.After fill up information, SUBMIT it.Print and Save submitted form for Future reference.Now You can get daily Free Govt.
