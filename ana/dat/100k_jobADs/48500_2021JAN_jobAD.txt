Candidates can know the Full Form of any company by visiting the respective company official website.How to apply for Indian Coast Guard?Candidates can apply for Indian Coast Guard 2020 from the official link provided in the recruitment page or visit the Indian Coast Guard official website. The step wise procedure to apply for the Indian Coast Guard 2020 will be mentioned in PDF released by Indian Coast Guard. Candidates should read the instructions carefully before applying for Indian Coast Guard 2020.How can I join in Indian Coast Guard?First candidates should check the official notification released by Indian Coast Guard. Candidates should check the eligibility criteria given in the official notification PDF. Candidates should apply only when they meet the eligibility criteria. After applying Indian Coast Guard will shortlist the eligible candidates and will intimate them to appear for the exam. Finally candidates can join the Indian Coast Guard only when he/she qualifies in all the selection process set by the Company.What is the Selection Procedure for Indian Coast Guard?The Selection Procedure for is Prelims Exam, Mains Exam and Interview. Candidates qualifying in the prelims exam will be eligible to appear for the mains exam. Those candidates who qualify in the mains exam will be eligible to appear for the interview which is the final stage. Candidates qualifying in all the selection process will be hired as in Indian Coast Guard.What is salary of Indian Coast Guard?Indian Coast Guard provides Rs. 21700/- (Pay Level-3) plus Dearness Allowance and other allowances to a Navik (General duty). The other allowances depends on nature of duty and on the place where the Navik is posted and the critical condition of the duty.What is Indian Coast Guard job?The most important duties of the Coast Guard are: Safeguarding and protecting artificial islands, offshore terminals, installations, and other devices in the maritime zone. Protecting and assisting fishermen in distress. Preserving and protecting marine environment, including controlling marine pollution.What is the difference between Indian Navy and Indian Coast Guard?The mission of the Indian Navy is to maintain, train, and equip combat-ready naval forces capable of winning wars, deterring aggression and maintaining freedom of the seas whereas coast guards are for law enforcement, search and rescue in the
