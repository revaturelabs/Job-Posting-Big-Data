Description:1) Operates press that forms carbon paste into anodes used in metal-reduction pots: Pushes panel buttons to activate hopper, chain conveyor, cooling chamber belt, and power rolls.2) Turns valve to regulate flow of water to carbon cooling chamber.3) Observes signal light on hopper which indicates hopper is filled and paste is discharged into mold.4) Starts mechanical vibrator to pack mix in mold.5) Moves levers to synchronize rotation of index table with dropping of ram to press mix into blocks.6) Adjusts ram stroke to press carbon block to specified size, using wrench.7) Stops
