We currently provide a variety of clinical physics services at more than 30 radiation therapy departments throughout the greater Pacific Northwest and beyond. NMPC has an inherent advantage in that it brings physicists into a group where the varied experience and expertise of individual physicists is pooled to produce better solutions to the clinical problems encountered. The NMPC staff currently consists of about 45 physicists plus administrative staff, interns, assistants, and a CAMPEP-accredited residency program.
