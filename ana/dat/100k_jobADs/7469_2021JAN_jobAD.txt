Bids have been invited for selection of two agencies to conduct training of employees of Points of Presence/ POP service Provider / Aggregators/ APY-service Provider/ Corporates/ Nodal officers of central and state governments, among others. PFRDA also aims to train other stakeholders registered with it and involved with promotion , collection, remittance of funds there under.source; THE FINANCIAL EXPRESS, DECEMBER 27, 2016.
