We are a small animal and exotics practice serving the South-Eastern Wisconsin area with our emphasis on preventative and compassionate, high-quality veterinary medicine. We have a large, caring, and talented support staff, including certified veterinary technicians, and a new state-of the-art facility including in-house laboratory, digital x-ray and digital dental x-ray, rigid endoscopy, laparoscopic surgery, ultrasound, and therapeutic laser. We are a fully digital practice with paperless records and are constantly improving our facility and technology. We have an amazing client base that is continually growing and expect the best for their pets. We strive to educate our clients and ourselves, with an emphasis on personal growth and continuing education.The ideal candidate should have strong communication skills, be team oriented with a strong interest in general medicine and have a fun-loving personality. In addition, the candidate that will thrive at Brook-Falls enjoys a challenge, is able to multi-task, balance a full workload successfully by delegating to the technical support team and develop lasting relationships with our clients. Our practice is approximately 50% exotics. Board certification and species-specific specialty is encouraged to apply. All new hires are onboarded through a strong and structured mentorship program. Associate veterinarians have future ownership potential in a privately-owned, non-corporate practice. We reward our veterinarians with a generous, competitive salary, a 4-day work week, bonus on production, budget for continuing education, paid vacation, and a matching Simple IRA program. In addition, we also offer coverage for licensing, dues, and liability insurance as well as health, dental and vision insurance plans.
