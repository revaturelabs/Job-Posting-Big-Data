Jobs , West Bengal , CSTM | 0 commentsCalcutta School of Tropical Medicine (CSTM), Govt. of West Bengal.In the latest job notification, CSTM Calcutta announces job vacancies for GNM posts on a contractual basis in reference to Notification No: STM/DT/01/GNM/366/2020.Under CSTM GNM Job Notification 2020, candidates completed GNM in relevant subjects can apply.On the basis of the Interview, an applicant for the desired post will be finalized on merit.The selected candidate will be recruited, with salary pay of Rs. 16,860 per month.An Interested and qualified candidate has to walk-in on the announced date and venue.Eligibility Criteria For CSTM GNM Posts:No. of Vacancies: 2.Name of the Posts:GNM.Age Limit:The candidate's maximum age limit should be 45 years.An applicant from reserved and special categories, the upper age limit is relaxed as per Govt. guidelines.Educational Qualification:Should complete GNM in relevant subjects from a recognized university/institute.Selection Process:On the basis of the Interview.Steps to Apply:Read job details @ Adv LinkInterested and eligible candidates along with required documents submit at the time of walk-in-interview on the below-mentioned venue.Venue:The Director, School of Tropical Medicine, 108, Chittaranjan Avenue, Kolkata-700073.Important Dates:Walk-in-interview date: 11.11.2020.Important Links:CSTM GNM 2020 Job NotificationRegional Inspector(Technical) Posts In UPPSCAPPLY NOWSubscribe for Job AlertsJoin our mailing list to receive the latest news and updates from jobalertshub.com.NameEmail *SubscribeYou have Successfully Subscribed!Your subscription was successful!
