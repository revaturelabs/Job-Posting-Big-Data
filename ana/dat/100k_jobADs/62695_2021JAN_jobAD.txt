As our supply chain intern, you will have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience through formal and informal training, managing projects, visiting multiple facilities, networking with fellow interns, and working with key Jack Link’s leaders and team members. We pride ourselves on giving our interns a meaningful experience that helps prepare them for their professional journey after school. There will be two primary areas of focus during the internship.The supply chain intern will shadow department team members within transportation, warehousing and customer service to gain an understanding of the daily functions, roles and responsibilities within each department.The supply chain intern will be assigned a project(s) to drive cost out of the supply chain within these departments.Warehouse Operations responsibilities: Shadow with operations manager, supervisors and leads Attend daily huddles Provide daily update reports (safety, staffing and volume outlook) Exposure to the warehouse management system Identification of improvement opportunities to improve Walmart’s picking and shipping accuracy Transportation responsibilities: Shadow with transportation manager and load planners Provide on time delivery reporting and identification of root causes for late deliveries Exposure to transportation management systems Identification of solutions to meet Walmart’s on time delivery requirements   Customer Service responsibilities: Shadow customer service team leads and customer service representatives Gain familiarity to all Jack Link’s products Perform order management duties working with sales and our customers Identification of improvement opportunities to improve Walmart’s case fill percentage Measurable output: Intern will develop a final presentation on their experience highlighting project work, accomplishments, and insight on Jack Link’s as a company.You will leave with a better understanding of the supply chain, the systems that connect it together and the collaboration needed to accomplish cost savings initiatives within their area of expertise.
