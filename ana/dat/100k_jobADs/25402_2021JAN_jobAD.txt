Intrigued by leading practice software development methodologies? Deloitte Consulting LLP is looking for candidates with outstanding leadership experience, strong academic performance, and excellent communication skills to join our Analyst program. Analysts collaborate with our clients’ business and IT organizations to deliver business-led, technology-enabled solutions that help enterprises adapt to their unique challenges and opportunities. Each incoming Analyst is provided with a range of project opportunities to develop their skills and interests while also building a strong foundation as future leader at Deloitte.Work You’ll DoAs an Application & Program Analyst, you will be an integral member of a client service team, collaborating with diverse and talented team members to help solve multidimensional business problems, improve performance, and generate value for our clients across a broad base of industries. You will support the full-lifecycle of packaged software implementations and can capitalize on our cross-industry presence to find your niche and build your individual brand within the organization. You should have strong analytical and critical thinking skills with the ability to solve complex problems and communicate findings effectively.While a career in consulting is dynamic and evolving, we look for people who will perform in specific areas, grow those related skills, and deliver exceptional results to our clients. We recognize that you have unique skills, experiences, and interests and work to align and develop you upon entry to your career to a particular technology solution across our Offerings (such as SAP, Oracle, Informatica, Anaplan, Hyperion, or Workday) and begin growing your knowledge in this focus area. Setting our people up for success is our highest priority.As an Application & Program Analyst, you will focus your time and energy on designing and implementing packaged technologies and be aligned to a specific solution within the Firm. Project management skills, such as project plan development and tracking, issue and risk mitigation, status reporting, and documentation support are core functions that this Analyst will perform. An Application & Program Analyst can also work with clients to identify business and data requirements, define functional user stories, redesign process flows, and configure technical solutions to solve real-world problems. This candidate helps clients in their efforts to create and implement technology driven solutions that can help improve their business processes and performance.We are looking for someone who is passionate about gaining depth of experience in a specific technology solution to deliver exceptional value to our clients. Additionally, we are looking for an Analyst who is energetic, creative, intellectually curious, self-motivated, and inspires a high level of confidence in team members and clients.The TeamOur Deloitte Consulting team plays a major role in directly embedding technology insights into our clients’ organizational goals. At Deloitte, our consultants create sharply-focused solutions within an organization’s operating model, accounting for its people, intellectual capital, technology, and processes. Engagement teams at Deloitte drive value for our clients but also understand the importance of developing resources and contributing to the communities in which we work. We make it our business to take issue to impact, both within and beyond a client setting.Required QualificationsBachelor’s Degree completed by Fall 2021 in these related areas of studyBusiness (Management, Entrepreneurship, Marketing, Finance)OperationsSupply ChainInformation Management / Information SystemsEconomicsEngineeringMust be legally authorized to work in the United States without the need for employer sponsorship, now or at any time in the futureStrong academic track record (minimum GPA of 3.4)Ability to travel up to 80-100% (While 80-100% of travel is a requirement of the role, due to COVID-19, non-essential travel has been suspended until further notice)How You’ll GrowTwo of the most important tools in today’s business world are experience and resources. At Deloitte, you’ll find plenty of both. We believe in equipping our people with some of the best resources available and offering the experience, education, and opportunities you’ll need at various stages of your career to be technologically fluent. As a new Analyst, you will attend a series of onboarding programs to develop foundational knowledge, build essential core consulting skills, and cultivate an in-depth understanding of Deloitte Consulting’s methodologies. Before hitting the ground on your first project, these programs will also offer the opportunity to apply your new knowledge and skills in a simulated environment modeled after real-life client engagements. Beyond the formal training, you will be part of the national analyst community, which fosters opportunities to add additional dimensions to your experiences through internal initiatives, programs, and events such as skills-based service trips, local office learning events, and internal innovation challenges. Your work, clients, and colleagues will challenge you intellectually, enabling you to build both your experience and an exceptional professional network.
