for in mind. Our bright new reception area provides the perfect spot for socialising and activities, and we also have a brand new dining room and refurbished bedrooms. It’s a warm, welcoming environment, and the perfect place to provide the very best in person-centered care.Our dedication can be seen in all we do including cooking, housekeeping, nursing and organising activities for all our residents and visitors. We offer a whole range of facilities, from landscaped gardens to a cafe/bar plus a wide range of services, from physiotherapy to pampering sessions in our hair salon and nail bar. Our activities are as varied as our residents with Friday morning Bingo, exercise sessions, skittles and arts and crafts sessions all proving popular.The team here is made up of managers, nurses, carers and a number of people in support roles in areas like administration and maintenance. All of us take the same pride in making the home somewhere residents and their families enjoy spending time.
