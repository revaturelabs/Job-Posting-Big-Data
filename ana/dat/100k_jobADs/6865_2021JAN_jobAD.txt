wathi: 2 “Ngisho nanamuhla ngisazoqhubeka ngikhononda; +Amandla ami aphelile ngenxa yokububula kwami. 3 Ukube nje bengazi ukuthi ngingamtholaphi uNkulunkulu! + Bengizoya endaweni ahlala kuyo. + 4 Bengiyokwethula udaba lwami phambi kwakhe,Ngiveze wonke amaphuzu angivunayo; 5 Bengiyoyilalela impendulo yakhe,Ngikunake akushoyo kimi. 6 Ingabe ubeyosebenzisa amandla akhe amakhulu ukuze alwisane nami? Lutho! Nakanjani ubeyongilalela. + 7 Lapho, phambi kwakhe, udaba loqotho luyoxazululwa,Futhi uMahluleli wami uyongithola ngingenacala. 8 Kodwa uma ngiya empumalanga, akekho lapho;Noma ngibuyela emuva, angimtholi. 9 Lapho esebenza kwesobunxele, angikwazi ukumbona;Ube esephendukela kwesokudla, kodwa nalapho angimboni. 10 Kodwa uyayazi indlela engiyithathile. + Ngemva kokuba esengivivinyile, ngiyophuma nginjengegolide elingaxutshiwe. + 11 Izinyawo zami zizilandele eduze izinyathelo zakhe;Ngihambe endleleni yakhe ngangaphambuka. + 12 Angizange ngisuke emyalweni wezindebe zakhe. + Ngenze okungaphezu kwalokho abekudinga kimi.* 13 Uma ezimisele ukwenza into, ubani ongamelana naye? + Uma efuna ukwenza okuthile, uyakwenza. + 14 Ngoba uyokufeza ngokuphelele enginqunyelwe kona,*Futhi unezinto eziningi ezifana nalezi engqondweni yakhe. 15 Yingakho ngikhathazekile ngenxa yakhe;Lapho ngicabanga ngaye, ngizwa ngishaywa uvalo. 16 UNkulunkulu ungenze ngaba netwetwe,UMninimandla Onke ungenze ngesaba. 17 Kodwa angikathuliswa ubumnyamaNoma amafu amnyama amboze ubuso bami.Imibhalo yaphansi^ Noma, “ebengikuyaleziwe.”^ Noma, “engikuyaleziwe.”EmuvaPhambiliPrintaThumelela Thumelela
