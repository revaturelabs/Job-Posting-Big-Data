Edmonton man who claims parents discriminated against him when they didn’t hire him as a babysitter.The Justice Centre for Constitutional Freedoms has written to the Alberta Human Rights Commission on behalf of two parents it is representing.The centre says a mother posted a Kijiji ad looking for someone to look after her three kids before school and asked one respondent if he had any children.The
