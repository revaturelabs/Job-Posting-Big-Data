Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential job functions.Physical Demands: While performing the duties of this job, the employee is required to stand, walk, see, hear, talk, reach with hands and arms, push, pull, bend, stoop, squat, crawl, twist, turn, and will be required to lift up to 50 lbs. when working on dies. Vision requirements consist of: close vision at 20 inches or less, color vision (ability to identify and distinguish colors), peripheral vision (ability to observe an area that can be seen up and down or to the left and right while eyes are fixed on a given point), ability to adjust focus (ability to adjust the eye to bring an object into sharp focus).Work Environment: The plant environment noise level is loud. Although the plant is climate controlled, the employee is exposed to weather conditions prevalent at the time. PPE consisting of a hard hat, safety glasses, steel toe shoes, and ear plugs must be worn at all times when entering and working in the plant. Gloves must be worn when performing certain duties of this job and when handling parts. The employee is exposed to moving mechanical equipment, moving mechanical parts, fumes, airborne particles, dust, weld sparks/flash, smoke, and vibration.
