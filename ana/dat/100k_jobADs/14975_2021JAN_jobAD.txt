The PCPS will serve as the customer facing clinical product expert responsible for developing greater customer depth in both product and clinical understanding. The PCPS team may also be required to help support and launch other products that may be developed, sold or promoted by the GIH division in the practice setting. Additionally, the PCPS is required to be continuously responsible for developing their personal greater depth of knowledge to serve as the clinical experts for the company. By doing so, the PCPS can convey this information in an effective manner to help support optimal utilization of the practice portfolio to the widest range of healthcare providers possible.**A Day in the Life**Clinical Education+ Provide clinical product demonstration and clinical education to targeted accounts through 1:1 and group interactions to increase customers understanding and proficiency of Medtronic GIH product portfolio in the practice.Lead post-sales activities, including implementation, product education, in-servicing, and ongoing support for Medtronic GIH productsPlanning Execution+ Support sales strategy; working with sales team to achieve business plans and drive utilization of the products+ Support sales team efforts in lead generation and pre-sale clinical evaluations and demonstrations of products to demonstrate clinical value in the practice setting+ Support Marketing and sales by appropriately communicating customer feedback, field product performance and competitive intelligence, as required for current technologies and product launches+ Partner with sales to bring awareness around customers who could benefit from extended coverage through service contracts/plans to new and existing hospital customers+ Collaborate and share best practices and other key insights with other PCPSs to help support them, their regions, and maximize the entire teams effectiveness and efficiency as well as support and train newly hired PCPS team membersAdministrative+ Through Salesforce.com (SFDC) update account information regarding installation, in-service and training records, per corporate guidelines and established standard operating procedures+ Consistently perform administrative responsibilities, such as: expense reports, activity reports, and other business requestsPerform all online trainings within assigned timelines demonstrating proficiency. **MITG BUSINESS DESCRIPTION** The Minimally Invasive Therapies Group (MITG) is part of Medtronic plc, the global leader in medical technology.
