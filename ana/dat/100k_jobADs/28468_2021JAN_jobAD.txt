2021	About UsWe are a global company of thinkers, designers, and doers. Freeman partners with our clients to build a connection with their customers by creating brand experiences that are seamless, innovative, and immersive. From strategy, creative, and digital to logistics and event technology, our teams place the client at the center, helping them connect to people in meaningful and personal ways. What makes us unique is our collaborative culture, intuitive knowledge, global perspective, and personalized approach developed from our 90-year legacy as an industry leader. Freeman connects people to a universe of opportunities that create a spark, build understanding, and inspire action.SummaryThe Digital Producer is responsible for the execution and management of digital programs and projects, related personnel, as well as related creative program elements. The Digital Producer is also responsible for managing the following: project teams consisting of employees and partners, timelines and budgets. Works closely with the VP of Digital Experiences and Digital Operations Director to manage and oversee client’s projects, develop cutting edge ideas, technologies, products and platforms and implement them throughout the enterprise through education, training and support. The Digital Producer will also provide account support and client education as necessary to ensure program success.Essential Duties & ResponsibilitiesManage and coordinate with external vendors to meet project timelines and deliver on internal and client expectations of design, user experience, technology(ies), timeline and budgetManage compliance with the digital production timeline to deliver the project to meet the established schedule and financial objectives. Drive digital production timeline compliance and deliver timely communications on all things digital, technology and budgetary in nature. Monitor digital projects’ progress throughout their duration, and communicate project status to senior management as necessary.Analyze data, anticipate, identify and mitigate risk factorsManage the deliverables of digital, creative, content and technical components of the digital proposal process, to ensure the technology solution meets the established financial goalsServe as a knowledgeable resource to internal and external colleagues on current digital and interactive production techniques, technologies and appropriate vendorsContinuously improve digital practice and standards, particularly through guides and other learning materialsContinuously improving personal digital and technology-related educationEducation & Experience4 year degree or equivalent and related work experience2 or more years of experience in an agency, production company or corporation producing communications programs (business-to-business, targeted customer, branding, marketing communications, events, etc.)Excellent organizational skills and ability to multi-taskProven ability to manage multiple projects, communicate clearly and achieve project timelines\"Account Manager\"-mindset in the handling of the client and account issuesDiplomacy & tact in challenging situations and in dealing with a variety of personality types and working stylesPositive overall attitude and customer service oriented attitudeExcellent computer skills in all MS Office programsAbility to function under pressure with efficiency and graceStrong verbal and written communication skillsDiversity CommitmentAt Freeman, our commitment to diversity and inclusion is helping us to create not only a great place to work, but also an environment where our employees, our customers and our communities around the world can reach their goals and connect with each other.
