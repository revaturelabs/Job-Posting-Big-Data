This museum engages visitors in the exploration of science and nature by providing them with a broad range of programs, exhibits and activities relevant to Colorado, the Earth and the world. The Denver Museum of Nature & Science seeks to pique the intellectual curiosity of community members of all ages by helping them discover the unparalleled beauty of the Earth and the surrounding environment and the significance of preservation.Some of the interesting and educational exhibitions offered by the Denver Museum of Nature & Science include North American Indian Cultures, Prehistoric Journeys, Wildlife, and Egyptian Mummies. Visitors can also attend films at the museum’s Imax theater or experience shows at the planetarium. The Denver Museum of Nature & Science has identified several core values that it adheres to at all levels of its organization. These values include loving science; being curious, creative, and playful; cultivating relationships with each other, diverse communities, the environment, and for the future; and thinking critically and acting empathetically.As an employer, the Denver Museum of Nature & Science seeks enthusiastic team players to join its organization who are eager to meet the needs of the group. Ideal candidates will have excellent interpersonal, organizational, and communication skills.
