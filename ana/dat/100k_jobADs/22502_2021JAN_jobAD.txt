Save 97%.News Sports Packers Business Life Obituaries E-Edition LegalsSubscribeEllis earns Aspirus Wausau contractNathan VineUSA TODAY NETWORK-WisconsinView CommentsAspirus Wausau Hospital recently awarded Ellis Stone Construction a contract for the expansion and renovation of its cancer center, ambulance garage, and food service areas.Part of the new campus master plan for the hospital, the 17,000-square-foot project is expected to be completed in phases over the next 24-30 months.As a result of the project, the capacity for the ambulance garage will double to four ambulances. The emergency department façade and canopy will be updated for improved visibility and access. The food service area expansion will include a 6,500-square-foot addition and 3,506 square-foot renovation. The dining room and serving area will be relocated to better serve patients, visitors, and staff.
