EVP, Distribution & Networks, Asia PacificThe Executive Vice President, Distribution & Networks is a highly motivated, strategic thinking leader who shares a passion for content and driving business growth. This position is responsible for managing all Asia Pacific activity across SPE television and home entertainment distribution covering all media, all distribution avenues (SVOD, AVOD, Transactional, Free TV and Pay TV) and all the studio's feature films and television series product, both current and library. This role is also responsible for overseeing the strategic direction, management and profitability of SPT Channels/Networks in the region.Sony's linear and digital networks offer high quality film and television content from Sony Pictures and third-parties, as well as original content commissioned globally and locally.Responsibilities:Vision, Business Planning and Strategic Positioning• Overall responsibility for P&L for 3 lines of business- TV Distribution, HE Distribution and Networks.• Strong financial and commercial skills are a necessity. business.• Represents the company and manages relationships with major operators, content distributors, content producers, advertisers, advertising agencies and other groups in the region.• Review and establish SPE's competitive positioning with goal to build the organization's capabilities to deliver on the competitive positioning.• Must have or be able to develop and nurture strong and lasting relationships with key clients and partners.Deal making, Acquisitions, JV, alliances and partnerships• Increase the breadth and depth of product offerings through local acquisitions, JV, alliances and partnerships in the region• Manage the relationship with JV, alliances and third-party distribution partners.• Oversee deal negotiations to ensure maximum value is achieved and targets are met.•
