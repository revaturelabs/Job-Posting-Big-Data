New store openings such as The Range are becoming visible in the city and suburbs again. Over 1,000 jobs are created in Ireland now on a weekly basis according to statistics revealed this week. CSO Stats on July live register are hereRetail outlets such as Penneys, Heatons and The Range are all currently recruiting for staff.
