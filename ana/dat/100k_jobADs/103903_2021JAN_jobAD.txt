The position offers an opportunity to develop and apply cutting- edge protein engineering techniques to address fundamental challenges in synthetic chemistry and biocatalysis. The successful applicant will use a deep understanding of organic and organometallic chemistry, combined with state-of-the-art methods in genome mining, directed evolution, next gen sequencing, and laboratory automation to develop enzymes or artificial metalloenzymes for new chemical transformations. Trainees receive regular guidance and feedback to ensure progress is made toward their career goals, whatever those may be.
