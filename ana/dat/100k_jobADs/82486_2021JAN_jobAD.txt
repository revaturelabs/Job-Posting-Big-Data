"Community ScoreCard Project Consultant - Nairobi Informal Settlements | Jacaranda Health | NairobiBack to jobsCommunity ScoreCard Project Consultant - Nairobi Informal SettlementsNairobi, KenyaOtherApply for position nowBackgroundJacaranda Health is a non-profit organization focused on ensuring that all women and their families have access to affordable, high quality and patient-centered maternal and newborn healthcare. Jacaranda Health’s non-profit arm partners with government facilities to adapt tools for impact in the public sector. Our program partnerships with public facilities have grown from 3 facilities in 2015, to 200 facilities serving over 250,000 women and babies across 7 counties in 2020. We currently run two large programs which focus on increasing health-seeking behavior amongst pregnant women/new moms through digital health (“PROMPTS”) and improving provider knowledge and skills to handle emergency obstetrics through a mentor model (“MENTORS”). Jacaranda has research and design components within each of these programs.Please visit www.jacarandahealth.org for more details.Job SummaryJacaranda Health is seeking a consultant to support the implementation of the Community ScoreCard (CSC), originally developed by CARE, with 5 health facilities. The CSC implementation is part of a USAID-funded implementation research project taking place in Kawangware and Mathare within Nairobi City County. The goal of the CSC is to increase linkages and understanding between users of maternal/child health services and the health workers who provide those services.The Consultant will report to Jacaranda’s Research Manager, working closely with Jacaranda’s project team working on the Nairobi health project. If possible, the consultant may also be given problem-solving and advisory support from CSC experts in Malawi.This position will be tied to specific deliverables which will be outlined in a schedule. Payment will be made upon successful completion of the deliverables and their approval by the Research Manager and Senior Research Manager.Primary ResponsibilitiesThis role will include the following responsibilities:The consultant will lead the implementation of the CSC with 5 health facilitiesThe consultant will need to work closely with the local communities surrounding the health facility sites and oversee the entire CSC cycle (initial engagement, preparation, first CSC meetings, interface meeting, charting next steps).The consultant will be responsible for documentation of the CSC process, including learnings and short reports.RequirementsProfessional QualificationsBachelor’s Degree in social sciences, international development, health, public policy, or related fieldsExperience and ExpertiseMinimum of 3 years of experience in community engagement activities, including trainingPrevious experience coordinating projects and documenting learnings from start to finishDemonstrated ability to work collaboratively with various stakeholders, from community level to county levelFlexible, self-motivated, and responsible, including the ability to manage finances responsiblyPersonal CharacteristicsDemonstrated high level written communication skills in EnglishStrong verbal communication skills in Kiswahili and EnglishPassion for improving health and community developmentWhat would get us excited?Previous experience with the community scorecardPrevious experience working in informal settlementsExperience working with health providersContract PeriodIt is anticipated that the execution of the contractor’s deliverables will take place over the course of ~6 months, with 5-10 days of work per month.
