usArchi-Union创盟国际建筑事务所于2003年在中国上海成立，致力于将建筑设计理论、技术与在地文化以及时代精神有效融合。具体专注于将数字化设计以及智能建造方法运用于实际建筑项目中。Archi-Union业务范围包括建筑设计、城市规划、景观设计和室内设计在内的整体创作设计工作。Archi-Union的研究立足于从建筑材料（木、砖、混凝土、塑料、金属等）的新建构视角，将国际前沿的数字化、智能化手段赋予新的当代内涵。在17年实践中，已建成的项目包括乌镇“互联网之光”博览中心、上海申迪集团横沔城中村改造指挥部、OCT四川安仁水西东林盘文化展示中心、上海西岸人工智能大会B馆、四川成都崇州道明竹艺村、上海康德双语实验学校改造、上海思南书局快闪书店、上海香格纳画廊、上海池社画廊、上海Fab-Union艺术空间、上海松江名企艺术园区、成都非物质文化遗产公园兰溪庭、上海浦东九间堂线性住宅、上海嘉定苔圣园石工坊等。近年来，Archi-Union的作品在国际上受到广泛的关注，曾受邀到世界各地参与展览活动。包括2020第八届深港城市\\建筑双城双年展、2018威尼斯建筑双年展，2018柏林建筑画廊Archi-Union个展，2017芝加哥建筑双年展，并多次参加上海双年展、米兰三年展、深圳双年展等。美国现代艺术博物馆MoMA将“竹里”和“池社”建筑模型及作品档案等作为永久收藏。一直以来，Archi-Union以批判性视野，专注于建造本体研究，通过设计实验室的前沿探索，试图打破研究与实践之间的二元对立关系，开拓思想边界，扩大建筑学的开放性和可能性，并以此将研究和实践二者交迭推进。请发送个人简历和作品集至: in 2003, Archi-Union Architects is a Shanghai-based architectural design firm with a Grade A design qualification certificate issued by Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development. Over the years Archi-Union adheres to the combination of traditional Chinese culture and digital construction technology, focuses on “parametric construction”, “robot construction” and “green industrialization” and some other objectives, and practices the symbiotic relationship of nature, urban and architecture.There are more than 70 designers in the office currently and it has finished large amount of projects in the past decade including Linear House-Jiujiantang in Shanghai, Lanxi Curtilage in Chengdu, Taisheng Garden, Jade Museum, Shangart Gallery on the West Bund, Fab-Union, Chi She, Hefei Bashang Street Mixed-use Project, Shanghai Stomatological Hospital, Songjiang Art Campus, Aux Campus (Shanghai) etc. Its business scope includes comprehensive design services such as architectural design, urban planning, landscape design and interior design.Archi-Union design team focuses on applying experimental research into practical projects. The parallel stage of complementary development of theoretical research and practice is a major feature of the company. Projects were published on many architectural magazines and mass medias like Time+Architecture, Architectural Journal, World Architecture, Urban Architecture, Design Trends Arquitectura Viva, ABITARE, Architectural Digest, PLAN, ID, Dezeen and Archdaily.
