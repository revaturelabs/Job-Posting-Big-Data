The Governance and Proxy (GAP) Analyst, as a member of the ESG team, is responsible for conducting in-depth proxy research culminating in proxy voting recommendations to the Investment Group and participating in engagements with portfolio companies on stewardship priorities. The associate in this role demonstrates a moderate to in-depth knowledge of Capital Group---s proxy voting guidelines and is a resource in their ongoing maintenance. To increase their capabilities assists more senior associates, Analysts/PMs and the ESG team as needed in conducting assigned sector and industry specific research in support of investment frameworks and case studies to determine best practices and standards. Displays a developing understanding of the proxy voting and engagement cycle and may attend industry events on behalf of Capital Group as a development opportunity. May development training materials in areas of proficiency and participate in the training of associates on these topics. The associate in this role works independently on most assignments and under the direction of more senior staff only on the most complex projects, analyses and tasks. PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES/ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: - Demonstrates familiarity with key governance topics while building an increasing depth of knowledge, and a sound understanding of the responsibilities of the GAP team - Conducts research with increasingly deep analysis on various proxy related proposals and applies proxy voting guidelines with good judgement and increasing independence across a broad range of governance, environmental and social topics in order to author vote recommendations.
