In addition, the University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics offers world-class healthcare.The population of Iowa City continues to grow with ~75,000 full-time residents in Iowa City and ~150,000 in the immediate area. We have excellent recreational activities with our proximity to the Iowa River and Lake McBride State Park.
