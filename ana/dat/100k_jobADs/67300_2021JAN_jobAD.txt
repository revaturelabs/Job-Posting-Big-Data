SLS provides a 24-hour diagnostic service to the patients of the local general hospital, ambulatory patients, and the public health department. Our mission is to provide high-quality service to our clients in a timely manner. SLS is accredited for both the ISO 15189 & ISO/IEC 17025 standard.The need for the quality service is required both nationally and internationally. SLS strives to maintain its high-quality service to St. Maarten and the regional islands. The purpose of this position is to maintain, monitor and further develop, the laboratory quality system and advising in the field of quality assurance.Key ResponsibilitiesOversee SLS’s Quality Management System (QMS) to ensure quality throughout all stages of the laboratory.Identify and seek appropriate resources for the functioning and improvement of the QMS.Conduct periodic internal laboratory audits for process control and improvement as well as staff competency. Document audit observations, and make recommendations for corrective actions.Drafting a yearly quality plan together with the Laboratory directorContribute to thepolicy developmentcommissioned bythe director to guarantee thequality of services conducted by the organizationAssist in ensuring continuous compliance with requirements of accrediting, and regulatory agencies.Review and update policy and procedure manuals as neededMaintain oversight of the Proficiency testing enrollmentIdentify problems that may adversely affect test performance or reporting of test results and either correct the problems or immediately notify the laboratory management.Follow established corrective action policies and procedures whenever test systems are not within established acceptable levels of performance.Document all corrective actions taken when test systems deviate from established performance specificationsProvide training on compliance and quality management tools to staff as needed.Requirements and skills neededCompleted bachelor’s degree in a relevant laboratory area: a management training is desirableMinimum three years experience in a laboratory environmentDemonstrate coaching and training skills to train and coach staffKnowledge of and experience with laboratory quality systems ISO 15189 and ISO / IEC 17025Knowledge and experience with laboratory (automated) information systemsExperience with document management systemsStrong analytical and communication skillsTime managementGood command of the English (Dutch would be a Plus)Create a Job Alert for Similar JobsAbout ST.MAARTEN LABORATORY SERVICES (S.L.S.)
