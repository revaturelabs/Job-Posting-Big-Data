This will involve reducing carbon emissions from all Council activities to zero by 2030 and the delivery of all of the actions identified in our Staff Travel Plan. This will include reducing the impacts of our vehicle fleet and property assets, our leisure centres and our stock of nearly 3,000 residential properties.You will also help engage with our local communities and our businesses to help the economy of South Derbyshire make a successful transition to zero carbon by 2050.You will play a significant role in helping to adapt Council policy and procedure in areas such as planning, housing and environmental regulation to enable the district to continue its remarkable growth as sustainably as possible.The Council has been committed to environmental improvement and has held the ISO14001 Environmental management standard for over 10 years.
