You will now get jobs alerts.National Research Centre for Banana (NRCB) was founded on 21st August 1993 at Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu by ICAR, New Delhi with the mission to increase the production and productivity of banana and plantains. NRCB works on four major thrust areas of research i.e, Crop Improvement, Crop Production, Post Harvest Management and Crop Protection. National Research Centre for Banana has well-equipped laboratories for tissue culture, biotechnology, soil science, nutrient management, physiology, biochemistry, entomology, nematology, fungal, bacterial, viral pathology and post harvest technology research.
