The seal represents the city's agricultural and industrial history.City SealThe great seal of the City of Anaheim has the words \"City of Anaheim California\" across the top and \"Founded 1857\" across the bottom. The lemons, oranges, walnuts and vineyards represent the agricultural history of the area. The barn and oil derricks represent Anaheim's industry.
