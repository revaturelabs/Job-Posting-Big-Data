Mentoring is available.All Creatures Veterinary Care was opened in July 1995 by Donald Gibson, DVM (Auburn '91). ACVC is a mixed animal practice that has grown from a one doctor practice with a staff of three to a three doctor practice with a staff of 15+ since moving from our original location to our new, larger building just eight years ago. At ACVC you have the freedom to choose to work in small animal, avian and exotic medicine only or mixed animal. ACVC is a busy practice that offers preventative medicine, surgery, dentistry, orthopedics, laser therapy, in house laboratory, digital radiology, digital thermography, ultrasound, geriatric care, farm calls, emergency services, boarding and grooming.At All Creatures Veterinary Care we pride ourselves on providing quality, compassionate care for our patients, personal service for our clients and being involved in our community.Meet our team and learn more about us and our involvement with the community on our website, www.acvcgrayson.com or our Facebook page, All Creatures Veterinary Care.Grayson is a beautiful, charming, small town located in northeast Kentucky along Interstate 64. Named the \"Heart of the Parks\" Grayson is a great place to live if you love the outdoors. Grayson Lake State Park, Greenbo Lake State Resort Park and Carter Caves State Resort Park are all located within 15 miles of downtown Grayson and offer boating, kayaking, fishing, golf, camping, horse back riding, pools, hiking, biking and picnicking.Downtown Grayson has many community activities and events throughout the year that include everything from car shows to art gallery events to the newly added Market 474 craft market. Crowd favorite weekend long events include Funtoberfest, Hometown Holidays and the annual Grayson Memory Days celebration. Held every Memorial Day weekend, Grayson Memory Days includes lots of music, food, family fun and events like the annual Dog Jog sponsored by All Creatures Veterinary Care for the past 19 years!Grayson is also home to Kentucky Christian University and the Carter County Fair. For more information visit the Grayson Area Chamber of Commerce Facebook page.If you love the small town life but enjoy the occasional big city excursion, Grayson is conveniently located in the tri state area of West Virginia, Kentucky and Ohio , 90 miles from Lexington, KY, 120 miles from Cincinnati, OH, 30 miles from Huntington, WV and 20 miles from Ashland, KY.We look forward to meeting you and welcoming you to the ACVC family!Small animal medicine and surgery required. Mixed animal medicine opportunities are available if desired.NOTES:Additional Salary Information: Competitive salary with bonus potential. Medical, dental and vision insurance allowance.
