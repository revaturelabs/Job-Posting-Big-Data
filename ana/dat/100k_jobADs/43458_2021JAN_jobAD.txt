Reporting to the SME Sales Manager, the goal of this role will be to lead loan client acquisition efforts for the branch and to create quality loans of up to $250,000 for medium and high-income borrowers and attract customers too.Job Description:The incumbents will be in charge of:Recruiting new clients, evaluating customer needs, and introducing different types of loans.Explaining the loan process to prospective clients and confirming clients’ eligibility for a loan.Interviewing applicants to determine financial eligibility and feasibility of granting loans.Undertaking risk assessment by gathering detailed information on each client through meetings and field visitsUpdating the needed data in the Bank’s Loan Management System.Preparing Loan Appraisal Report for Loan Committee meeting, collecting and/or filling documents for guarantees.Checking the correct use of the funds paid.Ensuring loan monitoring including periodic site visits and ensuring timely reimbursements.Taking further steps to shape relationships and drive customer loyalty.Working in compliance with relevant/prevailing FMFB policies/procedures; and full compliance with the audit recommendations.Job RequirementsEducation and QualificationsHolding a Bachelor’s degree in business administration.A robust background in the service industry coupled with the experience of dealing with business owners is a necessity to succeed in this role.Incumbents will have a successful track record in a frontline role, experience in banking is ideal but in another commercially driven environment will be surely considered.To be highly customer-driven, responsive, and enthusiastic.Have the ability to work cooperatively with a broad range of people both inside and outside the Bank.Strong communication skills are mandatory.How to apply for these positions?The bank encourages eligible applicants to apply by sending their CVs including a cover letter to the given email address below.Do mention the below.-mentioned vacancy number in the subject line of the email.
