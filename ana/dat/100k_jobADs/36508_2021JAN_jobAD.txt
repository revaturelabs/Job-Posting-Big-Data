Tetra Tech's global practice supports water and wastewater agencies across the globe with master planning, design, programming, secure remote operations and digital twin modeling. The successful candidate for this position will be responsible for managing a team that provides Control System/SCADA engineering planning, design, and programming support. Typical projects involve design activities, cost estimating, evaluations and report generation, and other engineering professional services. The candidate will provide support on business development activities, such as client visits during project pre-positioning, technical proposal development, and strategic planning. In addition, the candidate will provide project management for large control system upgrade projects.ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONSPlanning, design, and delivery of instrumentation and control systems for the network equipment set up and configuration including managed network switches/routers, firewall hardware devices, network radio telemetry equipment, PLC Ethernet and proprietary protocol communications cards, if required;Review Contractor-prepared submittals and shop drawing documents;Manage on-site start-up and commissioning support as well as on-going support of existing control and SCADA systems;Write Operations and Maintenance manuals based on developed PLC/HMI code;Plan and administer Owner training sessions for proper operations and maintenance;Strong focus on client
