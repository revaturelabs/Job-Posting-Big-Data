Our Mission is to accelerate the world's research, and it has never been more relevant.Academia hosts a repository of 20+ million free academic papers, allowing anyone in the world to read scientific and scholarly research papers. The platform enables faculty, students, and private sector researchers to discover the latest research and leading academics in their fields of interest. We are proud of our 145+ million registered users, growing by 3-4 million every month.We have a \"Freemium\" business model, providing free access to research for everyone and paid premium capabilities to subscribers. Subscribing academics also receive enhanced analytics like Mentions and Readers, and career building tools like Grants and Websites.
