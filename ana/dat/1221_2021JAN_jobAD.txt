 Kuwait Care Giver Jobs 
 Posted on  December 12, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A job order was recently approved by the POEA that will provide opportunities of employment in Kuwait to care givers who are looking for care giver jobs abroad. These  Kuwait Care Giver Jobs  was approved for recruitment by the POEA last December 8 for a recruitment agency located in Malate, Manila. For the said Kuwait care giver jobs, 19 applicants will be selected for placement in care giver jobs abroad. 
 To apply for the said Kuwait Care Giver Jobs, proceed directly to the office of Philemploy Services & Resources Inc. and submit your resume together with your application. For more details or if you’d like to make an inquiry, you can contact Philemploy through their landline numbers or email address provided below. 
 Philemploy Services & Resources Inc. 
S605, A.P. Building, 1563 F. Agoncillo cor. P. Gil Street 
Malate, Manila 
Tel Nos: 5253705 / 5671989 
Email Add: philemploy828@philemploy.com
