 Social Media Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 Shropshire, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Marketing and Digital 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary:

 Andrew Carr

 AndrewCarr@workwithglee.com

 Published:

 Expiry date:

 Consultant: 
 
 
 My client, an exciting retail business based in the West Midlands, is looking to recruit a Social Media Manager. 
 This position is a key hire and will play a lead role within the marketing team. 
 
 Management of all social accounts 
 Plan, define and delivery social campaigns 
 Creation of posts in Adobe Illustrator and photoshop 
 Scheduling content 
 Creating concepts 
 
 The success candidate for this role must have a strong social media background, managing multiple accounts across a range of platforms. You will have demonstrable track record of delivering growth and engagement. 
 My client is looking for a passionate individual to join at a hugely exciting time. A strong B2C background is essential. 
 Highly competitive package. 
 Balanced approach to office and home working. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
