 Seeking for the right job in Singapore 
 
 Seeking for the right job in Singapore 
 by Maria 
(Singapore) 
 Lord, 
Please hear my Heart's yearnings and make it possible for me to find the right job for my kids future. I am here in Singapore hoping to land a job. I know all possibilities can only happen in your own will. And I know that you will never forsake me nor forget me. I know that you are hearing my prayers and you know what is the best for me. I am just seeking for comfort as most of the time I feel stress and starting to lose faith. Please, Lord. Don't let that happen to me. Make me strong just like what you were when you were suffering because the people forsake you. Please lead me to the right job and or lead the right Company to me. In Jesus name, Amen!
