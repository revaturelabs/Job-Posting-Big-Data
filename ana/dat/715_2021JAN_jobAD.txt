 Springpod was born from our desire to transform the way young people engage with employers and universities. We empower students to take control of their career path by connecting them with leading employers and education providers. From virtual work experience placements to apprenticeships and beyond, we are determined to help students discover their future career through innovative and modern engagement platforms - before they leave education. 
 We're looking for an experienced, ambitious and pragmatic Finance Officer to join the Springpod team and lead our Finance department, as we continue to expand. You will take ownership of the financial framework, providing assistance both internally and externally. 
 We support some of the most exciting companies in the World, including Airbus, the NHS, Jaguar Land Rover, Cancer Research UK, Vodafone, BT, Fujitsu, Fidelity and many more. We also work with hundreds of education institutions across the UK, with a view to expand internationally in 2021. 
 WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING 
 Working closely with all elements of Springpod, you will be responsible for running the finance function on a daily basis and providing hands on support to the management team. 
 Within your role, you'll be: 
 
 
 
 Managing daily income/expenditure, including subscription monitoring and continuous accounts reconciliation. 
 
 
 
 Working closely with the Sales team to create, send and monitor quotes, contracts and invoices. 
 
 
 
 Assessing and improving internal and external procurement processes. 
 
 
 
 Running payroll for contractors and team. 
 
 
 
 Managing grant applications and awards 
 
 
 
 Providing commercial analysis on financial reports, providing key performance insight to business leaders. 
 
 
 
 Finance business partnering responsibilities with the operational teams, providing financial insight and assessment to bids and tenders. 
 ABOUT YOU 
 
 
 
 ACCA, CIMA or ACA qualified 
 
 
 
 3+ years of Financial Management experience/knowledge 
 
 
 
 2+ years of experience in a similar standalone/senior role 
 
 
 
 Excellent stakeholder management skills 
 
 
 
 Strong communication skills 
 
 
 
 Strong technical skills, ideally with experience of platforms including Xero, Hubdoc and Revolut. 
 
 
 
 High level of personal integrity and ability to remain independent. 
 BENEFITS 
 
 
 
 Fast-paced start-up environment with a lot of responsibility and autonomy 
 
 
 
 Competitive salary 
 
 
 
 Very sociable team with monthly team incentives 
 
 
 
 The ability to work remotely (post-COVID!) 
 
 
 
 Training & CPD budget
