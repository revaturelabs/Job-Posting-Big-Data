 Mastering Remote Work, Part 3: How to Start a Career Online 
 October 28, 2020November 4, 2020   Flo Alcasas 
 In this episode, we're slowing down a bit and talking about how you can kickstart your digital-nomad, work-from-anywhere, online career from scratch. 
 We'll cover: 
 
 What I wish I'd known when I got started 
 Cornerstones of building a full-fledged remote career 
 Identiying and enhancing your core skills 
 Doing a personal SWOT analysis 
 How to actually get started 
 Quantifying your value and setting your rates 
 Knowing what general skills are valuable to every company 
 
 The goal: giving you a starting block for your fully remote career. 
 Your thoughts, stories, questions, feedback, or personal top tips are welcome!
