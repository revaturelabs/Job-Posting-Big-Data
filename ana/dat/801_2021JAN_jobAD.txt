 Cash Management/Accounts Assistant temp to perm 
 
 Location 
 
 Solihull, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Temporary 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £10 - £12 per hour + . 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Paul Steele

 paul.steele@gleeson-rg.com

 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 A fantastic temporary opportunity for a Accounts Receivable/Cashier Assistant to join a large reputable organisation based in Solihull . The role is an going temporary contract. 
 The role includes: 
 
 Processing and allocating Payments including BACS, CHAPS, Cheques, Standing Orders and Direct Debits 
 Processing Petty Cash for Centres 
 Daily Bank Journals 
 Sale Journals 
 Assisting with centre banking's 
 Ensuring all controls are maintained 
 Provide input to continuous improvement within the department 
 Able to work to tight deadlines 
 Other ad-hoc month-end tasks and projects 
 
 If you have the required skills, please apply
