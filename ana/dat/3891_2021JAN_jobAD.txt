 Accounts Payable Specialist 
 Accounts Payable Specialist 
 
 Location 
 
 Birmingham, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £20000 - £25000 per annum + bonus, 25 days holiday 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Ryan Doolan 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 ryan.doolan@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH17220_1581422722 
 
 Published: 
 
 11 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-03-12 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 You will be joining a newly created team in the heart of Birmingham as an Accounts Payable Specialist. The salary on offer for this role is up to £25,000 with an additional bonus on top of this and the possibility for study support. 
 You will be reporting into the AP Manager and will play a key part in the smooth running of the AP process. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Daily processing of invoices 
 Payment runs 
 Investigating and resolving discrepancies 
 Month end / period close duties 
 Reconciliations 
 Effective communication with suppliers 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Previous AP experience - ideally within a high volume SSC type environment. 
 Strong IT skills including Excel (Navision would be great too). 
 AAT or equivalent would be beneficial. 
 Confident communicator
