 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Freelance job 
 Here's the deal: Fuzz Productions is looking for a Freelance Senior UX / Product Designer to design digital products in our Brooklyn, NY, USA office. I mean, your Brooklyn office. Or your Bali office. 
 About 
 We are looking for a contract mid to senior level Product Designer with a strong UX background. 
At Fuzz, Product Designers craft the next generation of services that go beyond a single device or product. We create holistic experiences for our clients, and their customers and employees. UX Designers work in a highly collaborative interdisciplinary team of product managers, engineers, strategists and other designers to ensure we are framing business problems from the perspective of user needs and goals. 
 Responsibilities 
 Collaborate with the team to establish meaningful user or brand insights, which you use to define successful solutions 
 Ensure that there is an strategically framed business problem â€“ always focusing on the needs and goals of users 
 Take full ownership for tasks both large and small, and execute them to completion 
 Collaborate with cross-functional peers (product, engineering, strategy, etc.) 
 Present and explain your design/reasoning to clients 
 Contribute to an open and collaborative team with humility, empathy, and compassion 
 Working knowledge of Figma and an expert in Sketch 
 Working knowledge of WCAG/accessibility guidelines 
 You 
 Have a strong, strategic point of view 
 Have contributed to multiple shipped digital products 
 Have experience with qualitative user research and testing
