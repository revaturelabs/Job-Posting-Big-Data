 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 3:1 Roster 
 
 
 ASAP Starts 
 
 
 Long-term opportunity for the right people 
 
 
 About our Client 
 Our client is a specialist mining services provider who deliver Open Cut Mining Projects across a range of commodities including gold, lead, iron ore, nickel and diamonds. 
 About the Role 
 We are seeking Grader Operators with experience in Grader 14 and Grader 140 (GPS) for a project at Western Turner. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Committed to a Safe Working Environment 
 
 
 3:1 DS/NS roster 
 
 
 Ability to be flexible and deal with daily challenge 
 
 
 12 hour shifts 
 
 
 $55.00 flat per hour plus Super 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 12 months experience in a similar environment 
 
 
 Proven experience operating Grader 14 and Grader 140 (GPS) 
 
 
 C Class Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia 
 
 
 Must be able to pass full medical and DAS 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities.
