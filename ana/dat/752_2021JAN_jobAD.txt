 Painter (Epoxy Floor) 
 San Jose, CA 
 $23/hr 
 Posted 4 months ago 
 This job has been filled 
 Job Description 
 We are looking for an painter who wants to do epoxy flooring with commercial and residential clients based out of San Jose. 
 JOB DUTIES: 
 
 Install of interior/exterior epoxy flooring for the residential and commercial space 
 Executing high quality floor work 
 Independent & teamwork responsibilities 
 Capable of taking direction to ensure job site success 
 Work with generators, heavy concrete grinders and industrial size vacuums 
 Maintain a company provided work truck 
 Driving short and long distances to job sites 
 Cover driving the bay area and occasionally further for special projects 
 Compliance with employer & job site safety standards, (including 
 COVID-19 protocols), company uniform and code of conduct policies 
 
 MUST HAVES: 
 
 Painting experience 
 Desire to get involved within the floor sealing industry 
 Comfortable with working overtime and occasional Saturday work 
 Excellent communication with management for real time job changes 
 Professional and friendly demeanor with clients 
 Be willing to work hard physically 
 Clear driving record 
 
 JOB DETAILS: 
 
 Rate: $23 p/h 
 Typical work week M-F 7:00AM - 3:00PM 
 Overtime can be common 
 This is a long term role with the opportunity to advance your career in the trade 
 
 References, driving record, & background checks will be required.
