 About Figments 
 We're building the next version of the WWE, for esports. We're doing this by creating brand new virtual characters with fictional storylines who play esports on Twitch and YouTube. 
 About the role 
 We're looking for a  Graphic Designer  to support the development of our character brands on Twitch, YouTube and other social media platforms. This role will seek to elevate a consistent level of design for character branding and viral content. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Concept, design, and execute Figments promotional materials including key art, digital/social assets, digital ads, web assets, email marketing, merchandise, and more 
 Collaborate with team through ideation to completion to ensure high level execution and project objectives 
 Provide design direction and support to internal and external partners 
 Proactively follow projects from conception to execution, delivering each step quickly and on time 
 Produce fully realized, layered, composite key art comps to sell ideas that are on strategy and 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 A well-rounded portfolio highlighting design, conceptual and typographic skills and sensibilities 
 History of composite/manipulated entertainment image-making required 
 Attentive eye for quality and detail 
 Positive team member, problem solver, clear communicator 
 Skilled in adapting to changing circumstances in a busy environment 
 Extensive experience in Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign 
 Cinema 4D experience 
 
 If you love animated series and video games, and have experience creating graphics in the animation, entertainment, or gaming/livestreaming industries, please reach out! 
 Technology 
 We're a team of problem solvers specializing in character-driven storytelling. We're building tools that empower actors, writers, video editors, graphic designers and animators to tell amazing fictional stories on new media platforms. These tools combine esports broadcast and livestreaming video/audio pipelines with next generation motion capture technology.
