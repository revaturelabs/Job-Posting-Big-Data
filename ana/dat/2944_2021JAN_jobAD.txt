 Interim Senior Management Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Cambridgeshire, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Temporary 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £225 - £250 per day 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Emily Mitchell

 emilymitchell@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 7 months ago

 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-07-07 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 A large manufacturing client of Gleesons, based North of Cambridge, are looking for an Interim Senior Management Accountant to join them on a 4 month contract with the possibility of being extended to 6 months. The successful candidate will Provide accurate and timely monthly management accounts, budgets, and forecasts to a high level of accuracy. 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
 
 Recognised accountancy qualification (CIMA, ACA, ACCA) 
 At least 3 years experience in a Management Accountant role 
 Negotiation and influencing skills 
 Strong matrix management experience 
 Manufacturing experience 
 Strong excel skills
