 Full-Time Preschool teacher 
 English Day Care Sunshine -  TAMPERE  , FI 
 Job Description 
 English Daycare Sunshine is a well-established private kindergarten and preschool offering English language early education for 2 - 6 -year-old children. We are now looking for a new preschool teacher to join our professional team in August 2014. This position is permanent. 
 Requirements for the position are: Kindergarten/Preschool teacher qualification Experience in teaching English as a second language Excellent (native or near-native) English language skills, Finnish is an advantage Enthusiastic and energetic approach to teaching Flexible and friendly, excellent communication skills Team working skills For the successful candidate we are offering an opportunity to work in a fun, dynamic and international team. In our program we emphasize the development of children's social skills, small group activities, participation of children and active co-operation with colleagues and parents. 
 The successful candidate must provide an extract of their criminal record as stated in the Act on checking the criminal background of persons working with children (504/2002). There will be a probationary period of four months.
