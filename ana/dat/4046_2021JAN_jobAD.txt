 Consultant: 
 
 
 A leading provider in their sector, are looking for a Business Analyst to join their IT team to work on critical projects across the business. 
 You will be responsible for providing analysis across both IT and change, along with identifying processes and improvements and produce documentation for senior stakeholders. 
 Experience required 
 
 Track record of supporting and delivering across varied projects 
 End to end project lifecycle experience 
 Ability to analyse findings and gather business requirements 
 Proactive approach, with experience of creating solutions/suggestions 
 Process mapping and improvements exposure 
 Work with governance frameworks and understand data modelling processes 
 
 If you are looking to excel your career with a well-known brand and have the skills required, then please apply here.
