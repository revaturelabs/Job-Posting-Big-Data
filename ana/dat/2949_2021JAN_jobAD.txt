 Registered General Nurse 
 An excellent new job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Ingol, Preston area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 Our clients are a family run care group providing quality residential and nursing care for the elderly. 
 They provide a full range of care for residential and nursing needs, for both long term and respite periods 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 Ensure compliance with all legal, regulatory and best practice guidelines - identify, investigate and resolve risks proactively 
 Participate knowledgably and professionally to all inspection visits, ensuring your team is ready at all times to do the same 
 Champion appropriate independence and personal choice; developing, reviewing and updating care plans to meet our residents physical, social and psychological needs 
 Ensure you follow a rigorous monitoring and recording practice; documenting efficiently, and thoroughly all matters relating to residents and employees 
 Understand the key targets of your host home and support the Home Manager in their achievement by promoting and practicing high standards of nursing care and ensuring staff and shifts are supervised in a smooth manner 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     To ensure the promotion of high standards of nursing care, by ensuring all staff is supervised appropriately and the shift runs in a smooth and efficient manner
 
 
 
     You will perform all general nursing duties, following CQC and NMC guidelines, and report to the home manager, update care plans and administer safe medication
 
 
 
     You will provide a high standard of care and work within a team as well as on your own initiative
 
 
 
     You will be dependable, able to work well under pressure and flexible to meet the needs of the residents and the home
 
 
 
     You must have excellent communication and written skills and have previous experience working within a nursing home or elderly care setting
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £16.75 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies for both days and night shifts available. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 Comprehensive induction and on-going training 
 Career development opportunities 
 Excellent work environment 
 Free parking on site 
 Free uniform 
 5.6 weeks of paid annual leave 
 
 To be considered for this role you must be qualified as an RGN with a current active NMC Pin
