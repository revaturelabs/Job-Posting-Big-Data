 Job description 
 Emergency Services Officer (ESO) 
 About the Company 
 WorkPac Group is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels. 
 About the role 
 Rio Tinto are looking for an Emergency Services Officer (ESO) for Rio Tinto's mine sites in Tom Price. If you possess the listed qualifications and are passionate about the wellbeing of others, be sure to send them a resume using the apply link on the side. 
 Responsibilities: 
 Promoting a safe culture according to the Rio Tinto safety standards. 
 Undertaking emergency and routine medical care, fire and rescue duties. 
 Managing site emergency incidents as Incident Commander. 
 Developing and maintaining the Emergency Response capability. 
 Fostering close relationships with both external and internal stakeholders. 
 Requirements 
 Minimum Cert IV health care (can be higher) 
 Certificate III Mines Rescue (or recently commenced the course) 
 2 years' minimum experience
