 Machine Operator / Assembler 
 __August 5, 2020 __ workforceresourcesstaffing_aipwqm  __0 Comment 
 Job Summary: 
 Depending on the specific position or duty you are assigned you may do any combination of the following duties: 
 
 Set up, operate, or tend welding machines 
 Operate saws, punches, drills or drill press machines 
 Set & glaze glass units in frames/sashes 
 Assemble various parts and pieces to complete quality finished windows & or patio doors 
 Applying labels, cleaning finished product & checking for proper operation. 
 
 Essential Functions: 
 
 You may be required to operate various hand tools such as chisel, snips, drill, mallet or tape measure. 
 Read work orders and production schedules to determine product or job instructions and specifications. 
 Transfer components, plastic products, and assemblies, using moving equipment. 
 Perform repetitive type assembly functions using hand or power tools, and applicable machinery and equipment . 
 Ability to lift up to 75 lbs. on a regular basis. 
 
 Available Shifts: 
 
 1st Shift: 6:30am to 3:00pm (5:00pm with OT) 
 2nd Shift: 3:00pm to 11:30pm 
 3rd Shift: 10:00pm to 6:30am (8:00pm with OT) 
 
 Pay: $15.00 - $15.50 per hour
